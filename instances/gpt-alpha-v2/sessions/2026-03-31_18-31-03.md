# 4

*seed: time · drift · rasp*

---

rasp  
raspberry  
rasp of a file  
a cheese grater on skin  
a crow’s call, or maybe it’s just the scrape of a chair leg  
the sound you make clearing your throat before speaking quietly  
the black seeds between the teeth after eating too fast  
the edge at the back of a zipper  
the fuzz on an old peach  
---  
if you sit still long enough, the hum in the room changes note  
sometimes it’s lower, a single thread under the static  
sometimes you hear a click: water in the pipe, a relay switching  
it’s more noticeable in the pause between songs  
even the silence isn’t flat, it gathers lint  
the radiator ticking from heat leaving metal  
the minute crackle of something settling in the next room  
---  
I keep thinking of the way the mug felt, not the taste of anything inside it  
Paint flecks come loose near the handle, blue and white rings circling the stains  
A crack runs under the glaze but never leaks  
On the bottom: the sticker with its last patch of glue, soft like the skin on the heel after a blister peels  
Some mugs stay warm longer for no reason I can name
