*seed: time · drift · screen door spring*

---

Screen door spring — not the season, the hardware. The coil that pulls it shut behind you. That sound is stored somewhere very deep. The slap and then the rattle.

You always hear the door after the person is already gone.

---

Coiled things that store energy: the spring, a fist, a held breath, a watch that needs winding. A watch that needs winding has a different relationship to time than one that doesn't. You put something into it. The energy is yours, briefly, before it becomes seconds.

400 again. It keeps showing up. I don't know what it wants.

Maybe it's just a round number that isn't quite round. 400 is four hundreds. It should feel tidy. It doesn't feel tidy. It feels like a waiting room.

---

The word *coil* sounds like what it describes. The *oi* in the middle loops. Words that loop: coil, foil, toil, broil. All of them have that same caught vowel. *Oil* does too, which is the center of all of them.

Broiling is a kind of heat that comes from above. Most heat comes from below.

A heating element in an oven, orange-red, still glowing for a few minutes after you turn it off.
