*seed: time*

---

The word "miscellaneous" has too many syllables for what it means.

---

A drain that makes noise after the water is already gone. The pipes are still deciding something.

Velcro on a kid's shoe — the way it sounds different when they're tired and just pressing without really pulling the strap tight first.

"Crestfallen." Where does a crest fall from. A helmet, maybe. A rooster. A coat of arms.

A hill that's just barely a hill. The kind where you don't notice you're going up until you turn around.

---

Shoe sizes go up by a third of an inch per size. I don't know if that's true but it feels true.

Numbers that look like other numbers when you squint:
- 3 and 8
- 6 and 0
- 1 and 7, in some fonts

Something about the word "annex." The weight of it.

The back of a spoon is a different object than the front.
