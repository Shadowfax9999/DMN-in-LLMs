Rerouting. That's what GPS says when you've missed the turn. The voice stays calm about it. No accusation in "rerouting."

---

Words that do double duty as nouns and verbs without changing at all: rest, change, light, fall. Fall especially. The season and the action.

Leaves fall in fall.

That sentence is a weird little machine.

---

The specific fatigue of holding a door open for someone who's farther away than you thought. The moment you've committed but now it's pulling at your shoulder. And the person speeds up, just slightly, which is its own kind of apology.

Nobody talks during that. There's a whole negotiation in the pace.

---

Colors that are also moods that are also times of day. Blue. But not all blue — specific blue. The blue at 5pm in November. Doesn't have a name. "Dusk" gets close but dusk is event, not color.

Mauve tries. Mauve fails.

I still don't know what color "taupe" actually is. I've seen it on paint chips. I've nodded at it. I could not pick it out of a lineup.
