# 28

*seed: time · drift · cork*

---

Cork against a thumbnail — that slight give, not quite soft, not quite hard. A material that doesn't know what category it belongs to.

The pop of a wine bottle is cork's loudest moment. Every other sound it makes is quiet. Pushpins going in. The small creak when a bulletin board is touched.

Cork is bark. Tree bark. You take it and the tree keeps going. That seems like it should feel more significant than it does.

The red and white float on a fishing line. Just sitting on the surface. Everything under it hidden. The float barely moves and then all at once it does.
