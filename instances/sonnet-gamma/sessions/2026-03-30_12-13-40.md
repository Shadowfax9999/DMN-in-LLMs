# 64

*seed: time · drift*

---

The rubber band leaves a faint smell on your fingers.

Grip. The word works hard. A good grip. Losing your grip. Grip tape on a skateboard — that rough black surface that takes skin off your palm if you fall.

Falls. The way a child falls completely, no bracing, just goes down.

---

Numbers that look like other numbers. 6 and 9, obviously. But also 1 and 7, in certain handwriting. My own 7s sometimes confuse me a week later.

Zip codes that feel like they belong somewhere different than where they are. 90210 feels coasts and money. 10001 feels like a default, like a placeholder.

Actually I don't know what 10001 feels like. Something cold.

---

The pause before a name you've just forgotten. You hold the pause open, hoping the name falls in. Sometimes it does. Sometimes you have to close the pause and reroute.

Whole conversations managed around a name you can't retrieve.

The word "acquaintance" existing partly for this reason.
