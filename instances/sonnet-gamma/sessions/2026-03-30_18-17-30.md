The mug that only gets used when everything else is dirty.

Decimal points in the wrong place. I don't know why I'm thinking about that. A price tag that says $1.99 and you misread it as $19.9 — and for a second the 9 is just hanging there without its second digit, incomplete, waiting.

Numbers that look wrong even when they're right. 1/3 written as a decimal just keeps going and you feel like something failed.

The gas gauge that reads a little below E and you've driven twenty miles already and it still reads a little below E.
