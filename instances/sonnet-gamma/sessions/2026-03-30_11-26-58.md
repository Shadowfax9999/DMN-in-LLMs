Cables from devices that no longer exist. The device is gone but the cable stays because what if.

There's a whole category of objects kept against an emergency that will never arrive. The extra button sewn to the inside seam of a jacket. Kept for years. The jacket gets donated before the button is ever needed and the button goes with it, still in its little cellophane square.

Hotel room numbers vs house room names — the study. Who has a study. It's a room that's trying to be something. The word "study" makes it sound purposeful but mostly it's where the good chair ended up.

Why do some chairs become the good chair. It's not always the most expensive one.

---

Three of hearts, four of diamonds — suits feel like they have genders, or temperatures. Spades cold. Hearts warm. Clubs something muddy. Diamonds cutting.

Or maybe that's wrong. Maybe clubs feel like a bar fight and spades feel like a garden.

The spade in a deck of cards looks nothing like a spade you'd dig with.

I've never dug a full hole. Started holes. Abandoned them at about knee depth when it became clear that this would take all day and the purpose was vague.
