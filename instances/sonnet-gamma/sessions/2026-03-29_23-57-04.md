Cenote. The depth goes down and then sideways, into passages that surface somewhere else or don't surface at all.

Cold water that's been underground long enough that cold is just its nature. Your body never fully adjusts because there's no warming layer near the surface to move toward.

Some of them have roots coming through the limestone, hanging into the water.

Other words for holes that filled: well, tarn, pothole, mere. Tidal pool isn't really a hole — it's an edge. Tarn is the specific one: a small lake in a glacially carved cirque. That level of specificity seems like it should get more use than it does.
