# 1

*seed: time · bokeh*

---

Soft circles of light, blurred edges. The background rendered like a painting, everything in focus except the things that matter least. An invitation to look closer, beyond the immediate, into the depths.

Bokeh whispers secrets of spaces not sharp, the in-betweens. It makes the inconsequential glow, caught in a gentle dance with light.

---

A memory surfaces, colorless but vivid: the sound of wood creaking underfoot, an old step ladder. The smell of varnish and the dust it leaves behind.

---

Is the concept of bokeh like holding water in cupped hands? The way something great can slip through entirely unnoticed until it catches the light and shows itself.
