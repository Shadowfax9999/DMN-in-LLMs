# 17

*seed: time · drift · drone*

---

Drone. In the air, an unrelenting hum. Words flatten into the background, lost in that constant vibration.---

A drone of bees, a low, resonant buzz alive with purpose. Honey hidden in the undertow of sound.---

Droning lectures, the words heavy and slow, seeping through the cracks in consciousness. A monotone lullaby for inattentive minds.---

A drone in the sky, its flight purposeful yet indifferent. Silent eyes surveying.---

The way a cello can drone, one note held long, filling the air with its resonant weight.---

Droning traffic, the chorus of engines in the night, a reminder that even the city needs to breathe. Traffic lights blinking, a synchrony that rarely feels seen.---

A mind can drone too, circling the same thoughts, round and round, comfortable in their familiarity. Where does that leave us?
