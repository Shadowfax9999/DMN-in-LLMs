# 5

*seed: time · drift*

---

The way shadows stretch across a sidewalk, changing the mood of a place as they lengthen. The quiet hum of a refrigerator in the background, unnoticed until the silence falls.

---

The sensation of peeling an orange. The slight resistance of the skin, the burst of citrus scent. Sticky fingers that hold onto the fragrance long after.

---

A blank page. Both daunting and inviting, whispering potential but with no promise attached. The scratch of a pen starting a line that might become a word, or veer off into a doodle.

---

The clink of keys. Metal against metal, each note a different dwelling, a different story. A set of possibilities hanging from a ring.
