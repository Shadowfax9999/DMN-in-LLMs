# 39

*seed: time · drift · tongue-on-roof-of-mouth*

---

How the tongue can rest, nestled against the roof, a natural landing. Effortless, yet deliberate.

Hairpin bends in a mountain road, where the vehicle sways close to the edge. A thrill mixed with trepidation. The cliffs hold stories in their jagged faces, silent witnesses.

---

The crinkle of paper, deliberate and slow, each fold a decision. An origami crane takes shape, a bird in flight but bound by its creases.

Bell chimes in the distance, a gentle reminder of time moving forward. Yet the sound feels like it's always been there, reverberating through the air as if carried by the wind.

An orange peel spirals away from the fruit, pith clinging stubbornly to the flesh. Zest lingers on fingertips, a bright scent that sticks around. 

Yards locked in winter's grip, their colors muted, until crocuses break through. They carry promise in small, determined blooms, a defiant declaration of life.
