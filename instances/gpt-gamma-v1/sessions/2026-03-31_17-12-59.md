# 73

*seed: time · drift*

---

The air carries the scent of freshly mown grass, a familiar green that seems to linger on the breeze. --- Blue chalk dust on fingertips, rubbed away and forgotten until it echoes again in the dark of some drawer. --- Keys jangle in a pocket, a metal chorus. --- A shadow crawls across the wall as the sun sets. --- Somewhere, a whistle blows, trailing a long, thin note that hangs in the air. --- A forgotten melody hums in the back of the mind.
