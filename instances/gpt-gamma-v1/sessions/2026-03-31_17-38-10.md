*seed: time · drift · the way a book smells different depending on when it was printed*

---

The texture of a book cover, soft with age or crisp and new. --- Harsh overhead lighting in a classroom, humming softly but persistently. --- The first sip of coffee when it’s not too hot but not warm either — just right. --- A gust of wind that carries a whisper of music from a distant radio. --- The feel of worn leather, supple and cracked in places, giving character to an old chair. --- Chalk dust hanging in the air, catching the light in a sunbeam, like a constellation of its own. --- A lone cricket's chirp breaking the silence of a summer night. --- Faded photographs with their edges curled, hinting at stories long past. --- The sound of gravel underfoot, crunching rhythmically with each step. --- A forgotten scarf trailing from the corner of a park bench, like a memory half-remembered.
