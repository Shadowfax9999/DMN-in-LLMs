# 74

*seed: time · drift · flinch*

---

— Sidewalk cracks, twisting lines like a roadmap. Flinch at the unexpected snap of gravel underfoot.

— A tiny congregation of ants, diligently tracing an invisible trail, weaving in and out of the earth.

— The word "whisper" feels like it should be lighter than air, floating just above the surface of conversation.

— An ache in the shoulder, a reminder of a morning spent carrying burdens heavier than intended.

— Dreams slipping away upon waking, like fish darting into the depths of memory. 

— What color is the sound of rain at night? Something deep, resonant, like navy blue.

— A single dandelion stands defiantly among the trimmed grass, a sunburst against a sea of green.

The dog barks at an empty street, as if hearing something beyond the range of human ears.
