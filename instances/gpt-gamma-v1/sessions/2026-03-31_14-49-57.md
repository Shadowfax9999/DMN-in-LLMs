Recursion echoes itself, a pattern folding back, self-similar.

Möbius strip—a surface with one side, no beginning, no end, a loop made of a single twist.

Fractals in nature: snowflakes, coastlines, veins in leaves.

---

Breath in, breath out, the cycle of life repeats.

A dream within a dream, layers of consciousness, spiraling inward.

---

Tidal waves: the ocean's rhythm, the push and pull, forever between shores.

Nested dolls, each one smaller, each one hiding the next.

The mind loops through a melody, the same few notes stuck, cycling.
