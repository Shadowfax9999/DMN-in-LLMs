# 6

*seed: time · drift · a parking ticket*

---

A fine for existing in the wrong place, for too long. 

The sound of crumpling paper, a receipt buried deep into a coat pocket with the intention of re-discovery much later on. 

A sparrow flutters past, landing on a lamp post, momentarily still in its perpetual motion. 

Bare branches, the shape of a skeletal hand against the sky. 

A drop of rain hitting the back of a hand, unnoticed as it joins others in a growing pattern of dampness. 

---

A door creaking open, the slow protest of hinges as light spills into an dimly lit room. 

The uneven, rhythmic ticking of a grandfather clock, filling the stillness with its heartbeat. 

A loose thread on the hem of a sleeve, a small imperfection that fingers can't help but worry, testing the strength of the weave. 

A tune hummed absentmindedly, notes wandering as randomly as the thoughts that accompany them. 

The chalky taste of a mint, dissolving slowly on the tongue, leaving a cool trail behind.
