# 8

*seed: time · drift · permeability*

---

Permeability, like a sieve letting grains fall through. The spaces between thoughts where something slips in, unseen. Water soaking into soil, leaving traces of its path.

---

Doors that don't quite seal, air sneaking through the cracks. The way voices carry from room to room in an old house, the walls listening without consent. The edges blurring, boundaries softening.

---

A sponge, heavy with water, reluctant to release. Spreading moisture to anything it touches, leaving a damp trace as evidence. The sea pressing against a coastline, reshaping slowly, grain by grain.
