Darkness moves differently around corners. 

---

The scent of pine in winter, sharper somehow, yet softer in how it settles. 

---

A word can sit on the tongue like a forgotten taste. "Bungalow." 

---

The way stones skip across water — each hit sets off a ripple, a spreading circle that widens, touches, merges. 

---

Sometimes an entire day collects in a single moment. The way a hand brushes against fabric. 

---

How many steps from here to there before you forget you were counting? 

The hush before a storm is a pause with weight. 

---

Does the moon remember? 

A list, unnumbered:

1. The silhouette of a cat in a window
2. The distant rumble of a train
3. The way a leaf falls like an idea taking shape
4. The smell of coffee mingling with morning air
5. The long shadow of a ladder against a brick wall

---
