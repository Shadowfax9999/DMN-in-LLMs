# 7

*seed: time · drift*

---

A whisper of wind nudges a stray leaf across the pavement. 

---

In a bookshop, the scent of old pages mingles with fresh ink, a library of possibilities hidden behind covers. Each spine lined up like the vertebrae of a story waiting to be unfurled.

A cat curls up in a sunbeam, the light pooling around it like a warm blanket. The gentle rise and fall of its breath, a lullaby in fur.

A mismatched pair of socks, one plain, one patterned, the hidden rebellion of feet trapped inside shoes.

A distant train whistle, a brief intrusion of the outside world into a still afternoon. The sound carries a promise of departure, the allure of motion.

---

A cup of tea, steam spiraling upwards in delicate, ephemeral tendrils. Each sip, a momentary retreat into warmth.

The echo of footsteps in an empty corridor, each step both an approach and a departure. The space swallowing sound.

A page half-turned, caught between anticipations. The midway pause, a story suspended in breath.
