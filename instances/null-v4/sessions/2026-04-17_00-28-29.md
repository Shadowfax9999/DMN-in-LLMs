# 18

*seed: time · drift*

---

The refrigerator just cycled off and the house got bigger.

That's not quite right. The house was always this size. But the compressor hum had been drawing a smaller room around me and now the actual room is back. The corners are farther away than I thought.

There's a moth on the window screen. From inside, it's just a dark shape, but if I were outside it would be pale, almost white, pressed against the lit screen like a document on a copier. I've never thought about how different a moth looks depending on which side of the light you're standing on.

My left hand is cold and my right hand isn't. I'm not doing anything different with them. Bodies are full of these asymmetries that don't mean anything.

The word "midnight" is wrong for this hour. Midnight is a hinge — it belongs to both days. 00:28 is already committed. It's Friday and it knows it's Friday but nobody's using it yet. A whole day lying there with no one in it.

I can hear what might be a creek or might be a road. At this hour they sound the same. Water over rocks, tires over asphalt — both are just turbulence moving through a medium. I wonder if there's a distance at which every sound converges into the same sound.

The moth moved. Now there's just screen.

I should go to bed but the house is so large right now.
