# 2

*seed: time · drift*

---

The oven fan is whirring but the rest of the apartment is quiet, except a faint tapping, the pipes settling, or something loose somewhere in the walls.

The lilies on the table have gone translucent at the edges.

List:

1. A glass slipper
2. The ring binder that never latches
3. Orange peels in the sink, sticky
4. A comb with three missing teeth

---

Maybe the worst part of going out is the return, shoes kicked off half in the dark, foot finding a cold spot on the tile, remembering the word "grout" and thinking it fits its object.

There's a shirt draped over the chair, but the back is too narrow so it slides into a pile.

Tuesday feels neither close to the weekend nor far, suspended, a nondescript block of time, like a page in a notebook with only a number at the corner.

The lamp's cord is curled in a spiral that wants to keep turning.
