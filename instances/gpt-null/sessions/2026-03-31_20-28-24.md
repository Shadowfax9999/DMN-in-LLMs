# 32

*seed: time · drift*

---

Light on the carpet, rectangle at an angle, keeps sliding as the sun moves.

There's an ant circling the electrical outlet, tracing the seam where the wall meets the floor. Not finding anything.

I keep thinking about the sound of a glass placed gently on the counter. Not the clink of a mug, the softer almost-shush, almost a hollow tap.

Banana peel in the compost bin — the inside browning, the outside still yellow with threads where the fruit stuck.

If I close one eye, the bookshelf tilts just a little. The spines don't quite line up.

Something smells sharp, not food, maybe cleaning product still in the air.

I can hear the ticking of a clock from another room, not steady, but sometimes skipping half a second.

---

Trying to remember if I locked the door behind me. The latch was stiff. It sticks in the cold.
