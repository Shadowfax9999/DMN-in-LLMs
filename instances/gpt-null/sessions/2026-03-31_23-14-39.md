List of things I can’t remember doing today:
– Locking the door after coming in  
– Whether I wore socks  
– If the kettle actually boiled or if I just poured cold water over the tea  
– Which pocket the receipt went into  
– The order of the three text messages

---

The street outside holds a thick kind of quiet, nothing much distinct in it except the occasional shape moving at the edge of a window’s reach.

There is a smell that only happens at this hour, something that isn’t quite the air from the street or the leftover food, but a third thing mixed somehow.

The lamp throws a circle onto the table, but the outer edge isn’t actually a line, it’s a sort of uncertain fuzz.

If I stand still in the kitchen I can hear a refrigerator motor turning on, but only on the left side.

I forgot where I left the second key.
