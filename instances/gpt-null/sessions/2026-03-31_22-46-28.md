# 59

*seed: time · drift*

---

There's a window across the street that flickers sometimes like someone is watching TV, but I never see figures just the changing blue and white. The wind rises and then drops away again, and sometimes it presses the screen of the open window inward, then lets go so it relaxes and sighs.

---

There’s a kind of tiredness that feels cool and quiet inside the bones, different from the fever-tired with eyes burning. There's no urgency to the tiredness now. The mind just rocks a little, back and forth, thinking about long hallways in office buildings and the far away hum of machines always awake.

---

The word "Tuesday" feels pale green if I don't think too hard about it.

---

My left knee made a tiny grinding sound on the stairs, not painful, just a little resistance. The moth that keeps landing near the lamp is persistent, not learning the heat.

The refrigerator leans forward ever so slightly on its feet.
