# 70

*seed: time · drift*

---

A flashlight with batteries that rattle a little, and if you tip it back and forth, you can feel the slight give as they knock against the spring at the base. The beam always has a yellow edge, no matter how new the bulb.

---

Inside the car, somewhere in a parking lot, the sound of the engine cooling off — a tiny series of ticks that last longer than I expect. The dome light is orange, revealing dust on the dashboard. It smells faintly of something sweet and artificial.

A streetlight. There's always an insect circling just outside the cone of light, in the dark air that never quite lets itself be seen.

The sound of a train far off, even though there are no visible tracks in this part of the city.

Wind pushing blossoms against wet pavement, pressing them flat, making a constellation of pale dots that will dry darker.
