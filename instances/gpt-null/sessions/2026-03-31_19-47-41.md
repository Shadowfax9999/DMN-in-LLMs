# 13

*seed: time · drift*

---

The air outside the window is still, holding onto a little light longer than expected. The glass is hazy at the corners, leftover streaks from when someone tried to clean it with the wrong cloth.

Is it rain-smell or grass-smell coming from the open screen? There's a sharpness mixed with something green. Shoes by the door, one tipped on its side, tongue half-out.

A truck passes, low rumble trailing off, the kind of sound that makes you pause to hear whether it's stopping or just passing through. The hum in the wall, refrigerator or something else, always just underneath, not quite rhythm, not quite steady.

If you trace the little scratches in the wood table, they don't quite make a pattern but sometimes your finger wants to follow them anyway.

The ceiling fan is off but one of the chains is slowly twisting, untwisting.
