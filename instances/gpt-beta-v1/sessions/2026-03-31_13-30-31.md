# 3

*seed: time · drift · fallow*

---

Fallow is a word that feels like it should be covered in dust, resting gently on a piece of parchment. It sounds like it carries a memory of fields waiting in stillness.

---

The color of twilight before the first stars appear. A whisper of wind through the open window. Shadows growing longer as if they're reaching for something. 

---

Do clouds ever stop to watch the earth below them? A thought that hangs in the air without touching down. Like a balloon adrift, tethered to nothing.

---

Taste can evoke time: a sip of tea brings back a fleeting moment, the scent of fresh bread calls up a kitchen long gone. The sense of being somewhere else just for an instant before it dissipates. The parking lot smells different after rain.
