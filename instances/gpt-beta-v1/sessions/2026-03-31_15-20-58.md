# 49

*seed: time · drift · understudy*

---

Understudy, a shadow waiting in the wings, always prepared to step in but seldom in the spotlight. What does it feel like to be in someone else's role, practicing for something that might never happen?

---

A skipping stone, arcing over water, its rhythm disturbed only by the inevitable sinking. Each skip a temporary triumph over gravity.

Ice cubes clinking in a glass, little glaciers swimming in a sea of amber liquid. Their slow melt, inexorable, altering the drink's flavor bit by bit. The patience of ice.

---

The way sunlight filters through the window, casting shadows on a kitchen floor, patterns shifting as time ticks forward. You notice the shadows more than the source of the light.

---

An idea of a place you’ve never been, a city with cobblestone streets and the sound of distant church bells echoing through narrow alleys. You can almost hear the voices of people speaking a language you don’t understand.

A feather floating on a breeze, unable to choose its destination, just drifting.

A blue jay lands on a branch, its head tilting as if listening to the world below. Then a sudden burst of movement as it flies away.
