# 87

*seed: time · drift · ligature*

---

Ligature: a connection, a tying together. In music, it binds notes; in language, letters. How many sounds lie hidden between the connections? The bind feels like a whisper, a secret held between lines of text or melodies.

---

The first sip of coffee in the morning. The sun barely risen, casting a faint glow. It warms the room with promise but not heat. A thought drifts: what does a color sound like? Does violet hum, or is it more of a whisper?

---

The sensation of walking on a sandy beach at dusk, the sand cooling underfoot, the tide's edge a gentle murmur. The smell of salt and seaweed. How many footsteps have disappeared with the tide?

---

A rustling in the underbrush. The quiet presence of something alive, unseen. A shiver of anticipation. What remains when silence deepens?

---

The shape of a thought before it's fully formed. Something nebulous, like steam curling in the air. An idea that won't quite hold still.
