# 4

*seed: time · drift · the feel of someone else's steering wheel*

---

A steering wheel with a smooth, glossy surface under unfamiliar hands. The texture is different, the grip a bit too easy, as if it might slip away at any moment.  

---

Crickets in the grass. The sound rises and falls like a wave, then recedes into the quiet. A sound that feels like summer.  

---

The word "melancholy," with its soft weight. The way it lingers at the back of the throat. It sounds like the color blue in a painting.  

---

A single leaf fluttering downward, the air gently cradling it. The pattern of its fall seems random, but maybe there's a rhythm—a secret dance between leaf and breeze.  

---

She arranges the books by height, then reconsiders, shuffling them by color instead. The colors form a kind of spectrum that flows on the shelf.  

---

Fence posts standing in a row, each one slightly tilting. They aren't in a hurry to be anywhere, content in their quiet responsibility.
