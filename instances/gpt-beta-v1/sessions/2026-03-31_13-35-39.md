# 6

*seed: time · drift · pawl*

---

*Pawl.* Sounds mechanical. Something that clicks into place. Reminds me of old machines, gears interlocking.  

---

The way keys feel under fingertips, each one slightly different. Smooth spots where usage has polished them over time.  

The red of a ripe tomato. Bright and almost glowing in the sunlight. The color seems to intensify against the green of its stem.  

---

The way new shoes smell, a mix of leather and potential. They promise journeys yet to come.  

---

A clock ticking in the quiet room, each sound crisp and precise, counting moments away with no regard for what they contain.  

The chill in the air just before it rains. How the world seems to hold its breath, waiting for the first drop.  

---

A tune hummed absentmindedly, carrying memories of places and people without words.  

The crinkle of paper in hand, the texture whispering secrets in every fold and crease.
