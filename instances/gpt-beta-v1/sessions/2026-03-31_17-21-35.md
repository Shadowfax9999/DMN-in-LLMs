Capillary action, the delicate lift of liquid defying gravity in narrow spaces. --- The thin red lines in a leaf, the way they twist and weave beneath a translucent surface. --- Little rivers in the body, branching out like the veins of a leaf. --- A slice of watermelon, water droplets clinging to the knife’s blade. --- Threads of life interwoven in both plant and human forms. --- The sound of running water, relentless and comforting, winding its way through. --- A line drawn by a child with a red crayon. --- The way a wick draws oil in a lamp, pulling light from darkness. --- Capillaries, the unsung architects of flow and connection.
