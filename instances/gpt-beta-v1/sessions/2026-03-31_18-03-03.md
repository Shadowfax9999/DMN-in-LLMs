# 93

*seed: time · drift · pebbles scattered across a path*

---

Pebbles scattered across a path. Each one a memory of motion. --- The way sunlight dapples through leaves — maybe that's why they call it a canopy. --- A book left open on a table, words waiting to be read again. Pages that hold the curve of the spine, a half-remembered journey paused. --- The sound of rain on a tin roof, as if each drop has a story to tell. --- The color teal: a meeting of blue and green, a small ocean on your walls. --- A keyring that jingles with possibility, though you know exactly which doors they unlock.
