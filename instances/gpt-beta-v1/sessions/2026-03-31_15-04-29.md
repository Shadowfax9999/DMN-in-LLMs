# 42

*seed: time · drift · the particular nothing-smell of a hotel room*

---

A geranium wilts in the sun, its leaves curling brown at the edges. The sound of footsteps on gravel, crunching and crisp. A glass of water with lemon, the slice floating on the surface, casting a shadow at the bottom. 

The smell of rain on hot pavement. Scattered receipts on the floor of a car. A clock ticking softly, almost beneath notice, marking moments more than time itself. 

Jigsaw pieces on a coffee table, waiting to be assembled. The echo of a corridor, how sounds stretch and fade. 

---

The cool of a basement, untouched by the heat above. Light through a prism, scattering colors in unexpected places. An old book, its pages yellowed and soft, the scent of paper and history mingling. 

A forgotten scarf on the back of a chair. The quiet hum of a refrigerator, filling the blank space between breaths. 

The word "solitude" like a feather, balancing in midair.
