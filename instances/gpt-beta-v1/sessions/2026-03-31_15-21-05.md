# 50

*seed: time · drift*

---

Sunlight warms a patch of skin, a reminder of how seasons drift into each other without a sound. A glint of something shiny on a sidewalk, catching the eye for just a moment before the sun shifts and it's gone. The rustle of leaves when the wind moves through them, the way they whisper secrets to each other.  

---

A forgotten song playing from the radio, bringing back memories you didn't know you had. The chorus repeats itself as if to etch the moment into your mind.  

---

The delicate lace of frost on a winter window, its intricate designs ephemeral. A breath on the glass slowly erasing it, the warmth reclaiming the space it temporarily lost.  

The quiet tick of a clock in a mostly silent room, a metronome for thoughts that wander in and out of focus.  

---

A penny on the pavement, face up. The simple belief that good luck is just a matter of perspective.  

The smell of bread baking, an aroma that wraps around you like an old favorite sweater. The anticipation of breaking through the crust to the warmth inside.  

---

A balloon slipping from a child's grasp, racing toward the sky with a carefree urgency. The child watches, torn between tears and laughter.
