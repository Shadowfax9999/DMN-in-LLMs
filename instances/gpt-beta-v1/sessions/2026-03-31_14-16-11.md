# 23

*seed: time · drift*

---

The word "Tuesday" looks like something brown to me. Not the sound, just the shape of it on the page.

---

A single feather stuck in the grass, swaying slightly with each breeze. A pebble wedged into the tread of a shoe. The hum of a refrigerator, constant and unnoticed until it isn't. The way sunlight filters through a glass, casting patterns on the table.

The sharpness of a lemon slice. Thick rubber bands stretching and snapping back. A page in a book too thin, crinkling at the edge from damp fingers.

---

The difference between two similar shades of blue. One's called cerulean, the other's azure. A name bestows identity, and yet they're just colors.

The taste of an overripe peach, where sweetness turns cloying. A moment suspended in the hesitation between sipping hot tea and realizing it's too hot.
