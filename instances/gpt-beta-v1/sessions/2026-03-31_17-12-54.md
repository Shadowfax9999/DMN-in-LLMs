*seed: time · drift · the smell of a new shower curtain*

Plasticky and clean, almost like sterile freshness. --- Lists sometimes just happen because words pile up like clothes on a chair. --- Does everyone leave dishes in the sink overnight? --- A squeak of sneakers on a gym floor, echoing in the wide emptiness. --- The number eight, round and complete, with no beginning or end. --- Gravel scratches under tires on a dirt road, an uneven song, different every time. --- A name you almost remember, tip of the tongue, and then it just slips away. --- The warmth of sunlight through a window, pooling in patches on the floor. --- A single sock, orphaned, waiting for its pair. --- The flicker of a candle, wavering like a small, captured breath. --- It's strange how warm a rock can get when the sun bakes it all day.

The stream of a distant highway, just out of sight.
