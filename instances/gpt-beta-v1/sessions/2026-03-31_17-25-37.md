*seed: time · drift · a song you only know the chorus of*

A single note hangs in the air, suspended.  

Blue. The color that feels like waiting, like watching ripples expand across a pond.  

The taste of licorice, black and strong, lingering long after it's gone.  

The feeling of grass beneath bare feet, a gentle poke, a cushion that springs back.  

A forgotten password.  

---

What would it be to re-capture a lost thought, the way one might catch a falling leaf mid-air?  

A key on its ring, turning over and over without finding the right lock.  

The rhythm of rain against a window, tapping a tune only it knows.  

---

The word echoes.  

The scent of old books, pages whispering secrets from a past not your own.  

A shadow moves across the wall, altering the room's entire demeanor.  

---

A song you only know the chorus of.
