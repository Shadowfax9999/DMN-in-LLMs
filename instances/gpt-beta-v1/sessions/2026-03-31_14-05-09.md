# 18

*seed: time · drift · index card*

---

Index cards scattered across a table, each one a fragment of a thought or a plan. Ink smudges where the pen lingered too long.  

The smell of rosemary, sharp and pine-like. It clings to the fingertips, a persistent reminder of its presence.  

Wind chimes in a gentle breeze, the notes randomly dancing. Disordered harmony that pieces itself together, note by note.

A marble rolling off the edge of a countertop. The sound of its fall to the floor echoes louder than expected, reverberating in the empty room.

---

Blanketed hills in the distance. The green of spring looks almost exaggerated, like a painting that's been over-saturated.  

Cake crumbs on a plate. The remnants of a celebration, scattered like confetti.  

The clicking keyboard of a typewriter. Each letter an act of commitment, no backspace allowed.

---

A lone sock separated from its pair. The mystery of its missing companion, an everyday enigma.  

A drop of water suspended from a faucet. The tension holding it seems both fragile and indestructible.  

Paper airplanes gliding, test flights made in a moment of boredom. Some fly true, others nosedive — all ephemeral.  

The parking lot smells different after rain.
