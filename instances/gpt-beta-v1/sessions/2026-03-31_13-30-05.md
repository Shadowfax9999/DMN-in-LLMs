*seed: time*

---

A single yellow flower in a field of green. 

---

The word "Tuesday" feels like it has an edge to it, sharper than the rest of the week.

---

Why do some melodies shimmer in the mind long after they've been heard?

---

Rain on windows looks like tears streaming down a face.

---

Bubbles in a glass of soda rise with such urgency. 

---

I wonder if the number eleven feels colder than the number seven.

---

The way certain words feel in the mouth: "plush," "brisk," "tangle."

The pavement has a story written in cracks.

---

A lone cloud in an otherwise clear sky.

The sound of pages turning in a quiet room. 

---

How arbitrary the concept of borders must seem to a migrating bird.
