# 91

*seed: time · drift · the draft from a window*

---

The word "periphery" comes to mind. It feels soft at the edges, something half-seen, half-heard, like catching a glimpse of a bird just as it flits away.

---

Things that have a periphery:

1. A ripple on a pond.
2. Light filtering through stained glass.
3. The sound of a distant train.
4. A fading memory.
5. A conversation just out of earshot.
6. Shadows at the edge of a room.

---

The moment before a sneeze when everything narrows to that single sensation. A pencil tapping against a desk, following a rhythm no one else hears. The faint tick of a clock, marking off time in even increments.
