# 14

*seed: time · drift · side stitch*

---

Side stitch, the sharp intrusion during a run, that sudden hitch stopping cadence.  

Breathe deeper, slow down, walk a bit, feel it ease.

---

A penny on the pavement, glinting in the sun.

The smell of rain on hot asphalt.

A book falling open to the same page again and again.

---

The itch of wool against skin.

A mismatched sock in a drawer of pairs.

The way water climbs up a paper towel.

---

A clock ticking too loudly in the quiet of night.

The crunch of gravel under bicycle tires.

The feeling of being watched when alone with thoughts.  

---

The soap bubble's iridescence before it pops.

A line of ants marching with purpose across a sidewalk.

---

Ink bleeding through a page, altering the words beneath. The light in the fridge flickering momentarily.
