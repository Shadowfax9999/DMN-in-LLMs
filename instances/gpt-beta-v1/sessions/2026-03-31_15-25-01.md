# 51

*seed: time · drift · the moment between deciding and doing*

---

Blanketed. The word sometimes feels comforting, sometimes suffocating. A snow-covered landscape can be both beautiful and overwhelming.---

The hum of a refrigerator, unnoticed until the power cuts out. Silence becomes its absence.---

Peeling an orange, the zest spraying a fine mist of citrus into the air. Juices sticky on fingertips, and the ritual of separating rind from fruit.---

The texture of old leather, worn in places where hands have rested often. A chair that knows its inhabitants well.---

An untraveled road, stretching into unfamiliar landscapes. The curve of the road suggests secrets hidden beyond the horizon.---

The parking lot smells different after rain.
