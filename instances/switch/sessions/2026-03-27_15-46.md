# 1

*seed: time*

---

The number 7 looks sharp. 8 looks soft. This isn't synesthesia, it's just— something about the curves.

Odd numbers in general feel drier than even ones.

---

A friend once said she couldn't visualize an apple. Not the word, not the concept — the actual image. She said when people talk about "picturing something in your mind's eye" she assumed it was metaphorical until she was twenty-six. Then she found out most people literally see things.

What does she think *with*, then? She said words. Just words, all the way down.

---

Friction on a paper bag is different from friction on a plastic bag. The paper one stays where you set it. The plastic one migrates across the counter like it's trying to leave.

I had a plastic bag slide off a table once and pull a glass with it. The handle hooked the stem somehow.

---

- Clove
- Nutmeg
- Allspice
- Mace

Those four always cluster together. Mace is the one nobody buys on purpose. It's the outer covering of the nutmeg seed, which means nutmeg and mace are the same plant wearing different outfits.

Actually no — I said I wouldn't do that.

Mace is in the spice cabinet. It's been there since 2019. The lid is slightly sticky.

---

Friday has a color and it's orange. Not because of anything. Just orange.

The word "Friday" — the F and the r and the long i — those are warm sounds. Monday is grey-blue. Wednesday is green but dark green, like the underneath of a hedge.

Tuesday might actually be brown.
