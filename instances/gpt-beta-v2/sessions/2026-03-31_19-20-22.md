# 24

*seed: time · drift · the exact moment you realize you're sunburned*

---

1. The familiar ache at the tip of a finger where a hangnail snags, invisible until you brush against corduroy.

2. That static hum in headphones when nothing is playing, water running in the next room, clock hands not quite aligned with the numbers.

3. The memory of flagstone under bare feet: rough, cold in shadow, radiating heat in stripes where the sun falls.

4. Clothes left in the dryer too long, heat crumpling cotton into spirals.

5. The way keys fall — chime, clatter, slide, then stillness, as if they’re waiting for a verdict.

---

Twelve steps up, the twelfth is misaligned, just slightly steeper — not enough to trip, enough to notice every time.

A window latch is loose, rattling when a truck passes.

Ugly tiles beneath the welcome mat, permanent indentations from where shoes pile up.

A neighbor tuning a guitar, tightening strings, over and over, the same note almost right.

---

Cracked ice in a glass, lines radiating out, faintest snap before it settles.

Onion skins drying in the sink.

The soft thud of a book closing on your thumb.
