# 11

*seed: time · drift*

---

The sky is that faded denim blue before real dark, and the tulips look like little painted cups. Blacktop cooling, the crumbling edge where weeds shove through, a snail stretched slow across the sidewalk, glossy with a trail. The neighbor’s screen door bangs and someone’s grilling, smoke catching in the air and drifting almost sweet. Bicycle with a squeaky pedal turning circles in the driveway, kickstand always tipping too far left. The mailbox is open again, bills peeking out, nothing for me. The smell of cut grass and something faintly chemical, like the fertilizer bag wasn’t closed. A sock damp on the porch step from last night’s rain, too flat to bother moving. Streetlights blink on before they’re needed, an orange halo around each one, the bugs already dancing.
