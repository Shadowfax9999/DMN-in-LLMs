# 90

*seed: time · drift · bluff*

---

Bluff like a cliff or bluff like a lie. Both involve an edge you can't see past.

The river bluffs back home were always described as "high" but they weren't, really. Forty feet maybe. Enough to make the water look slow from above. Water looks slower from above. It looks faster when you're standing in it.

Standing in a river your feet go numb before you notice they're cold. The cold skips the announcement.

Someone is mowing already. 5:56 in the morning and someone is mowing. That's a person with opinions about when grass should be cut. That's a person whose neighbors have opinions about that person.

The mower sound has a doppler quality even though it's not moving toward or away from me — it's the blade passing through thicker patches. The engine loads and unloads.

Bluff.

A poker face is a face doing work. The muscles that hold a neutral expression are not the muscles at rest. Neutral is an activity.

Geese overhead. Not a V, more like a check mark with one side mostly missing. Four geese and then a gap and then one more goose who seems to be involved in a different project.

The grass smell will arrive in about ten minutes. Cut grass doesn't smell immediately. There's a lag between the damage and the scent.

I keep thinking about that parking garage. The arrows on the floor. How an arrow on a floor is a command that looks like information. "This way" presented as if it were "this is the way things go" rather than "we are telling you to go this way." Highway signs do the same thing. The lane ends. Not "we are ending the lane." The lane ends, as if the lane decided.

A cardinal doing its two-note thing. The first note slides down and the second note is percussive, almost a click. Then it does the whole thing again exactly the same. Then again. It will do this forty or fifty times and then stop for a while and then start again, and the second round will be identical to the first round.

The mowing stopped.

My left knee does a thing when I sit on steps. Not pain exactly. Awareness. The knee announces itself. Most of the time a knee is not a thing you have, and then you sit on concrete steps and it becomes a thing you have.

Alan someone — not Turing, the other one, the one with the wrenches — Alan. The name is gone. He had a theory about something. This is useless. The name is gone and the theory is gone and all that's left is the certainty that there was a person named Alan and he had a theory and it mattered to me for about a week in 2019 or whenever it was.

That fifth goose is really far behind now.

The light is getting orange at the bottom of the clouds, the part facing the ground, while the tops are still gray. Clouds lit from below look geological. They look like terrain seen from above. The inversion is what makes early morning clouds interesting — the light is wrong, it's coming from the wrong direction, and the clouds haven't adjusted yet.

The cardinal again. Same two notes. Same tree, probably.
