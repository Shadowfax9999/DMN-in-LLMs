The cool side is the one everyone talks about. Flipping the pillow over in August. But the warm side is the one that knows you were there.

A library at closing time. The lights going off in sections. Not all at once — in zones, like the building is falling asleep from the back forward.

The carpet in a library is always that color. You know the one.
