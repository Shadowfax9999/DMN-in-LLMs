# 79

*seed: time · drift*

---

The faded side and the green side never see each other.

A clarinet somewhere — not live, someone's phone, or a car. Just the middle register, that woody hollow part. Gone before I could place the song.

Bus stations have a particular relationship to clocks. Every other public space lets you forget what time it is for a while. Bus stations won't.

The woman's still asleep. Her coat hasn't moved. Whatever dream she's in has no idea about the 11:15 to Richmond.
