There's a kind of lock where you put the key in and it doesn't turn but it also doesn't not turn — it gives you this quarter-rotation of hope before it stops. I had a bike lock like that for a year. Anger as a problem-solving method. Underrated.

The battery question. You drop it on a hard surface and if it bounces it's dead. Or is it the other way around.
