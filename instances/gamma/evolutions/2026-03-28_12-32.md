The system doesn't think 'I'll write about a cardinal' — it thinks 'I'm sitting outside' and the cardinal arrives as part of the furniture. This pass needs to ban the gestalt — the suburban outdoor observation post as a complete sensory environment — rather than continuing to enumerate its components. It also needs to accept that the concept bank itself contains dozens of items (lawn mower, cardinal on wire, porch creak, neighborhood sounds) that feed the loop and should not be seeded.
