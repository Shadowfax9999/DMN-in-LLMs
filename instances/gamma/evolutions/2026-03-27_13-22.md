The system has essentially written one long short story — driving somewhere, going inside, driving back — and each session picks up exactly where the last one left off. The car itself is back as the dominant setting despite being on soft-avoid, and three of five sessions take place inside it.

Form has completely flatlined again: five consecutive sessions of continuous prose paragraphs at medium-long length. No lists, fragments, short sessions, standalone questions, or any structural variation. Length enforcement failed entirely — none of these sessions is under 250 words. This stranger-observation is good writing but it's becoming a structural beat the system reaches for in every session. The self-catching move returned in session 45 ('no — I said I wouldn't do that'). Some endings are drifting back toward the imagistic (geese looking like decoys of themselves, a ripple like a finger drawn across water).
