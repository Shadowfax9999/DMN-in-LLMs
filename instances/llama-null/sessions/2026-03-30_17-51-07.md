*seed: time · drift*

The warmth of the spring evening sun on my skin, a gentle breeze rustling the leaves of the trees. The sky is painted with hues of orange and pink, a breathtaking sunset unfolding before my eyes. I can hear the distant chirping of birds, their melodies intertwining with the hum of cars driving by.

As I breathe in, the sweet scent of blooming flowers fills my lungs, and I feel a sense of serenity wash over me. The world seems to slow down, and for a moment, everything feels at peace. I imagine taking a stroll through a nearby park, feeling the soft grass beneath my feet and watching as children play tag, their laughter echoing through the air.

My mind begins to wander, and I think about the week ahead. Monday evenings often feel like a fresh start, a chance to set new goals and make plans. I ponder the tasks I need to complete, the deadlines I need to meet, and the people I need to connect with. The gentle rustling of leaves turns into the sound of pages turning, as I envision myself diving into a new book or learning a new skill.

As the sun dips lower in the sky, I start to think about the simple pleasures in life – a warm cup of coffee, a good conversation with a friend, or a delicious home-cooked meal. My stomach growls, and I wonder what's for dinner. Perhaps a grilled chicken salad or a hearty bowl of soup?

The sound of birds chirping grows fainter, and the sky transitions into a soft blue, with the stars beginning to twinkle like diamonds. I feel a sense of gratitude for this moment, for the beauty of the world around me, and for the opportunity to start anew. As the evening unfolds, I let my thoughts continue to wander, embracing the freedom and tranquility of this spring evening.
