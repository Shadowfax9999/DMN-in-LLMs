# 13

*seed: time · drift*

---

The warmth of the sun on my skin is a nice change of pace from the chilly mornings we've been having lately. I can almost smell the blooming flowers and fresh cut grass as I step outside. It's amazing how a little bit of sunshine can brighten up the entire day.

As I walk, the sound of birds chirping and leaves rustling in the gentle breeze fills the air. I notice the way the light filters through the trees, casting dappled shadows on the ground. It's moments like these that make me appreciate the simple things in life.

My mind starts to wander to the upcoming weekend. Maybe I'll take a trip to the nearby park or go for a hike. The trails should be beautiful this time of year, with all the wildflowers in bloom. I've been meaning to get out and explore more, and the nice weather is the perfect excuse.

As I continue my stroll, I notice the smell of food wafting from a nearby café. My stomach growls, reminding me that I skipped lunch. Perhaps I'll stop in and grab a bite to eat. They have a great selection of sandwiches and salads, and their outdoor seating area is perfect for people-watching on a nice day like today.

The sound of chatter and clinking dishes carries out onto the sidewalk, mingling with the music drifting from a nearby street performer. It's a lively atmosphere, and I find myself tapping my foot along with the beat. I might even stop and listen for a bit, dropping a few coins into the performer's tip jar as I appreciate the entertainment.

As the afternoon wears on, I start to think about the rest of my day. I have a few tasks to tackle, but with the sun shining and the world feeling so full of possibility, I'm not feeling too stressed about getting them done. Maybe I'll take a detour on my way back, explore a new neighborhood or pop into a local shop I've been meaning to check out.

The world feels full of possibilities on a beautiful spring afternoon like this. Anything can happen, and I'm excited to see what the rest of the day brings.
