# 38

*seed: time · drift*

---

The warmth of the sun on my skin is a gentle reminder that spring is finally here. As I step outside, the sweet fragrance of blooming flowers wafts through the air, carrying the whispers of a season renewed. The sound of birds chirping and leaves rustling in the gentle breeze creates a soothing melody that seems to lull the world into a peaceful slumber.

I close my eyes, feeling the warmth seep into my skin, and let my thoughts drift. Memories of childhood spring afternoons spent playing in the park, chasing after butterflies, and laughing with friends begin to resurface. The smell of freshly cut grass and the taste of lemonade on a sunny day transport me back to a carefree era, where worries were few and laughter was plentiful.

As I breathe in the scents of the season, my mind starts to wander to the possibilities that spring brings. The promise of new beginnings, the chance to start anew, and the opportunity to watch the world around me transform into a vibrant tapestry of colors and life. I think about the plans I've been putting off, the projects I've been meaning to start, and the adventures I've been eager to embark on.

The sound of children's laughter in the distance breaks the spell, and I open my eyes to find a group of kids playing tag in the park. Their joyful shouts and giggles are infectious, and I find myself smiling as I watch them twirl and spin, their faces aglow with happiness.

As I stand there, taking in the sights and sounds of the spring afternoon, I feel a sense of contentment wash over me. The world may be busy and chaotic at times, but in this moment, all seems right with the world. The sun shines bright, the flowers bloom, and the laughter of children fills the air, reminding me that sometimes, it's the simple things in life that bring the most joy.
