# 54

*seed: time · drift*

---

The warm sunlight streaming through the window is a pleasant distraction. I gaze out at the vibrant blooms of spring, the gentle breeze rustling the leaves of the trees. The sound of birds chirping and the distant hum of a lawnmower create a soothing melody.

My mind starts to wander to the weekend that just passed. I recall the outdoor hike I took with friends, the scenic trails, and the breathtaking views from the top of the hill. The smell of freshly cut grass and the taste of a cold beverage on a warm day still linger in my memory.

As I sit here, I start to think about the upcoming summer months. The long, lazy days, the beach trips, and the countless possibilities for adventure. I imagine the smell of saltwater, the sound of the waves crashing against the shore, and the feeling of sand between my toes.

My thoughts then shift to the present moment, and I realize I have a few hours left before the day comes to a close. I consider taking a walk outside, feeling the warmth of the sun on my skin, and perhaps stopping by a nearby café for a cup of coffee and a pastry.

The freedom to let my thoughts roam is exhilarating. I feel my mind expanding, making connections between seemingly unrelated ideas, and exploring new possibilities. The world outside my window is full of life, color, and sound, and I'm grateful to be a part of it, if only for this moment.
