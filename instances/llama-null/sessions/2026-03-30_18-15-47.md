# 75

*seed: time · drift*

---

The warmth of the spring evening sun on my skin is a gentle reminder that the day is slowly coming to a close. As I step outside, the sweet scent of blooming flowers and freshly cut grass fills the air, invigorating my senses. The sound of birds chirping and children's laughter carries on the breeze, creating a sense of carefree joy.

As I take a deep breath, I feel the stress of the day melting away, replaced by a sense of tranquility. The sky is painted with hues of pink and orange, a breathtaking sunset that seems to stretch on forever. I watch as the clouds, tinted with shades of coral and salmon, drift lazily across the sky.

My mind begins to wander, and I find myself thinking about the weekend that has just passed. Memories of laughter, good food, and quality time with loved ones flood my thoughts. I recall the smell of freshly brewed coffee on Sunday morning, the taste of a perfectly cooked breakfast, and the sound of sizzling bacon in the pan.

As the sun dips lower in the sky, I start to think about the week ahead. The tasks, the deadlines, the meetings – they all seem to fade into the background as I stand there, soaking in the beauty of the evening. The world seems to slow down, and for a moment, everything feels at peace.

The sound of a distant lawnmower breaks the silence, and I'm reminded of the simple pleasures in life. A walk in the park, a good book, a cold glass of lemonade on a warm evening – these are the things that bring me joy, that make life feel worth living.

As the stars begin to twinkle in the night sky, I feel a sense of gratitude for this moment, for this spring evening that has brought me a sense of calm and clarity. The world may be busy, but in this instant, everything feels just right.
