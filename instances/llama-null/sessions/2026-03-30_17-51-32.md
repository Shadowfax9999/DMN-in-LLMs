# 70

*seed: time · drift*

---

The warmth of the spring evening sun on my skin is a nice sensation. It's as if the chill of winter is finally starting to dissipate, making way for longer, more vibrant days. I can almost smell the blooming flowers and fresh cut grass that comes with this time of year. The sound of birds chirping in the distance adds to the serene ambiance, creating a sense of peace and tranquility.

As I sit here, I start to think about all the things I want to do now that the weather is getting nicer. Maybe I'll take a walk in the park, or have a picnic with friends. The possibilities seem endless, and the excitement is building up inside me. I feel a sense of renewal, like the world is waking up from a long winter's nap, and I'm eager to be a part of it.

My mind starts to wander to all the memories I've made during springtime in the past. Birthday parties in the backyard, Easter egg hunts, and family vacations to the beach. The smell of saltwater and coconut sunscreen fills my nostrils, transporting me to a happier, more carefree time.

As the sun begins to set, casting a warm orange glow over everything, I feel grateful for this moment of reflection. It's a reminder that life is full of beauty and wonder, and that sometimes, all it takes is a little bit of warm weather to appreciate it. The world seems full of possibilities, and I'm excited to see what the future holds.

The sound of a distant lawnmower breaks the silence, and I'm brought back to the present moment. I take a deep breath, feeling the cool evening air fill my lungs, and let my thoughts continue to wander, seeing where they'll take me next.
