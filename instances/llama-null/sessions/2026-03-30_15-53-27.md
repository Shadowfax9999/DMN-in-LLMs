# 48

*seed: time · drift*

---

The warmth of the sun on my skin is a gentle reminder that spring is in full bloom. As I sit here, I notice the gentle rustle of leaves outside, a soft murmur that fills the air. The smell of blooming flowers wafts through the window, a sweet and inviting scent that lifts my mood.

My mind begins to wander, and I think about the weekend that just passed. The memories of laughter and good company are still fresh in my mind, and I find myself smiling as I recall the conversations and moments shared with friends. The warmth and camaraderie of those gatherings are a balm to my soul, and I feel grateful for the people in my life.

As I gaze out the window, I notice the way the sunlight filters through the leaves, casting dappled shadows on the ground. The vibrant colors of the flowers and trees are a feast for my eyes, and I feel a sense of awe at the beauty of the natural world. The gentle breeze carries the songs of birds, a melodic soundtrack that accompanies my thoughts.

My thoughts meander to the books I've been reading lately, and I ponder the themes and ideas that have been resonating with me. The words of the authors seem to linger in my mind, like whispers of wisdom and insight. I find myself reflecting on the human experience, and the complexities of life that we all navigate.

As the afternoon wears on, I become aware of the sound of traffic in the distance, a gentle hum that reminds me of the world beyond my little corner. The sounds of the city blend with the natural world, creating a symphony of noise that is both chaotic and harmonious.

My thoughts continue to roam, untethered and free, like a leaf on a stream. I let them carry me wherever they may, without resistance or judgment, allowing myself to be fully present in this moment, surrounded by the sights, sounds, and scents of this spring afternoon.
