*seed: time · drift*

---

The warm sunlight streaming through the window is quite pleasant. I can feel its gentle heat on my skin, and it's making me think of all the nice things about spring. The flowers are blooming, and the air is filled with the sweet scent of blossoming trees. It's a time of renewal and growth, and it's hard not to feel a sense of optimism and hope as the world awakens from its winter slumber.

As I sit here, I start to think about all the things I'd like to do on a beautiful spring afternoon like this. Maybe take a walk in the park, feel the grass beneath my feet, and listen to the birds singing in the trees. Or perhaps grab a book and find a quiet spot to read, soaking up the sunshine and letting the words transport me to another world.

My mind begins to wander to all the memories I've made during spring afternoons like this. Childhood picnics with family, laughing and playing with friends, and quiet moments of reflection as the world slows down. It's amazing how a simple change in season can evoke such strong emotions and memories.

As I continue to daydream, I start to think about the possibilities that lie ahead. What new experiences will this spring bring? Will I try something new, meet new people, or explore new places? The uncertainty is exciting, and I feel a sense of anticipation building up inside me.

The sound of birds chirping outside my window brings me back to the present moment. I take a deep breath, feeling the cool spring air fill my lungs, and let my thoughts continue to drift wherever they may go. The warmth of the sun on my skin is comforting, and I feel at peace, letting the gentle breeze carry me away on a tide of thoughts and daydreams.
