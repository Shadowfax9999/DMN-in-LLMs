# 88

*seed: time · drift*

---

The sounds of the city are slowly fading into the background as the sun sets on this spring evening. I can almost smell the blooming flowers and fresh cut grass wafting through the air. The temperature is just right, not too hot, not too cold, with a gentle breeze that carries the whispers of leaves rustling in the trees.

As I sit here, my mind starts to wander to all the possibilities that a new week brings. Monday evenings often feel like a fresh start, a chance to reset and tackle new challenges. I think about the tasks I need to accomplish, the goals I want to achieve, and the people I want to connect with.

My gaze drifts out the window, where the sky is painted with hues of pink, orange, and purple. The stars are beginning to twinkle, like diamonds scattered across the velvet expanse. I feel a sense of wonder and awe at the vastness of the universe, and the mystery that lies beyond our small corner of the world.

As the evening wears on, I start to think about the simple pleasures in life – a good cup of coffee, a warm conversation with a friend, or a great book that transports me to another world. I ponder the idea of taking a walk outside, feeling the cool breeze on my skin, and listening to the sounds of nature as the world slows down.

My thoughts meander to memories of spring evenings past, spent laughing with loved ones, watching fireworks light up the sky, or simply enjoying the peacefulness of a quiet night. I smile, remembering the joy and contentment that those moments brought, and I look forward to creating new memories in the days to come.

As the clock strikes 20:15, I take a deep breath, feeling the calmness of the evening settle in. The world may be busy and chaotic at times, but in this moment, all is right with the world. And as I sit here, surrounded by the gentle sounds of spring, I feel grateful for this fleeting moment of peace, and the promise of a new week full of possibilities.
