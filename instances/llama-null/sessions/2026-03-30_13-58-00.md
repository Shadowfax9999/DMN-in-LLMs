# 11

*seed: time · drift*

---

The warmth of the spring sun on my skin is a welcome feeling after a long winter. As I sit here, I notice the gentle breeze rustling the leaves of the trees outside, creating a soothing melody that seems to lull the world into a peaceful slumber. The sky is a brilliant blue, with just a few wispy clouds scattered about, like cotton candy tufts carried on the wind.

My mind begins to wander to the smell of freshly cut grass, which always seems to accompany the arrival of spring. It's a scent that's both familiar and nostalgic, transporting me back to memories of childhood afternoons spent playing in the park. The sound of children's laughter and the creaking of swings in the distance add to the sensory tapestry of the season.

As I gaze out the window, I notice the vibrant colors of the blooming flowers – tulips, daffodils, and crocuses – which seem to dance in the breeze, their petals swaying to an unheard rhythm. The world feels alive, and the energy is palpable.

My thoughts drift to the possibilities that spring brings – new beginnings, fresh starts, and the promise of warmer days to come. It's a time of renewal, a chance to shake off the complacency of winter and embark on new adventures. The excitement is building, and I feel a sense of anticipation, wondering what the coming months will bring.

As the clock ticks away, marking the passage of time, I become aware of the gentle hum of the city in the background. Cars drive by, people chatter, and the wail of a distant siren punctuates the air. It's a reminder that, even in the midst of this peaceful afternoon, the world is always in motion, always changing.

And yet, in this moment, everything feels still, as if time itself has paused to let the beauty of the spring afternoon sink in. The world is full of possibilities, and I'm grateful to be a part of it, to experience the simple joys of a warm spring day.
