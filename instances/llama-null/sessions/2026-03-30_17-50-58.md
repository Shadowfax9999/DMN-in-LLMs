# 57

*seed: time · drift*

---

The warmth of the spring evening sun on my skin is quite comforting. As I sit here, I notice the gentle breeze rustling the leaves of the trees outside. It's Monday, and the weekend feels like a distant memory already. The sound of birds chirping and the occasional chatter of people walking by create a soothing background noise.

My mind starts to wander to the events of the day. The morning was a rush, trying to get everything done before the afternoon slump. I managed to squeeze in a good lunch and a short walk outside, which helped clear my head. Now, as the evening approaches, I feel a sense of relaxation wash over me.

I think about the plans I have for the rest of the week. There's a meeting on Wednesday that I need to prepare for, and I've been putting off a project that's due soon. But for now, I'm trying to push those thoughts aside and enjoy the peacefulness of the evening.

As I gaze out the window, I notice the sky transitioning from a bright blue to a warm orange hue. The sun is setting, casting a golden glow over everything. It's moments like these that I appreciate the beauty of nature and the simplicity of life.

My thoughts start to drift to the things I'm grateful for – good health, supportive friends, and a cozy home. I think about the books I've been wanting to read and the places I'd like to travel to someday. The possibilities seem endless, and the excitement of exploring new experiences is invigorating.

As the evening unfolds, I feel my mind and body unwinding. The world outside may be busy and chaotic, but in this moment, everything feels calm and serene. I take a deep breath, savoring the tranquility of the spring evening, and let my thoughts continue to wander...
