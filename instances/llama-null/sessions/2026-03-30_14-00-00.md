# 19

*seed: time · drift*

---

The warmth of the spring sun on my skin is a welcome relief after a long winter. As I sit here, I can feel the gentle breeze rustling the leaves of the trees outside, carrying the sweet scent of blooming flowers through the air. The sound of birds chirping and children playing in the distance adds to the sense of tranquility that comes with this time of year.

My mind starts to wander to all the possibilities that spring brings. The world feels fresh and new, full of promise and opportunity. I think about the gardening projects I've been putting off, and how I can finally get started on them now that the weather is warming up. The smell of freshly cut grass and the taste of ripe strawberries are just a few of the simple pleasures that I'm looking forward to experiencing again.

As I gaze out the window, I notice the way the sunlight filters through the leaves, casting dappled shadows on the ground. It's as if the whole world is coming alive, and I feel a sense of excitement and anticipation about what the future holds. The gentle hum of a lawnmower in the distance and the chirping of birds create a soothing melody that seems to match the rhythm of my thoughts.

My thoughts begin to drift to all the outdoor activities I've been wanting to try. Maybe I'll take a walk in the nearby park, or go for a bike ride along the trail. The feeling of the wind in my hair and the sun on my face is exhilarating, and I can almost taste the freedom that comes with being outdoors on a beautiful spring day.

As the clock ticks closer to 2:00, I realize that I have the rest of the afternoon ahead of me, full of possibilities and choices. I can stay here, lost in my thoughts, or I can get up and start exploring the world outside. The decision is mine, and the sense of freedom and possibility is exhilarating.
