# 37

*seed: time · drift*

---

The warmth of the sun on my skin is a nice feeling. I wonder what the rest of the day will bring. Maybe I'll take a walk outside later and enjoy the blooming flowers. The smell of freshly cut grass is always a pleasant surprise this time of year.

I think about all the things I need to do this week, but for now, I'll just let my mind wander. A memory from childhood pops up – playing in the park on a sunny day like today, laughing with friends, and feeling carefree. It's amazing how a simple sensation like warm sunlight can bring back such vivid memories.

As I sit here, I notice the sounds around me. Birds chirping, the gentle hum of a lawnmower in the distance, and the occasional chatter of people walking by. It's a soothing background noise that seems to match the rhythm of my thoughts.

My gaze drifts out the window, and I watch as the leaves rustle in the breeze. The trees are still getting used to the warmer weather, and their branches sway gently, like they're dancing to an unheard melody. I feel my thoughts slowing down, becoming more relaxed, as I take in the serene atmosphere.

Perhaps I'll grab a cup of coffee and sit outside for a bit, soaking up the sunshine and letting my mind continue to wander. The world seems to be moving at a slower pace today, and I'm happy to join in, savoring the tranquility of this spring afternoon.
