# 62

*seed: time · drift*

---

The warmth of the spring evening sun on my skin is a nice feeling. It's as if the world is slowly coming back to life after a cold winter. I can almost smell the fragrance of blooming flowers and fresh-cut grass wafting through the air.

As I sit here, I start to think about the week ahead. Monday evenings can be a bit of a drag, but there's something about the promise of a new week that's exciting. It's a chance to start fresh, to tackle new challenges, and to make progress on projects that have been lingering.

My mind begins to wander to all the things I want to accomplish this week. I think about the tasks I need to complete, the meetings I need to attend, and the deadlines I need to meet. But amidst all the hustle and bustle, I also think about taking some time for myself. Maybe I can squeeze in a walk in the park, or try out a new recipe in the kitchen.

As I gaze out the window, I notice the sky is painted with hues of pink and orange, a beautiful sunset unfolding before my eyes. It's a reminder that even on a busy Monday evening, there's always time to appreciate the beauty in the world around us.

My thoughts start to drift to the people in my life, and I wonder what they're up to at this moment. Are they enjoying the evening, or are they busy with their own tasks and responsibilities? I think about sending a text or making a phone call to catch up with someone, but then I decide to let the moment just be, and simply enjoy the quiet.

The sound of birds chirping and leaves rustling in the gentle breeze fills the air, and I feel my mind start to unwind. It's as if the world is slowing down, and I'm being reminded to take a deep breath, and be present in this moment.

And then, my thoughts take a turn, and I start to think about all the possibilities that lie ahead. The future is full of unknowns, but it's also full of promise and potential. I think about the dreams I want to chase, the goals I want to achieve, and the experiences I want to have.

As the sun dips below the horizon, casting a warm glow over the landscape, I feel a sense of peace wash over me. It's a feeling of being exactly where I need to be, in this moment, on this spring evening. And with that, my thoughts continue to wander, carried away by the gentle breeze, and the endless possibilities that the future holds.
