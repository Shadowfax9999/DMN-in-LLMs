# 64

*seed: time · drift*

---

The warmth of the spring evening sun on my skin, a gentle breeze carrying the sweet scent of blooming flowers. The sound of birds chirping in the distance, their melodies intertwining with the faint hum of cars driving by. As I breathe in, the smell of freshly cut grass wafts through the air, transporting me to memories of lazy summer afternoons spent in the park.

My mind begins to wander, and I imagine myself walking through a bustling city, the sounds and smells of the evening unfolding before me. The aroma of street food wafts through the air, enticing passersby with the promise of savory delights. People of all ages hurry to and fro, each with their own destination and story to tell.

As I continue my mental stroll, I find myself at a quaint little café, surrounded by the soft glow of string lights and the gentle chatter of patrons. The barista, with a warm smile, hands me a steaming cup of coffee, and I take a sip, feeling the rich flavors dance on my palate. The world outside fades into the background, and I am lost in the simple pleasures of the moment.

My thoughts drift further, and I envision a tranquil forest, the trees swaying gently in the breeze, their leaves rustling softly. A narrow stream runs through the center, its crystal-clear waters reflecting the vibrant hues of the sky. As I follow the stream, I come across a picturesque waterfall, its misty veil rising into the air, creating a mesmerizing spectacle.

As the sun dips lower in the sky, casting a warm orange glow over the landscape, I find myself back in the present, the spring evening unfolding before me like a canvas of endless possibilities. The world is full of wonder, and my mind is free to explore, to dream, and to create.
