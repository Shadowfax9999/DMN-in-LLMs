# 49

*seed: time · drift*

---

The warmth of the sun on my skin is a nice change from the chilly mornings we've been having. I wonder if the flowers in the park are blooming yet. The ones near the lake are usually the first to show their colors. Maybe I'll take a walk there later and see if the cherry blossoms are out. 

I heard the ice cream truck driving by earlier, and it made me think of summer days spent playing outside with friends. We'd chase after the truck, trying to flag it down, and then debate which flavor to get. Those carefree days seem so far away now.

The sound of birds chirping outside is a soothing accompaniment to my thoughts. I should probably open the window to let in some fresh air. As I get up to do that, I notice the pile of books on my desk. I've been meaning to read that new novel, but I haven't had a chance yet. Perhaps I'll take it with me to the park and read under a tree.

Oh, and I almost forgot – I need to call my sister later to catch up. We've been meaning to talk for weeks, but our schedules haven't aligned. I hope she's doing well. I should also check the weather forecast for the rest of the week. It would be nice to plan some outdoor activities if the weather holds up.

The smell of coffee wafts from the kitchen, reminding me that I should probably make a fresh cup. As I head to the kitchen, my mind starts to wander to all the things I need to do this week. But for now, I think I'll just savor this peaceful spring afternoon and let my thoughts meander...
