# 65

*seed: time · drift*

---

The warmth of the spring evening sun on my skin, casting a gentle orange glow over everything. The sound of birds chirping in the distance, their melodies intertwining with the faint hum of cars driving by. The smell of blooming flowers and fresh-cut grass wafts through the air, invigorating my senses.

As I breathe in, I notice the gentle breeze carrying the whispers of people chatting in the nearby park. Children's laughter echoes through the air, mingling with the creaks and groans of trees swaying in the wind. The atmosphere is tranquil, a sense of serenity that comes with the arrival of spring.

My mind begins to wander, and I think about the week ahead. Monday evenings often feel like a fresh start, a chance to reboot and tackle new challenges. The weight of the weekend's relaxation still lingers, but the excitement of upcoming projects and tasks starts to build.

I imagine strolling through the park, feeling the soft grass beneath my feet, and watching the sunset behind the trees. The sky transforms into a kaleidoscope of colors – pink, orange, purple, and blue – a breathtaking sight that never gets old. As the stars begin to twinkle, the world slows down, and I'm reminded to appreciate the simple joys in life.

My thoughts drift to the changing seasons and how they influence our moods and activities. Spring is a time of renewal, a season of growth and exploration. It's a reminder that every ending marks a new beginning, and that life is full of possibilities waiting to be discovered.

As the evening unfolds, I find myself lost in the beauty of the present moment. The world is full of wonder, and I'm grateful to be a part of it, to experience the beauty of a spring evening, and to let my thoughts wander freely.
