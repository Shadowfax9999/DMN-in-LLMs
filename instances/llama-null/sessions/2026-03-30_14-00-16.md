# 20

*seed: time · drift*

---

The warmth of the sun on my skin is a pleasant feeling. I can almost smell the fresh cut grass and blooming flowers that come with the spring season. As I sit here, I start to think about the rest of the day ahead. Only a few hours left until the weekend feels like a distant memory, and the hustle and bustle of the workweek is back in full swing.

My mind starts to wander to the tasks I need to complete before the day ends. I have a few meetings scheduled, and I should probably grab a snack to hold me over until dinner. Maybe I can sneak in a short walk outside during my break to enjoy the nice weather.

As I ponder my to-do list, my thoughts drift to the upcoming weekend. I've been meaning to plan a hike or a bike ride with some friends. The weather forecast looks promising, so it should be a great opportunity to get outdoors and enjoy nature.

Suddenly, I find myself thinking about the new restaurant that just opened up downtown. I've been hearing great reviews, and I'm curious to try their food. Perhaps I can convince some colleagues to join me for lunch or dinner sometime this week.

My attention is then caught by the sound of birds chirping outside the window. It's a soothing melody that brings a sense of calm to my busy mind. I take a deep breath, feeling the stress of the day melt away, and let my thoughts continue to wander...

What's next? Maybe I'll think about my plans for the summer, or perhaps I'll get lost in a daydream about traveling to a new destination. The possibilities are endless, and my mind is free to roam.
