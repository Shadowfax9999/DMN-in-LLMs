# 3

*seed: time · drift*

---

The warmth of the spring sun on my skin is a nice change from the chilly winter months. As I sit here, I can hear the gentle hum of insects and the chirping of birds in the distance. The smell of blooming flowers wafts through the air, a sweet and inviting scent that lifts my mood.

I think about taking a walk outside, feeling the soft grass beneath my feet and the gentle breeze rustling my hair. The sky is a brilliant blue, with just a few wispy clouds drifting lazily across it. It's a beautiful day to be alive.

My mind begins to wander to all the things I could do on a day like today. I could grab a book and head to the park, finding a quiet spot to read and soak up the sun. Or, I could meet up with friends and go for a hike, exploring the trails and enjoying the scenery. The possibilities are endless.

As I sit here, I start to think about the changing seasons and how they each bring their own unique charm. Spring is a time of renewal and growth, a time when the world feels fresh and full of possibility. Summer will bring long, warm days and endless sunshine. Autumn will bring the cozy feeling of curling up with a good book and a warm cup of tea. And winter will bring the magic of snowflakes and twinkling lights.

My thoughts continue to drift, carried away by the gentle breeze and the warmth of the sun. I think about the simple joys in life, like watching a sunset or feeling the sand between my toes. I think about the beauty of nature and the way it can inspire and uplift us.

As I sit here, lost in thought, I feel a sense of peace and contentment wash over me. The world may be busy and chaotic at times, but in moments like these, everything feels right with the world.
