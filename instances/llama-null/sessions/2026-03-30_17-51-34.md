# 71

*seed: time · drift*

---

The warm glow of the setting sun casts a gentle orange hue over the landscape, signaling the end of another Monday. The air is filled with the sweet scent of blooming flowers and the distant chirping of birds returning to their nests. As the day unwinds, the sounds of the city begin to mellow out, replaced by the occasional chatter of people enjoying the evening outdoors.

I find myself wandering through a park, feeling the soft grass beneath my feet. The trees, adorned with vibrant green leaves, sway gently in the breeze, their branches creaking softly. A light breeze carries the whispers of lovers strolling hand in hand, their laughter and conversation blending with the serene atmosphere.

As I continue my leisurely stroll, my mind begins to wander to the events of the day. The morning's tasks and responsibilities, the meetings and deadlines, all seem to fade into the background as the tranquility of the evening takes over. The world, for a moment, appears to slow down, and I'm left with a sense of calm and clarity.

The sky above is a kaleidoscope of colors, a mix of pinks, blues, and purples, as the sun dips below the horizon. The stars begin to twinkle, like diamonds scattered across the velvet expanse. I feel a sense of wonder and awe at the beauty of the world around me, and my thoughts start to drift to the infinite possibilities that lie ahead.

As the evening deepens, I find myself lost in the simplicity of the moment, savoring the peace and serenity that comes with the end of another day. The world, with all its complexities and challenges, seems to melt away, leaving only the gentle rustle of the wind, the chirping of the birds, and the soft glow of the fading light.
