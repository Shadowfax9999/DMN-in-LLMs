# 34

*seed: time · drift*

---

The warm sunlight streaming through the window is a pleasant surprise on a Monday afternoon. As I sit here, I notice the gentle breeze rustling the leaves of the trees outside, carrying the sweet scent of blooming flowers into the air. It's as if the world is slowly waking up from its winter slumber, and the vibrant colors of spring are starting to emerge.

My mind begins to wander to the weekend that just passed. I recall the long walk I took in the park, feeling the soft grass beneath my feet and listening to the chirping of birds. The smell of freshly cut grass and the sound of children's laughter still linger in my memory. It was a perfect day to recharge and prepare for the week ahead.

As I sit here, I start to think about the tasks I need to complete today. The to-do list on my desk seems to be staring at me, reminding me of the deadlines and responsibilities that await. But for now, I'm going to let my thoughts drift away from the mundane tasks and focus on the simple pleasures in life.

Perhaps I'll take a short break later and grab a cup of coffee from the café down the street. Their spring-themed latte art is always a delight, and the barista's friendly smile is just what I need to brighten up my day. Or maybe I'll take a few minutes to browse through a book or watch a video about a topic that interests me, just to spark some creativity and inspiration.

As the afternoon wears on, I wonder what the rest of the week will bring. Will it be a productive and successful few days, or will unexpected challenges arise? Whatever the case, I'm feeling grateful for this moment of calm, surrounded by the sights and sounds of spring, and I'm ready to take on whatever comes next.
