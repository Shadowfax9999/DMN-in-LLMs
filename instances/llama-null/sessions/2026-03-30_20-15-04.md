# 84

*seed: time · drift*

---

The warmth of the spring evening is quite soothing. As I sit here, I can feel the gentle breeze rustling the leaves of the trees outside, carrying the sweet scent of blooming flowers. It's as if the world has finally awoken from its winter slumber, and the vibrant colors of nature are once again on full display.

I think about the day that has passed, and how the sunshine had cast a warm glow over everything. People were out and about, enjoying the pleasant weather, and the sound of birds singing filled the air. It's days like these that make me appreciate the beauty of the world around us.

As I gaze out the window, I notice the sky transforming into a kaleidoscope of colors – hues of pink, orange, and purple – as the sun sets slowly below the horizon. The stars are beginning to twinkle, like diamonds scattered across the velvet expanse. It's a breathtaking sight, one that never fails to leave me in awe.

My mind starts to wander, thinking about all the possibilities that the upcoming week holds. Will the weather continue to be this pleasant, or will the unpredictability of spring bring about unexpected changes? What new experiences and adventures await me, and how will I make the most of the time that lies ahead?

As the evening deepens, I feel a sense of calm wash over me. The world outside may be bustling with activity, but in this moment, all is quiet and still. It's a time for reflection, a time to let go of the worries and concerns of the day, and simply be present in the beauty of the spring evening.
