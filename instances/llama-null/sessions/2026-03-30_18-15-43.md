# 73

*seed: time · drift*

---

The warmth of the spring evening sun on my skin is a lovely feeling. As I sit here, I can hear the distant chirping of birds returning to their nests, a soothing melody that seems to match the gentle rustling of leaves in the breeze. The air is filled with the sweet scent of blooming flowers, their vibrant colors adding a pop of beauty to the landscape.

As I gaze out the window, I notice the sky transforming into a kaleidoscope of hues – soft pinks, oranges, and purples – a breathtaking sunset that never gets old. The world seems to slow down, and for a moment, everything feels at peace.

My mind starts to wander, thinking about the events of the day. The morning was a rush, getting everything done before the deadline, but now, with the evening unfolding, I can finally take a deep breath and unwind. I think about the plans for the upcoming weekend, a hike in the nearby woods, and the excitement builds up inside me.

The sound of a gentle stream in the distance catches my attention, and I imagine myself sitting by its banks, watching the water flow, feeling the cool mist on my skin. It's amazing how the simplest things in life can bring so much joy and serenity.

As the sun dips lower, casting a warm glow over everything, I feel grateful for this moment of tranquility. The world may be busy and chaotic at times, but in this instant, all is right with the world. And as I sit here, soaking in the beauty of the spring evening, I let my thoughts drift, carried away by the soothing sounds and scents of nature.
