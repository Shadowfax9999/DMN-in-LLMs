# 72

*seed: time · drift*

---

The warmth of the spring evening sun on my skin, gentle and calming. As I breathe in, the sweet scent of blooming flowers and fresh-cut grass fills my lungs. The sound of birds chirping and leaves rustling in the gentle breeze creates a soothing melody.

My mind begins to wander, and I find myself thinking about the weekend that just passed. Memories of laughter and good times with friends and family start to resurface. We had a barbecue in the park, and the smell of sizzling burgers and hot dogs still lingers in my mind.

As I continue to relax, my thoughts drift to the upcoming week. I start to think about the projects I need to work on, the deadlines I need to meet, and the tasks I need to complete. But for now, I push those thoughts aside and let the tranquility of the evening wash over me.

The sky is painted with hues of pink, orange, and purple as the sun begins to set. I imagine myself walking along a beach, feeling the soft sand between my toes and the cool ocean breeze on my skin. The sound of the waves crashing against the shore creates a sense of peace and serenity.

As the stars start to twinkle in the night sky, I find myself lost in thought, contemplating the mysteries of the universe. I wonder about the infinite possibilities that lie beyond our planet, the secrets that the cosmos holds, and the magic that happens when stars are born and die.

The world is full of wonder and awe, and in this moment, I feel grateful to be a part of it. The spring evening has cast a spell of tranquility over me, and I let myself drift into the beauty and simplicity of the present moment.
