# 4

*seed: time · drift*

---

The warmth of the spring sun on my skin is a welcome change from the chill of winter. As I sit here, I notice the gentle rustle of leaves in the trees outside, their green hues a vibrant contrast to the dull grays and browns of the colder months. The air is filled with the sweet scent of blooming flowers, and I can hear the distant chirping of birds, their melodies a symphony of joy and new life.

My thoughts begin to wander to the smell of fresh-cut grass, and I'm reminded of afternoons spent playing in the park as a child. The feel of the warm sun on my face, the smell of the grass, and the sound of laughter and playful shouts all blend together to create a sense of carefree joy.

As I continue to sit here, I start to think about the changing of the seasons and how each one brings its own unique charm. The coziness of winter, the vibrancy of spring, the warmth of summer, and the coziness of autumn all have their own special qualities. And yet, as much as I enjoy the changing of the seasons, I also appreciate the consistency and routine that comes with each one.

My mind starts to drift to the concept of time and how it seems to pass so quickly. It feels like just yesterday was the start of a new year, and now we're already in the midst of spring. The days blend together, and before I know it, another year will have come and gone. It's a reminder to appreciate the present moment and make the most of the time I have.

As I sit here, lost in thought, I notice the sound of a gentle breeze rustling through the trees. It's a soothing sound, one that seems to wash away my worries and concerns. My thoughts begin to slow, and I feel a sense of calm wash over me. The world outside may be busy and chaotic, but in this moment, all is right with the world.
