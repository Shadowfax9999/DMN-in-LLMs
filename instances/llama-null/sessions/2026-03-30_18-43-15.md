# 77

*seed: time · drift*

---

The warmth of the spring evening sun on my skin, a gentle breeze rustling the leaves of the trees. The sound of birds chirping in the distance, a melody that never gets old. I find myself standing in a quiet neighborhood, the smell of blooming flowers and fresh-cut grass filling the air.

As I breathe in, I notice the sky transforming into a canvas of hues – soft pinks, blushing peaches, and gentle lavenders. The sun, a burning orange, slowly dips below the horizon, casting a warm glow over the landscape. The stars begin to twinkle, like diamonds scattered across the velvet expanse.

My mind starts to wander, and I think about the day that's passed. The morning's hustle and bustle, the sound of coffee brewing, and the smell of freshly baked bread wafting from the nearby bakery. The conversations, the laughter, and the quiet moments of solitude all blend together in a beautiful tapestry.

As the evening deepens, I find myself strolling through the streets, taking in the sights and sounds of the neighborhood. The smell of barbecue wafts from a nearby backyard, and I can hear the distant chatter of friends and family gathered together. The world feels at peace, and I feel a sense of contentment wash over me.

My thoughts begin to drift to the future, to the possibilities that tomorrow may bring. The promise of a new day, full of opportunities and experiences waiting to be had. The thrill of the unknown, the excitement of discovery, and the joy of connection with the people and world around me.

As the stars grow brighter, I feel my thoughts expanding, like the universe itself. I ponder the mysteries of the cosmos, the secrets of the human heart, and the intricate web of relationships that bind us all together. The world is a complex, beautiful, and ever-changing tapestry, and I feel grateful to be a part of it, if only for a moment.

And so, I stand here, in this quiet neighborhood, on this spring evening, feeling the world slow down, and my thoughts wander freely, like the gentle breeze that rustles the leaves of the trees.
