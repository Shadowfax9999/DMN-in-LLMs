# 7

*seed: time · drift*

---

The warmth of the sun on my skin is a welcome respite from the chill of winter. As I sit here, I notice the gentle rustle of leaves in the breeze outside. It's a sound that's both calming and invigorating, like the promise of new beginnings that comes with every spring.

My mind starts to wander to the memories of springs past. I remember the smell of freshly cut grass, the vibrant colors of blooming flowers, and the sound of children's laughter as they play outside. It's a time of renewal and growth, a time when the world feels full of possibilities.

As I gaze out the window, I notice the way the sunlight filters through the leaves, casting dappled shadows on the ground. It's like nature's own work of art, constantly changing and evolving. I feel a sense of awe at the beauty of the world around me, and a sense of gratitude for this moment of peace.

My thoughts begin to drift to the plans I have for the upcoming weeks. The warmer weather means I can finally start working on my garden, planting new flowers and vegetables, and tending to the ones that have survived the winter. I imagine the satisfaction of watching my hard work come to fruition, of harvesting fresh produce and enjoying the fruits of my labor.

As I sit here, lost in thought, I become aware of the sounds around me. The hum of the refrigerator in the kitchen, the chirping of birds outside, and the distant rumble of traffic all blend together to create a symphony of sounds. It's a reminder that even in the stillness of the moment, there is always movement and life happening around me.

My mind continues to wander, jumping from one thought to the next, like a leaf blowing in the wind. I think about the book I've been meaning to read, the phone call I need to make, and the errands I need to run. But for now, I'm content to simply sit, breathe, and let the warmth of the sun soak into my skin.
