# 15

*seed: time · drift*

---

The warm sunlight streaming through the window is a perfect accompaniment to the gentle hum of the clock on the wall. It's a Monday, and the afternoon is slowly unfolding, carrying with it the promise of a new week full of possibilities. The sound of birds chirping outside creates a soothing melody, a stark contrast to the hustle and bustle of the morning rush.

As I sit here, I start to think about the changing seasons. Spring is a time of renewal, a season of growth and rebirth. The once-dormant trees are now bursting with green leaves, and the flowers are blooming in every color of the rainbow. The air is filled with the sweet scent of blooming lavender and the earthy smell of freshly cut grass.

My mind begins to wander to the plans I have for the upcoming weekend. Maybe a hike in the nearby woods, or a picnic in the park. The thought of spending time outdoors, surrounded by nature, is exhilarating. I imagine the feeling of the warm sun on my skin, the sound of the wind rustling through the trees, and the smell of the blooming wildflowers.

As I continue to daydream, I start to think about the simple pleasures in life. A good cup of coffee on a lazy Sunday morning, a good book on a rainy day, or a walk along the beach at sunset. These are the things that make life worth living, the things that bring us joy and happiness.

The clock on the wall ticks away, a reminder that time is passing, but my mind is still free to wander. I think about the people I've met, the experiences I've had, and the lessons I've learned. Life is a journey, and every moment is an opportunity to grow, to learn, and to explore.

As the afternoon wears on, I start to feel a sense of contentment wash over me. The world outside may be busy and chaotic, but in this moment, everything feels peaceful and serene. The warm sunlight, the gentle breeze, and the sound of birds singing all combine to create a sense of tranquility, a sense of being exactly where I'm meant to be.
