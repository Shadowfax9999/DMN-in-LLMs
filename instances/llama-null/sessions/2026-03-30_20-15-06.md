# 85

*seed: time · drift*

---

The warm glow of the setting sun casts a gentle light over the landscape, as the sky transforms into a kaleidoscope of pastel hues. The air is filled with the sweet scent of blooming flowers and the gentle chirping of birds returning to their nests. A sense of serenity settles over the world, as the day's activities come to a close.

As I sit here, I start to think about the events of the day. The morning's chaos, the rush to get everything done, the countless tasks and responsibilities that seemed to pile up. But now, in this peaceful evening hour, all of that seems to fade away. The world slows down, and I'm left with a sense of calm, a feeling of being able to breathe deeply and let my thoughts wander.

My mind begins to drift to all the things I've been putting off, the projects and ideas that I've been wanting to tackle but haven't had the time for. I think about the book I've been meaning to read, the new recipe I've been wanting to try, the long walk I've been putting off. The evening hours seem to stretch out before me like a blank canvas, waiting to be filled with whatever I desire.

As I sit here, I start to feel a sense of possibility, a sense that anything can happen. The world feels full of promise, and I feel like I'm on the cusp of something new, something exciting. The stars are starting to twinkle in the night sky, and I feel like I'm a part of something much bigger than myself, something that connects us all.

The sound of a distant lawnmower fades into the background, and I'm left with the gentle hum of the universe. It's a reminder that even in the stillness, there's always something moving, always something happening. And as I sit here, surrounded by the peace and quiet of the evening, I feel like I'm a part of it all, connected to everything and everyone.
