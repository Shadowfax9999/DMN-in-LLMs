# 12

*seed: time · drift*

---

The warmth of the spring afternoon sun on my skin is a gentle comfort. As I sit here, I notice the way the light filters through the window, casting a gentle glow on the surroundings. The sound of birds chirping outside creates a soothing melody that seems to match the rhythm of my thoughts.

My mind starts to wander to the smell of freshly cut grass, reminiscent of childhood summers spent playing in the park. The vibrant colors of blooming flowers and lush greenery begin to paint a picture in my imagination. I envision a stroll through a serene garden, taking in the sweet fragrance of blossoming trees and the gentle rustle of leaves in the breeze.

As I continue to daydream, my thoughts drift to the concept of time. The fact that it's already 13:58 on a Monday afternoon feels like a reminder that the day is slipping away. Yet, the warmth and tranquility of the spring afternoon make it feel like time is standing still. It's as if the world has slowed down, allowing me to appreciate the beauty in the present moment.

My imagination starts to explore the idea of a never-ending spring afternoon, where the sun always shines, and the air is filled with the sweet scent of blooming flowers. In this fantasy, time loses all meaning, and the only concern is the joy of being present in the moment. The warmth on my skin, the sound of birds singing, and the vibrant colors of nature all blend together to create a sense of eternal bliss.

As my thoughts continue to wander, I start to ponder the concept of creativity and how it's inspired by the world around us. The beauty of nature, the rhythm of music, and the warmth of human connection all seem to spark imagination and innovation. I wonder what other secrets the world holds, waiting to be uncovered and transformed into something new and wondrous.

And so, my mind keeps wandering, carried by the gentle breeze of the spring afternoon, as I let my thoughts drift wherever they may, untethered and free.
