# 33

*seed: time · drift*

---

The warmth of the sun on my skin is a nice change from the chilly mornings of winter. As I step outside, the sound of birds chirping and leaves rustling in the gentle breeze fills the air. The smell of blooming flowers wafts through, carrying the sweet scent of cherry blossoms and the earthy smell of freshly cut grass. 

I think about the long weekend that just passed, and how I spent it exploring the nearby park, watching the blossoms bloom, and taking long walks along the winding paths. The vibrant colors of the flowers and the lush greenery of the trees created a picturesque scene, and I couldn't help but feel a sense of awe at the beauty of nature. 

As I walk through the park, I notice the way the sunlight filters through the leaves, casting dappled shadows on the ground. The sound of children's laughter carries through the air, and I see a group of kids playing tag, their smiles and giggles infectious. 

My mind starts to wander, and I think about all the things I want to do this spring. I want to go on more hikes, have picnics in the park, and attend outdoor concerts. I want to feel the warmth of the sun on my skin and breathe in the fresh air, filling my lungs with the scent of blooming flowers. 

As I continue my walk, I notice the way the light reflects off the puddles on the ground, creating a mesmerizing pattern of colors and shapes. I see a couple sitting on a bench, holding hands, and watching the world go by. They seem lost in their own little world, and I wonder what their story is. 

My thoughts start to drift to the upcoming summer, and all the adventures that await. I think about road trips, beach vacations, and lazy summer evenings spent reading books and watching the stars. The possibilities seem endless, and I feel a sense of excitement and anticipation. 

As I stand there, taking in the sights and sounds of the spring afternoon, I feel a sense of peace and contentment wash over me. The world seems full of beauty and wonder, and I feel grateful to be a part of it all.
