# 14

*seed: time · drift*

---

The warmth of the spring sun on my skin is a great feeling. I can almost smell the blooming flowers and fresh cut grass. It's amazing how a simple change in seasons can evoke such strong emotions and sensations. The cold winter months are finally behind us, and the world seems to be coming alive again.

I find myself wondering what the rest of the day will bring. The clock reads 13:58, so I've got a couple of minutes before 2 PM. Perhaps I'll take a short walk outside to soak up some of that sunshine. The sound of birds chirping and the gentle breeze rustling through the trees is like music to my ears.

As I stroll, I start thinking about all the things I've been putting off lately. You know, the usual stuff – cleaning out the garage, organizing my desk, and responding to those pending emails. But for now, I'll just let those thoughts drift away, carried by the wind. No need to worry about them on a beautiful spring afternoon like this.

My mind begins to wander to more pleasant topics. I recall a conversation I had with a friend last week about traveling. We were discussing all the amazing places we want to visit someday. The thought of exploring new cities, trying exotic foods, and immersing myself in different cultures is exhilarating. Maybe this summer will be the perfect time to plan a trip.

The sound of children laughing in the distance catches my attention. It's a reminder that spring is a time of renewal and growth, not just for nature, but for people as well. It's a season of new beginnings, a chance to start fresh and pursue our passions.

As the clock strikes 2 PM, I feel a sense of freedom and possibility. The world feels full of promise, and I'm excited to see what the rest of the day, and the rest of the season, will bring.
