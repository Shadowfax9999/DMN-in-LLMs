*seed: time · drift*

---

The warm sunlight streams through the window, casting a gentle glow over the room. I can feel the warmth on my skin, and it's a pleasant sensation, like a soft hug. As I sit here, I start to think about the sounds of the season. Birds chirping, leaves rustling in the breeze, and the distant hum of lawnmowers all blend together to create a symphony of spring.

My mind begins to wander to the outdoors, and I imagine taking a walk through a nearby park. The air is filled with the sweet scent of blooming flowers, and the grass is a vibrant green. I picture children laughing and playing tag, their smiles and giggles carrying on the wind. It's a carefree atmosphere, full of joy and possibility.

As I continue to daydream, I start to think about the smell of freshly cut grass. It's a distinctive aroma that always brings back memories of summertime and outdoor adventures. I remember playing in the sprinklers as a kid, running through the water on a hot summer day, and feeling the cool droplets on my skin.

My thoughts then shift to the changing colors of the seasons. Spring is a time of renewal and growth, as the world awakens from its winter slumber. The once-dormant trees now burst forth with green leaves, and the flowers bloom in every color of the rainbow. It's a beautiful time of year, full of hope and promise.

As I sit here, lost in thought, I start to feel a sense of gratitude for this moment. The warmth of the sun, the sounds of nature, and the vibrant colors of the season all combine to create a sense of peace and contentment. It's a feeling that's hard to put into words, but it's one of those moments where everything feels right with the world.
