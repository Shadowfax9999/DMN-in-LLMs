# 93

*seed: time · drift*

---

The warmth of the spring evening sun still lingers on my skin as I step out into the gentle breeze. The sky is a canvas of soft pinks and blues, a breathtaking sunset that seems to stretch on forever. The air is filled with the sweet scent of blooming flowers and the distant chirping of birds, a soothing melody that calms the mind.

As I take a deep breath, I feel the stress of the day melting away, like the ebbing tide of a peaceful ocean. My thoughts begin to wander, drifting on the wind like a leaf on a lazy stream. I think about the events of the day, the conversations I had, the tasks I completed, and the ones I left undone.

My mind starts to meander through memories, both old and new. I recall the smell of freshly cut grass from my childhood, the taste of warm summer days spent playing in the park, and the sound of laughter shared with loved ones. The memories come flooding back, like a dam breaking, and I'm carried away on a tide of nostalgia.

As the stars begin to twinkle in the evening sky, I find myself pondering the mysteries of the universe. I wonder about the secrets hidden in the cosmos, the unexplored depths of the ocean, and the untold stories of human history. The world is a vast and wondrous place, full of mystery and awe, and I feel grateful to be a part of it, if only for a brief moment in time.

The sound of a distant car driving by breaks the spell, and I'm drawn back to the present. I look around, taking in the sights and sounds of the evening. The world is a complex tapestry, woven from threads of joy and sorrow, beauty and ugliness. And yet, in this moment, all seems right with the world. The stars are shining, the breeze is gentle, and my heart is at peace.
