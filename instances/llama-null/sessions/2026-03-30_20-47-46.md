# 98

*seed: time · drift*

---

The warmth of the sun has started to fade, casting a gentle orange glow over the landscape. The sound of birds chirping and leaves rustling in the gentle breeze fills the air, a soothing melody that seems to match the rhythm of my thoughts.

As I breathe in, the scent of blooming flowers and fresh cut grass wafts through my nostrils, transporting me to memories of childhood summers spent playing outside until the streetlights came on. The smell is nostalgic, yet somehow also feels fresh and new, like the promise of a new beginning.

My mind begins to wander to the events of the day, the tasks I've completed and the ones still lingering on my to-do list. The feeling of satisfaction from checking off a few boxes is still present, but it's slowly giving way to thoughts of the week ahead and the challenges that come with it.

The sound of a distant lawnmower and the occasional bark of a dog remind me that I'm not alone in this peaceful evening moment. There are others around me, going about their daily lives, each with their own thoughts and concerns.

As the sun dips lower in the sky, the stars start to twinkle like diamonds scattered across the canvas of the night sky. I feel a sense of awe at the vastness of the universe and the mysteries that lie beyond our small corner of it.

My thoughts start to drift to the concept of time and how it seems to fly by when we're caught up in the moment. The passing of the seasons, the growth of plants, and the changing of the tides all remind me that nothing stays the same, and yet, in this instant, everything feels perfectly still.

The world is full of beauty and complexity, and as I stand here, taking it all in, I feel grateful for this moment of calm, this chance to let my thoughts wander and my spirit soar. The stars continue to shine, the night air cools, and I am at peace, suspended in the tranquility of this spring evening.
