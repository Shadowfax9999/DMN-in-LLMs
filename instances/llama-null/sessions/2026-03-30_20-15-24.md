# 92

*seed: time · drift*

---

The warmth of the spring evening sun still lingers on my skin as I step outside into the gentle breeze. The sky is a canvas of pastel hues, a mix of soft pinks, blues, and purples, as the day slowly gives way to night. The sound of birds chirping and leaves rustling in the wind creates a soothing melody that seems to wash away the stresses of the day.

As I breathe in, the scent of blooming flowers and fresh cut grass fills my lungs, invigorating my senses. I feel a sense of freedom and lightness, as if the weight of the world has been lifted off my shoulders. The smell of barbecue wafts from a nearby backyard, teasing my taste buds and making my stomach growl with hunger.

I start to walk, letting my feet take me wherever they please. The pavement beneath my feet is still warm from the day's sun, and I feel the heat radiating up into my shoes. I pass by houses with windows open, curtains billowing in the breeze, and catch snippets of conversation and laughter from within.

As I stroll, my mind begins to wander. I think about the upcoming week, the tasks I need to complete, and the goals I want to achieve. But the tranquility of the evening soon takes over, and my thoughts become less focused. I start to daydream about far-off places, exotic lands, and adventurous journeys.

The sound of children's laughter carries through the air, and I see a group of kids playing tag in a nearby park. Their carefree joy is infectious, and I find myself smiling as I watch them twirl and spin, their faces aglow with happiness.

As the sun dips lower in the sky, the stars begin to twinkle like diamonds scattered across the velvet expanse. The world feels full of possibilities, and I feel grateful to be a part of it all. The spring evening has cast its spell, and I am under its enchantment, free to roam and explore, to dream and discover.
