# 17

*seed: time · drift*

---

The warmth of the spring sun on my skin is a welcome feeling after a long winter. As I sit here, I notice the gentle breeze rustling the leaves of the trees outside, creating a soothing melody that seems to match the rhythm of my thoughts.

I start to think about the week ahead, and all the tasks I need to complete. But my mind doesn't want to focus on work just yet. It starts to wander, and I find myself thinking about the smell of freshly cut grass and the sound of children playing in the park nearby.

I imagine taking a walk in the park, feeling the soft grass beneath my feet, and watching the blooming flowers sway in the breeze. The vibrant colors of the flowers – red, yellow, pink, and purple – create a beautiful tapestry that's both calming and uplifting.

As I continue my stroll, I notice the different types of birds singing their songs, each one unique and melodious. The air is filled with the sweet scent of blooming trees, and I feel a sense of peace and tranquility wash over me.

My thoughts start to drift to a memories of spring afternoons from my childhood, when I would spend hours playing outside with friends, exploring the neighborhood, and enjoying the simple joys of life. The feeling of freedom and joy that comes with being young and carefree is something that I still cherish to this day.

As the sun begins to shine brighter, I feel my thoughts becoming more vivid and imaginative. I start to think about all the possibilities that spring has to offer – the opportunity to start anew, to try new things, and to watch the world around me come alive after a long winter.

And as I sit here, basking in the warmth of the spring sun, I feel my thoughts becoming more relaxed and contemplative. The world seems to be moving at a slower pace, and I'm grateful for this moment of peace and tranquility on a busy Monday afternoon.
