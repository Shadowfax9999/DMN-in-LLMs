# 10

*seed: time · drift*

---

The warmth of the spring sun on my skin is a pleasant sensation, like a gentle hug from nature. As I breathe in, the sweet scent of blooming flowers fills my lungs, and I feel invigorated. The sound of birds chirping in the distance creates a soothing melody that seems to match the rhythm of my heartbeat.

I imagine myself strolling through a lush green park, surrounded by vibrant tulips and daffodils swaying gently in the breeze. The soft rustle of leaves and the chirping of birds create a sense of serenity, as if the world has slowed down to appreciate the beauty of this spring afternoon.

As I walk, the warm sunlight casts a golden glow on the scenery, making everything feel fresh and new. I notice the way the light filters through the petals of the flowers, creating intricate patterns and highlighting the delicate textures of the blooms.

My mind begins to wander to the possibilities of this season. The arrival of spring brings with it a sense of renewal and rejuvenation. It's a time for new beginnings, for planting seeds and watching them grow, for exploring the outdoors and reconnecting with nature.

I think about the simple joys of spring: the taste of freshly picked strawberries, the feel of warm rain on my skin, the sound of children laughing as they play in the park. It's a season that reminds me to appreciate the little things, to slow down and savor the beauty that surrounds me.

As I continue my stroll, I notice the way the light changes, casting long shadows across the grass. The air is filled with the sweet scent of blooming trees, and I feel a sense of peace wash over me. It's as if the world has come alive, and I'm grateful to be a part of it, to experience the beauty and wonder of this spring afternoon.
