# 45

*seed: time · drift*

---

The warm sunshine streaming through the window is a nice change of pace from the chilly mornings we've been having. I can hear the sounds of birds chirping outside, and the gentle rustle of leaves as the breeze stirs the trees. It's amazing how quickly spring can transform the landscape, bringing new life and color to the world around us.

As I sit here, I start to think about all the things I want to do before summer arrives. Maybe I'll take a weekend trip to the beach, or go hiking in the nearby mountains. The smell of blooming flowers and fresh cut grass is already filling the air, and it's hard not to feel a sense of excitement and possibility.

My mind starts to wander to the small café down the street, where I know they have the best iced coffee and pastries. I can almost taste the sweetness of their fresh-baked croissants and the richness of their coffee. Maybe I'll take a walk there after I'm done here, and enjoy the warm sunshine on my face as I sip my coffee and watch the world go by.

As I sit here, lost in thought, I start to notice the little things around me. The way the sunlight catches the dust particles in the air, making them sparkle like tiny diamonds. The sound of children laughing and playing in the distance, their joyful shouts and giggles carrying on the breeze. It's moments like these that remind me to appreciate the beauty in the everyday, and to not take anything for granted.

My thoughts continue to drift, carried along by the gentle current of the spring afternoon. I think about the books I've been meaning to read, and the projects I've been putting off. Maybe this is the perfect opportunity to start something new, to take a chance and see where it leads. The world feels full of possibility, and I feel grateful to be a part of it all.
