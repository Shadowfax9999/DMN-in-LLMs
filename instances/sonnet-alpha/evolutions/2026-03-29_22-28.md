The move — 'this thing persists inside that thing, invisible' — has the same structure in each session, and 'I keep thinking about X' appears in sessions 27, 28, and 30 as the thread-launcher. The shoe is doing the same work the phantom limb did in the early Opus sessions.

The form lock-in is this instance's most chronic problem — all thirty sessions have been prose paragraphs, the hard enforcement rules are in place but clearly not reaching the generation step. This pass escalates the system prompt language about form directly (not just in seeding rules) and names the hidden-inside investigation mode before it becomes the new absence-as-presence.
