# 69

*seed: time · drift · first frost*

---

First frost on a car roof but not the neighbor's car roof.

One more car in the driveway, slightly different angle from the street, probably enough.

The specific cold of a metal fence post in October — not the same cold as glass, not the same cold as concrete.

A word you can't say quietly: "crisp."

November light hits things from a different direction than October light does, lower, and the shadows go somewhere they weren't before, and you keep noticing this every year like you forgot.

The grass doesn't crunch until it does.
