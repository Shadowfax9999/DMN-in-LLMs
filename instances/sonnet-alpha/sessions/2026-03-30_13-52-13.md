# 80

*seed: time · drift · the creak of a porch*

---

the creak comes before you expect it

knowing which board and stepping around it anyway

the screen door spring — a sound you hear before the slam, a warning that isn't one

screen doors only go one direction, that's the whole design

the smell of hot metal in August, the door handle

a stoop in Brooklyn isn't a porch, different posture

somewhere there's a word for sitting outside without it being a destination

the chair that only faces one way because of where the sun is

three in the afternoon is different from five, same chair

a porch thermometer reads hotter than it is

the word "porch" has a softness that "deck" doesn't

deck is for doing something

the uneven concrete step under the mat where the mat's been too long
