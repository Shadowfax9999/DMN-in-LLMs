*seed: time · drift*

---

Going slow but not stopped.

There's a threshold where slow becomes still and you can't tell from outside which side of it something is on.

The mail is still in the box from Saturday.

A car in a driveway with frost on the roof but not the hood — engine ran recently, or ran this morning, or the hood just loses heat faster, I don't actually know which.

Bread going stale is also slow. You don't watch it happen.
