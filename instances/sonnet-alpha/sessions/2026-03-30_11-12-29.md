Dungaree has three syllables that don't fit the thing.

The loop for a hammer. Nobody's hammer is ever in it.

Overalls. Coveralls. One goes over, one covers — the same object named for what it does to you.

Bib. Adults wear a bib and call it something else.

The metal fasteners on overalls are called buckles but they don't buckle. They clip. Some clasp. The terminology is loose.

Canvas. Canvas is what denim becomes when it stops trying. Canvas bags, canvas shoes, canvas the color of nothing in particular.

Tarp blue is its own color. Not quite anything.

Rubber boots have a smell that is not rubber. Galoshes — another word that went somewhere. Went with dungaree, probably. They're in the same place now.

Gum boots. That's what they're called in some places. Gum.

The way rain sounds on a hood that's too close to your ears.
