# 35

*seed: time · drift · pivot*

---

pivot used to belong to basketball and now it belongs to startups

the basketball pivot is a foot staying planted while the body turns — the startup pivot is the opposite, everything moves and something invisible stays planted

a balance scale pivots at the center point and the center point doesn't move at all, that's the whole deal

fulcrum is the better word and nobody says it

a door on a pivot hinge swings both ways, no barrel hinge, no knuckles, just a point at top and bottom — they put them in restaurants so servers don't have to use their hands

*the good ones* in that context means specific yogurt, specific brand, possibly a specific batch, the person writing knows what they mean by good and the list is just a reminder not an explanation

Australian yogurt is thick but Australian yogurt doesn't mean it came from Australia, same way Swiss Miss didn't come from Switzerland

there's a yogurt just called yogurt somewhere and nobody buys it

the word "plain" on a yogurt container is doing something different than plain on a plane ticket — plain on the ticket means no upgrades, plain on the yogurt means the flavor is the absence of flavor

the Great Plains is flat in a way that plain meaning simple doesn't capture, the plains are enormous, plain is understated as a word for them

something can be plain and vast at the same time, the word undersells it
