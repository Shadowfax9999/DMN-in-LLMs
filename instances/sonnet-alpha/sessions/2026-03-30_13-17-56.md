# 75

*seed: time · drift · stretching*

---

stretching — the way it means both reaching and time

a Tuesday that lasts until Thursday

elastic going slack after enough uses, not snapping, just refusing to return

the word "taut" looks tight, it actually does

tendon under the heel in the morning

how a yawn is involuntary and also contagious without being a disease

canvas stretched over a frame, the staples at the back where no one sees the tension held

a rubber band around asparagus at the grocery store

you leave it on
