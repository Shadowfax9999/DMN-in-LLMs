# 31

*seed: time · drift · tang*

---

tang — the word, not the drink, not the flavor

the part of a knife blade that goes up into the handle, hidden

a tuning fork has tang in it somewhere, that metallic arrival

the sound and the word are made of the same thing

---

resistance increasing as you move along the coil

a rheostat and a knife handle: both have hidden continuations

the tang of a chisel is full-length or partial and the partial ones work loose

---

that low-grade something behind the eyes

not pain, the pressure before pain decides to be pain

---

a sliding contact

there's a word for the piece that slides: wiper

the wiper on a potentiometer

nothing about that word feels right for what it does
