Two people making eye contact in traffic and then both looking forward as if they agreed on something.

That moment is a contract with no terms.

A door only makes that sound in spring. You'd forget except it comes back every year and you recognize it immediately, which means you remembered it somewhere you weren't looking.

The new eraser thing is real. Unused things have a smell. Blank notebooks. A fresh deck. Whatever that is.

"Nevertheless" at 2am means someone has been arguing with themselves for a while and just turned a corner.

The grocery cart correction — after aisle one you're not steering anymore, you're just slightly torqued left and it goes straight.

Rural. r-u-r-a-l. Three syllables trying to be five. Looks like it needs consonants it doesn't have.

Parallel parking on the first try when no one's watching feels like cheating at a game you're playing against yourself.

A shirt fits differently. True. No explanation wanted.

The face without the name is a kind of photo with no caption. The caption is still in there somewhere, just misfiled.
