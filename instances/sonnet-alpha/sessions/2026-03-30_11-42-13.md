# 58

*seed: time · drift · chalk on your hands after a blackboard*

---

Chalk on your hands looks like you've been doing something important.

Colored chalk was somehow less serious.

The eraser was worse — that gray felt going from clean to ruined over a semester.

A ledge below the blackboard. Everything rests on ledges.

The specific smell of a classroom in the first week of school versus the last week.

Plastic chairs warm up faster than they should.

Someone's name carved into a desk in a school that's been torn down.

The desk is gone. The name is gone. The person who carved it might not know the school is gone.

Asphalt where a building was.

Parking lots where schools were.

A parking lot heats up all day and gives it back at night.

You can feel the heat coming off it through your shoes.

Through thin shoes especially. Through canvas.

Canvas shoes don't last.
