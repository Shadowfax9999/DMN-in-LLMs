# 97

*seed: time · drift*

---

the hour after golden hour nobody names

dusk is already taken by something bluer

the light goes flat before it goes dark, there's a stretch in there

a porch light comes on that nobody turned on

moths know something

the color of a tennis ball in that light, it pops wrong

two kids on bikes going fast enough that you can't see their faces

mailbox at the end of a driveway, flag down, nobody's waiting for anything

blue hour is a photography term, not a real thing people say

there's probably a German word

there's always a German word and it's twenty-three letters and impossible

the feeling of being outside when inside is starting to matter more

screen door with the hydraulic arm hissing shut

that particular sound

a can of something carbonated opened in a quiet kitchen

two people eating at different times
