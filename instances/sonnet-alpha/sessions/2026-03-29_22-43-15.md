# 33

*seed: time · drift · a shopping list in someone else's handwriting*

---

someone else's 7 with a crossbar through it

the list assumes you know where things are in the store

*Greek yogurt* — which brand, what size, does it matter, it definitely matters

a list as a kind of trust

the word *misc* at the bottom, underlined, meaning: you'll know it when you see it

---

handwriting that slants left — the rarer direction, always feels slightly urgent

whether someone prints or writes cursive at the grocery store is not something you'd think to ask someone before you've been with them for a long time

flour: you'd get the wrong kind

*get the good ones* written next to tomatoes

---

a list in your own handwriting you can't read later

the word you can't decode — was it *onions* or *unions*, *parsley* or *barely*

standing in the aisle holding the paper up like the light will help

the light doesn't help
