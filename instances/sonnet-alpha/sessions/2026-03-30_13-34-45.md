the word looks like it should crackle

a wool sweater pulled over a dry head

the feeling right before a shock, the doorknob, the half-second where you already know

the opposite of dynamic

the opposite of dynamic is also: stable, motionless, fixed, at rest — none of which sound like static sounds

a joint that doesn't move anymore is called that in medicine

electricity that doesn't go anywhere, just sits on the surface waiting
