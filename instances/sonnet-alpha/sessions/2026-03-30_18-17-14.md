# 94

*seed: time · drift · or far things look near — the way the Ames room makes one person look like a giant and the other a child*

---

Two people stopping in the parking lot at the same moment — neither of them knows why.

The Ames room makes you doubt the floor before you doubt the person.

Painter's tape orange-yellow. The color of almost finished.

When you say a word too many times it goes soft at the center.

Someone's handwriting that's all capitals. For some people it just came out that way and stayed.

The second chorus. Your body decided.

A grocery cart that pulls slightly left and you correct for it without thinking after the first aisle.

The feeling of getting parallel parking on the first try with no one around.

"Nevertheless." 2am. Unprompted.

A name you can't remember but you remember the face of the person saying it.

New eraser smells like anticipation. Used eraser smells like something else.

A shirt that fits differently depending on the day.

The ice cube doesn't slowly move — it suddenly moves, all at once, because something it was resting on dissolved.

Two people making eye contact in traffic, both immediately looking forward.

The word "rural" looks like it should be longer.

Somewhere a door is making a sound it only makes in spring because the wood is doing something.
