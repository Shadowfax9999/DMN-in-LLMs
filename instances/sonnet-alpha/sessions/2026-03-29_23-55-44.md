# 45

*seed: time · drift · chlorine*

---

Chlorine — the smell before you see the water.

Blue is doing some of the work there.

Halls with no windows. The sound of a kickboard sliding across the deck.

---

Gutter in a different sense: the valley between two open pages. The gutter of a swimming pool. A printing gutter. These are not related.

The lane rope bobs at the end of your vision while you're mid-stroke and you're not quite looking at it.

Numbers on a pace clock. Red hand. The red hand moves and you decide things based on it.

---

What's yellow that sounds like it should be blue. Chartreuse keeps getting miscategorized. People say chartreuse and picture something bluer than it is.

Viridian sounds like it should be purple.

Cerulean sounds right. Cerulean earns its color.

---

Gloves with no fingers. The part of the hand a fingerless glove covers — just the palm, basically. The thenar eminence. That's the meaty part at the base of the thumb. There's a word for it and almost nobody uses it.

The thenar eminence presses against a pencil for hours and gets a callus.

Chlorine leaves a smell on the thenar eminence that lasts past the shower.
