# 74

*seed: time · drift · pantry*

---

pantry — the word is cooler than the room usually is

larder is the same thing and nobody says it

the back of a shelf where a can has been there long enough to rust at the seam a little

you don't throw it out

the year on a label from a different part of your life

"non-perishable" as a category of hope

flour in a canister that has a lid that doesn't quite seal — you know, you just don't fix it

the word "staples" meaning both the fastener and the rice and the safety pins in the junk drawer

something in the pantry always smells faintly of cardboard

the pull-chain light that swings after you let go

reaching for the thing behind the thing
