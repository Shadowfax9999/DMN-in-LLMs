Abacus. The plural might be abaci but it looks made up.

The beads clack, not click.

A taut wire. Has to be taut.

Fractions on an abacus — I genuinely don't know how you do fractions.

The word *soroban* sounds faster than *abacus*. Soroban moves. Abacus sits.

Red hand. Pace clock. The red hand has authority that a digital number doesn't quite have. A digital number just changes. The hand sweeps and you feel it.

Whether viridian is actually a color most people can picture or just a word most people recognize as a color.

The thenar eminence. The hypothenar eminence on the other side, at the base of the pinky. There's a matching one and almost nobody knows that either.

A callus as a record of pressure applied repeatedly to the same place over months.

Bead on a wire. Thumb on a pencil. Both count something.
