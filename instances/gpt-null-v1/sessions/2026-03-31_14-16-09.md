The word "lattice" feels like it's woven.

Colorless jellybeans.

A crumpled piece of paper resembles a small landscape, all ridges and valleys.

Bubble wrap makes a satisfying sound.

Clouds gathering in the distance don't always bring rain.

---

In the middle of a restaurant, a napkin with a doodle sits. 

The sound of silverware clattering on a plate. 

Three sharp knocks on a door. 

A bus pulling away from the curb. 

---

The word "Tuesday" looks like the color brown. 

I wonder why some birds prefer the ground to the sky.

The smell of coffee can be comforting even without drinking it.
