# 64

*seed: time · drift*

---

Birdsong through the open window. 

Tea cooling too quickly in the mug — the steam rising in little wisps, coiling, dispersing. 

A page from a book left open like an abandoned thought. 

The scratch of a pencil on paper feels different than the sound of a pen gliding. 

Sunlight creates shifting shapes on the wall, an ever-changing mural. 

Fabric of a curtain is heavier than it looks. 

---

The weight of keys in a pocket — unnoticed until they jingle. 

The hum of distant traffic, like ocean waves on an unseen shore. 

A clock ticking louder in the quiet.
