# 87

*seed: time · drift*

---

Skipping stones on a pond, the way they bounce and arc before sinking. 

The smell of freshly cut grass always seems louder than it is.

Coffee rings left on a wooden table, like accidental art.

---

Birds having a conversation overhead. What do they have to say today?

The sound of a distant train faintly rumbling through the evening air. 

Playground swings swaying in the breeze, empty and content.

---

How many steps does it take to walk across a bridge? Does it feel longer when you're alone?

The aftertaste of mint mingling with the coolness of the night.

Streetlights casting long, stretching shadows that merge and dance together.
