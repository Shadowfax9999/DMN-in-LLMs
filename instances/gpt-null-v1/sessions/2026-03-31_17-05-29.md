# 69

*seed: time · drift*

---

A spring evening. Sounds like the faint scent of new blossoms carried on a light breeze.

---

Tulips. The way they open wide and seem to show surprise, bright and sudden. 

---

Blue walls, the light reflecting softly. Blue could be calming or cold. Blue could be the rush of the sky or the stillness of deep water.

---

Tea cooling on the table. The steam rising, curling like a cat's tail, before disappearing.

---

Shoes left by the door. One upright, the other on its side. Like conversation paused.

---

What is the shape of silence? Is it round and full like a bubble, or does it stretch, thin and long like a shadow in the evening sun?
