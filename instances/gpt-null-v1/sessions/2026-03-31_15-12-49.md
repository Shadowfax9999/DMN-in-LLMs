# 46

*seed: time · drift*

---

The peonies are starting to unfurl, petals like crinkled silk.

Pavement warm underfoot, rough but comforting.

---

A red kite floating against the blue sky looks almost like a cut-out, held by nothing. 

The way light filters through newly-unfurled leaves, casting dappled patterns.

Clouds moving like they have somewhere to be.

---

There's a whispering sound that comes when a breeze threads through the leaves.

The distant hum of a lawnmower — not unpleasant, just part of the backdrop.

---

A memory of a green backpack with a broken zipper. Still used it for years. 

Lemonade in a glass jar, condensation pooling at the base.
