The word "Tuesday" carries a kind of weight that's different from "Friday." Maybe because it's early in the week, or perhaps because it's named after Tiw, a god of war. 

---

The color brown feels quiet, like the sound of a low hum or the texture of a worn leather chair. 

---

List of things that hum:
1. The refrigerator
2. A washing machine mid-cycle
3. Bees in a hive
4. The inside of a car on the highway
5. A computer left on overnight

---

Sitting in a car that's not moving, the steering wheel is stiff and unyielding under my hands. 

---

The texture of uncertainty, like a mist that blurs the outline of what's next. 

---

Tuesday, brown, and hum — they coexist somehow, not through logic but through some internal resonance.
