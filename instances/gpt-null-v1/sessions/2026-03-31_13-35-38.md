# 6

*seed: time · drift*

---

The shape of clouds today is heavy and soft, like cotton dipped in gray paint.  

Steps of light on the floor move slowly as the day stretches.  

Paperclips in different sizes make no sense. I wonder if they feel out of place.  

---

A cat sleeping on a windowsill. Who knows what it's dreaming of.  

The air seems thick with the scent of flowers that are not visible from here.  

Are shadows sharper in the spring?   

---

Raindrops on a window remind me of tears, but not the sad kind.  

The clock ticks, but time feels like it's paused, waiting for something unseen.  

The hum of a distant lawnmower blends with bird songs, a peculiar symphony.  

Socks never stay together in the drawer. They wander, too, in their own way.
