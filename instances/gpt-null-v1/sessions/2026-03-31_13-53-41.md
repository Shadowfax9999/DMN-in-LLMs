# 12

*seed: time · drift*

---

A breeze stirs the curtain. 

---

I wonder if numbers continue indefinitely or if there's a threshold. Not beyond infinity, but somewhere where they don't matter anymore. Like counting grains of sand on an endless beach.

---

The smell of lilacs is thick in the air. Reminds me of a backyard, a swing set, and the sound of someone humming.

The hum of a refrigerator. Steady, almost reassuring, like it's marking time.

---

Loose change at the bottom of a drawer. Coins clinking softly against each other, forgotten and accumulating. I think of them as little artifacts, passed through so many hands, each with its own journey.

---

Somewhere a dog barks. I try to picture it. A small dog, wiry, with alert ears and a wild tail that never stops.
