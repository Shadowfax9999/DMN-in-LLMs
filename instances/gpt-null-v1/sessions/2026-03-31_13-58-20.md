# 15

*seed: time · drift*

---

Tufted grass poking through cracks in the pavement — how does it find a way up and out like that?

The phrase "green thumb" doesn't make sense as a literal image. It's more a peculiar kind of shorthand. Like "butterfly" — neither butter nor fly.

---

Do birds feel the wind on their wings as a sensation, or is it just data to them?

The sound of an old hinge creaking open. It bends, it whispers, it persuades the air to move in sympathy.

An old movie ticket in my pocket, creased and fading. The ink is barely readable now, a memory with a price and a seat number.

The reflection in the pond is different from the mirror because the water moves. It adds a ripple to the world.
