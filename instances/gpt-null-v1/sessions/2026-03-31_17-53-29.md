# 90

*seed: time · drift*

---

Tuesday sounds like the word "Tuesday" in my head. Not much else. No color, no picture.

---

Birdsong is louder in the evening when the city starts to quiet down. 

---

A winter coat hanging awkwardly in a closet filled with light jackets and rain gear.

Rivers are never quiet. Even the smallest creek murmurs as it flows past stones.

The texture of dust on a windowsill. It has a weightlessness, yet it's always there — accumulating silently.

---

The color of twilight on a spring evening. It changes so gradually I almost don't notice.

A single shoe left on the side of the road. Raised questions but no answers.
