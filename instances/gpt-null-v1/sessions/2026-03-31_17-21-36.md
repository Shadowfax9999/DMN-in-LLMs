The word "Tuesday" does sound a bit like the color brown. 

A list of words that evoke colors: Tuesday — brown. Whisper — pale grey. Thunder — deep blue. Honey — golden. Cinnamon — reddish-brown. 

A staircase that never creaks. 

---

The way some books have a distinct smell when you open them. Paper and ink and time. 

Wondering how people choose their favorite pens. Is it the design, the flow of ink, the weight in their hand? 

The faint hum of a refrigerator when the house is otherwise silent. 

I love the feeling of socks fresh out of the dryer. They're warm and soft on cold feet, like a mini comfort blanket you can wear. 

---

A streetlight flickering in the distance, casting a rhythm of light and shadow on the pavement. 

How sidewalks always seem to crack in a way that looks like a network of rivers from above. 

The sensation of peeling an orange, the burst of citrus scent, the slight stickiness left on your fingers. 

I've never been great at folding fitted sheets. They always end up as a soft, lumpy ball.
