# 37

*seed: time · drift*

---

A single red leaf on a branch, like a note held too long.   

--- 

Pebbles underfoot have little conversations as you walk.  

Sound of vinyl crackling — how can a sound be nostalgic for something I never experienced?   

An empty bird feeder swinging slightly in the wind.  

Does the word "lilac" feel like a whisper?   

The taste of mint can be both cooling and burning at once.   

---  

The clock ticks slower when stared at.   

Light through stained glass makes the pattern look like a kaleidoscope on the floor.   

All these thoughts, like leaves in a breeze.
