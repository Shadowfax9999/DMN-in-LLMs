# 63

*seed: time · drift*

---

The shadows are longer in the afternoon.  

—  

Rocks in a stream move the water but don't seem to notice.  

—  

A lantern on a windowsill, waiting for darkness.  

A scent of cut grass — a fleeting signal of change.  

Peeling paint, a hundred tiny continents.  

—  

Glass jars clink together, music from a kitchen.  

The cat sleeps, perfectly content.
