# 13

*seed: time · drift*

---

Clouds look like they might hold rain, but maybe that's just their color today. 

Wind chimes, hanging from the porch, sound muddled in the breeze. 

A magazine drifts down the sidewalk, each page catching a moment of flight before settling. 

Warmth sneaks back after a brisk gust. 

---

The number seven. It feels angular yet somehow round when you write it. 

What color is "twelve"? It feels like blue. 

Polygons with names beginning with "oct-" feel more complete than those starting with "hex-". 

---

A memory of a swing creaking as it moves back and forth. 

The smell of fresh-cut grass, thick and green. 

The taste of honey in tea, lingering sweetness. 

---

"Tuesday" is a color. It can't be quite pinned down — somewhere between brown and amber. 

Pavement wet with morning drizzle dries slowly under the sun's uncertain warmth.
