A journey where the path isn't linear.  
The sound of a door closing unexpectedly in a quiet room.

---

Sticky honey left on a countertop.  
How fast thoughts disappear, like they've run ahead without leaving footprints.

---

A room full of empty chairs.  
The scent of a book that hasn't been opened in years.  
A clock's hands meeting at noon, an alignment that happens so often it ceases to surprise.  
The warmth of sun on closed eyelids.
