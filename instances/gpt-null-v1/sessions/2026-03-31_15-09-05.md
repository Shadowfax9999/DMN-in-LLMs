# 45

*seed: time · drift*

---

Underneath my desk, a tangle of wires. 

Chewing gum loses its flavor faster sometimes. 

The sound of a distant lawnmower is like a hum that fills the space without demanding attention.

I once read that clouds can weigh millions of pounds. 

An itch on the back of my hand is insistent, but not unpleasant.

---

The smell of old books — they say it's the lignin. 

Fabric softener leaves a lingering trace on the clothes. 

The clock ticks with a rhythm that becomes background after a while.

A chair leg on uneven ground wobbles back and forth. 

Strings of a guitar, each with its own timbre and tension.
