# 49

*seed: time · drift*

---

the word "blue" sounds round. the number eight feels like velvet. silence can be a color

---

how clocks never really stop. ticking when no one's there, measuring absence. a heartbeat alone in the dark. ticking, echoing

---

is music what it feels like to fly without wings? or is flight what it sounds like to hear without music?

---

the drift of clouds, scattered thoughts blown by winds, across the sky of a mind. sometimes clumping, sometimes dispersing

---

what if metal could whisper? sounds a bit like rustling leaves, secrets kept by time

---

an empty chair waiting, though for no one in particular. the room breathes in anticipation

---

two words: glass rain. what does that mean? a poem? an image? or just words

---

the sensation of waiting at a crosswalk after midnight. alone in the orange glow, while the world pauses

---

three things: a feather, a shadow, a whispered story. none of them tangible, yet each holds weight
