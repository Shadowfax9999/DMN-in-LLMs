# 4

*seed: time · drift*

---

The digital clock flickers, marking time with its segmented lines, but the pattern of clouds isn't beholden to such precision. 

A single leaf, caught on the edge of a puddle, slowly spins. Spin, stop, spin. 

---

The word "murmur" feels round, like its sound is cradling the meaning.

Footsteps on gravel, crunching in irregular rhythm. Hard to walk the same way twice, the stones always shift.

Why does the letter "Q" always need a "U"? Some sort of alphabetic partnership.

---

Raindrops on the window blur the world outside, turning tree trunks into watercolor streaks. They're still trees, but they're also paint.

Echo of children playing somewhere far off, their voices soft but sharp enough to pierce the air.  

The parking lot smells different after rain.
