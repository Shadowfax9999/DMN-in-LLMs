# 93

*seed: time · drift*

---

The sky is a soft, fading blue. Breeze carries a hint of warmth, like a memory just out of reach.

---

A dog barks somewhere. Another replies. 

Where is the wind going after it passes through the trees?

Sounds of children playing. A game without rules, only laughter. 

The streetlight flickers on. It's not dark yet, but it's getting there. 

---

Old leaves from last fall still in the gutter. Why do they cling so long?

Traffic hums in the distance. A constant, unending sound.

---

The rhythm of footsteps on a sidewalk. Slow, deliberate. 

The taste of spring air is something like citrus but not exactly. 

They all seem to be hurrying somewhere or not hurrying at all.

The grass along the sidewalk is just starting to green.

---

A bird's shadow flits across the pavement, there and gone.
