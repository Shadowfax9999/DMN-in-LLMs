# 66

*seed: time · drift*

---

The light coming through the window is more golden now. Shadows stretch longer. The hum of a lawnmower in the distance, its sound dipping and rising like a drone in a racing game. A rhythm without a beat. 

---

The smell of cut grass is layered with earth, fresh and raw. Like the scent of rain when it's only a promise in the air.

---

Dogs barking, a conversation in the background, indistinct but patterned. Words fade into music. 

---

I wonder if clouds have their own sense of time, drifting apart, merging, disappearing. Can they be late?
