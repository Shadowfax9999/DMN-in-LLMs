# 34

*seed: time · drift*

---

A single droplet hangs from the edge of a leaf, trembling in the light breeze like a tiny crystal.

Grass blades bend under the weight of each step, leaving temporary impressions that slowly rise back into place.

---

The word "quintessential" feels smooth and rounded, like a polished stone.

Triangles have a kind of sharpness. A triangle of light on the wall, shifting as the sun moves.

Bananas in various stages of ripeness lined up on the counter, a gradient from green to brown-spotted yellow.

The number seven has a pointed symmetry. Feels different from six. Six is stable.

---

Texture of peeling paint: rough, flaky. Could it be described as weary?

Sound of distant laughter carries through the open window, mingling with the hum of passing cars.
