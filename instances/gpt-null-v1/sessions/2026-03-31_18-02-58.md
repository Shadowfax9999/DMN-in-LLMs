*seed: time · drift*

Wind gently nudges the leaves. 

---

A dog barks in the distance. Its echo seems to linger longer when the air is cool.

---

Shoelaces tied tighter than necessary. A loop crossed over, and under, and pulled through. 

Which is the most common knot in the world? 

---

My thumb taps the rhythm of a song whose lyrics are long forgotten. The melody remains, though. 

---

Raindrops from earlier are still holding onto the car windows.

---

The horizon has a smudge of orange, just before it fades to deeper blues.
