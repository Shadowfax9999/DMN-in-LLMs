Tuesday always feels like it's waiting for something. Could a word have a color? I remember when I used to think of "Tuesday" as brown, but now it feels more like an overcast gray.

A spring evening, when the air starts to soften. Daffodils pushing through the soil, determined and yellow. The smell of rain on the pavement, somewhere between metallic and fresh.

---

Steps on gravel: sharp, uneven, a rhythm that doesn't want to settle. Each step a percussion note amidst the chorus of dusk. It's like walking through a song composed by the earth itself.
