*seed: time · drift*

---

Tuesday. Sounds like a flute. Light, carrying, slightly hollow. 

An empty cup, upside down, sitting on the counter. Trapped air smells slightly different.

7. 14. 21. Numbers like stepping stones. One at a time or all in a leap.

---

A cat's tail curves like a question mark, but holds no question.

Grass pushes up between the sidewalk cracks, unaware of the constraint.

---

What if the word "spring" didn't mean a season or a coil? Just an impulse, a thing that happens.

A single crayon broken in half still colors the same.

Leaves on the breeze whisper secrets that no one can quite catch.

The radio crackles with static as if it's straining to find its own tune.
