# 16

*seed: time · drift*

---

The texture of a wool sweater against skin. Cozy but sometimes itchy.  

Cracks in the pavement, where tiny plants push through. Determined.  

---

The scent of lilacs. Purple has a smell. Or is it that the smell creates purple?  

Why do I always think of rain when I smell wet earth, even if it's sunny?  

The minutes after waking up — a transition that feels elongated.  

Whistling tea kettle, the tone rising sharply, like a call to attention.  

Running a fingertip along the edge of a book, feeling the pages shift slightly.  

---

The way sunlight slants through blinds, stripes on the floor.  

What does a whisper feel like against the ear?  

A memory is like a pebble in the shoe sometimes.  

Dew on grass, each droplet a miniature lens.
