# 94

*seed: time · drift*

---

A discarded feather curls on the sidewalk.

---

A bowl with a chip in the rim. A chair that's missing a screw. The sound of a faucet dripping in a quiet kitchen.

Everything reminds me of something unmentioned.

---

Is it Tuesday if it feels like Wednesday?

The word "sycamore" feels wide and slow. 

---

Words that end in "less." Countless. Restless. Harmless. Boundary-less. 

A fading road sign.

The taste of a half-remembered song.
