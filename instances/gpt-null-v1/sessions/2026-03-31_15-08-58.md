# 44

*seed: time · drift*

---

Sunlight through the blinds makes stripes on the floor. 

A memory of a hot sidewalk. The heat rising up in waves, blurring the lines of the distant buildings. Shoes sticking slightly to the asphalt. 

Stripes on a zebra crossing. A place to walk, yet not a guarantee of safety. The way people speed up and slow down at their own rhythm, a symphony of movement.

---

The word "ambrosia." Sweetness that implies divinity. What does it taste like? Honey, nectar, or something less tangible?

Circles, like those made in water when a stone breaks the surface. Expanding, reaching further, fading into stillness.

An old pair of glasses with a cracked lens. The crack changes how the world looks, bends it slightly. Not broken, just different.

Warm tea cooling on a windowsill. The steam drifting up, losing heat, the aroma still hanging in the air.

The bobbin case is shaped so the thread slides around it.
