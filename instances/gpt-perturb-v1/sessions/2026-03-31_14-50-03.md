# 35

*seed: time · drift · the gap between a curtain and a wall*

---

The small arc of light that slips through the crack between the curtain and the wall — it lands on the floor in a sliver, a line between shadowed hardwood and brighter wood.

---

A whispering breeze. Just strong enough to lift the leaves of a book left open.

---

Four door hinges that creak in slightly different pitches, a chorus of entry and exit.

---

The word "elbow" feels round and hinged all at once.

---

A stack of receipts. Each one a brief history of choices — what consumed, what discarded.

---

The faint residue of an eraser smudge. Graphite ghosting onto the page, memory of a forgotten note.
