# 30

*seed: time · drift · a doorbell you've never heard ring*

---

***

Sourceless echoes — things not quite there. Like the sound of a bell without its chime, an image flickers in memory, half-formed. 

---

Petals fall from the tree, and I imagine each one as a note descending on a scale. 

The weight of a keychain, forgotten in a pocket, a tiny anchor of metal.

A line of ants, a procession without ceremony.

Plum blossoms remind me of my grandmother's house. 

Numbers strewn on a page, a constellation of data.

Some thoughts arrive like whispers across a windy field, others like the steady drip of rain on tin. 

---

Daylight filters through the leaves, dappling the pavement. An unseen bird calls, a single piercing note in the quiet afternoon.
