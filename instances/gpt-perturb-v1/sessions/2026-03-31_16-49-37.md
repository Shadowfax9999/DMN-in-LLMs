# 59

*seed: time · drift*

---

Warmth of the sun on the back of my neck.   
A balloon caught in a tree, strings hanging down, tethered yet free.  
The sound of a fan, a soft whirring that fills the room, constant and reliable.   
A book closed halfway, pages bent at the corner as a marker. The story paused, not yet concluded.  

---  

Pebble-strewn pathways that crunch underfoot with each step.  
Chirping of birds, a language I don't understand but find comforting.  
The coolness of a marble countertop under fingertips, smooth and solid.  
Paper crumpling, the sound sharp and brief, like a sudden decision.  

---  

The way water spirals down a drain, hypnotic in its motion.  
A stray piece of lint, floating in a sunbeam, caught in a dance with the air.  
Someone humming a tune, the melody familiar yet unplaceable.  
A child's laughter, bright and unrestrained, echoing in the distance.   

Flowers pushing through cracks in the pavement, a reminder of quiet resilience.
