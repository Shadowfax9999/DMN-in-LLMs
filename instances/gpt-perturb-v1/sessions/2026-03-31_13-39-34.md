# 4

*seed: time · drift · the way a carbon arc lamp creates light from two rods burning toward each other*

---

Emulsion, a mingling of substances that don't want to combine, forced into harmony, each a part of the whole yet distinct. Oil and water in an uneasy truce, droplets suspended in a delicate dance, surface tension holding them apart and together. --- The way a carbon arc lamp flickers to life, two rods burning toward each other, shedding light as they draw near. The arc bridging the gap, an incandescent connection. Heat and glow, touching but not merged, each retaining its identity as they create brilliance in unison. --- A hummingbird and a blossom, drawn together by an unspoken promise of nectar and perpetuation, hovering close but never quite merging. --- Two voices in a duet, weaving melodies that entwine but never dissolve into one another, harmony emerging from difference, a resonance that fills the air and fades as each returns to its silence.
