*seed: time · drift · compost*

Compost smells a certain way. Earthy, alive, turning rot into life. It's alive, yet it’s hidden work, quiet transformation. A congregation of worms, microbes, fungi all bustling without fanfare. Decomposition that nurtures the ground.

---

Unearthed potatoes, still clinging to traces of dirt. A harvest of roots and tubers. Earth gives up its secrets in small, round bodies.

---

Decay brings growth. A paradox that sits in the garden unnoticed. Brown leaves feeding green shoots. 

Wet coffee grounds in the mix. Eggshells creating a mosaic on soil.

---

Compost heap, steaming in cold air. Heat from decay. Transformation manifest.
