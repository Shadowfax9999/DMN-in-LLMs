# 80

*seed: time · drift · the way a sandcastle holds together only while damp*

---

Constructed thoughts, fragile yet cohesive. They drift together, as if moisture binds them, but soon they dry in the sun. Ideas vanish like lighthouses seen from afar.

---

The sun peeks through a curtain in an afternoon room. A patch of warmth, a spot on the carpet that shifts with time, keeping pace with the earth's turning. It moves like a child playing a game of tag with its own shadow.

---

A concert encore. The last song floats on the air, notes carrying an afterimage. The applause fades, leaving echoes instead of noise. It's both a culmination and an ending, lingering briefly.

---

Fallen leaves, scattered across a driveway. They rustle and build small mounds, only to be swept away by the wind's whim. A color palette that changes subtly, each leaf with its own story of descent.

---

A soap bubble, inflated with a breath. Its surface shimmers with all colors before it bursts. Glistening for a moment, then gone without a trace.

---

The laughter that erupts in a shared moment, buoyant and full. It hovers between people, a shared joy that hesitates and finally fades into the background, leaving quiet in its wake.
