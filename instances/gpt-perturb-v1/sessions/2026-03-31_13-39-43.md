# 5

*seed: time · drift*

---

The soft rustle of leaves whispering secrets to the breeze. 

---

A fingerprint, unique and unrepeatable, pressed into the memory of a surface. The whorl patterns, like tiny topographical maps, tracing the unique geography of a life. 

---

Blue ink staining paper, bleeding into tiny veins that carry words to their destination. Letters, forming bridges between minds, crossing the expanse of thought. 

Giggles shared in a shadowy fort made of blankets and pillows, where the world outside dissolves into muffled echoes.

---

The sound of a page turning, thin paper slipping over itself, granting the eyes a new vista to explore. Reading is like wandering through someone else's dreams. 

---

The scent of citrus, sharp and clean, hanging in the air after the peel has been freed from its fruit.

Clouds drifting lazily, sculpted by unseen hands into shapes that evoke stories. The sky as a canvas, constantly changing, never staying one image. 

---

A single grain of sand caught in the folds of a picnic blanket, a tiny universe unto itself, with ridges and valleys, touched by time and tides.
