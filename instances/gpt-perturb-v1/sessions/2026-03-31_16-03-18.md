*seed: time · drift · canopy*

Canopy makes me think of trees shading a path, casting dappled light. The play of sun and shadow.---

Giraffes grazing among acacia trees in a distant landscape where the horizon stretches for days.

Soft rustling sounds, like whispers between leaves.---

Canvas tents in a campsite, flapping gently in the breeze. A makeshift roof, barely covering the wandering ambitions of those beneath.

A cathedral ceiling, arching high, a man-made forest of stone and light.
