# 31

*seed: time · drift · a missing poster that's been up too long*

---

— fade of ink, blurred by rain. A message once urgent, now part of the landscape. Once there was a time when missing posters were a rare thing. Now they blend into the urban scenery, much like fire hydrants or telephone poles.

List of things that fade:
1. Chalk on a sidewalk
2. Memory of a fleeting scent
3. Old photographs on a sunlit windowsill
4. A song that played often, once
5. The feeling of joy from an unexpected compliment

The warmth of a late afternoon sun lingers on skin, even when standing in shadow. Shoes tapping on cobblestones, the rhythm of a walk recorded in time.

A single tap of a piano key, not quite in tune.
