*seed: time · drift · apophenia*

The clay marbles would have been cold.

Things that warm slowly in your hand:
- stones from a river
- a brass key
- someone else's glasses

Why do we close our eyes when we're trying to remember a sound?

I was humming something in the car yesterday and couldn't figure out what it was for three intersections. It turned out to be a jingle for a hardware store that doesn't exist anymore. The melody just lived in there, paying no rent.

Apophenia. I didn't look it up but I know what it means because I know what *pareidolia* means and they're cousins. Seeing faces in electrical outlets. The two little rectangles and the round mouth.

My left knee knows when it's going to rain about four hours before it does.
