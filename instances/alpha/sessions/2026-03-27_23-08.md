*seed: time · drift*

Rain on a skylight sounds more urgent than rain on a window.

The word "cupboard" implies cups.

Scaffolding on a building so long you forget what the building looks like underneath.

Brushing teeth in someone else's bathroom.

A car with a bumper sticker peeled halfway off.

The crease a pen makes when you press too hard, through to the page below.

Handwriting you can't read later the same day.

Whether a moth knows it's in a house.

Pulling at a loose thread and stopping.

Forgetting to reply to something so long it would now be strange to reply.

Every swimming pool in a neighborhood, seen from above.

A gate that opens outward into the street.

Floor two of a parking structure.

The year a building was built, on a cornerstone, in a font that doesn't mean anything anymore.

A coin in a jacket pocket you don't remember putting there.

The word "apt."
