Folding a fitted sheet. Not well. Nobody folds a fitted sheet well, they just commit to a method at some point and stop adjusting. The elastic corners don't want to be flat and you can either fight that or just make a rectangle that's thicker in the middle. I make a rectangle that's thicker in the middle.

The dryer is still running with the other load. That particular sound where something with a zipper is hitting the drum every rotation. Tick. Tick. Tick. Tick. Not quite regular because the thing shifts a little each time around. If you listened long enough you could probably figure out the circumference of the drum from the interval but I'm not going to do that. The sheet smells like the dryer sheet which smells like what someone in a lab decided "clean" smells like and now it does, it just does, that's what clean smells like to me, that specific synthetic blue smell that isn't blue but is.

There's a cardinal outside the laundry room window. Red on the telephone wire against grey sky. It's the male that's red. The female is — I actually don't know what color. Brownish? I've seen them but I can't pull the image up. Some birds the female is just a muted version of the male and some the female looks like a completely different species. I can picture a male cardinal instantly, the crest and everything, but the female is just a blank shape.

Pillowcases are easy to fold. Satisfying. Flat things that want to be flat. Towels are somewhere in between — they cooperate but they're thick enough that the final fold always has that rounded edge that won't stay down. I stack them with the rounded edge facing the back of the closet so it looks neat from the front. I don't know when I started doing that. It seems like the kind of thing I watched someone do once and absorbed without filing it as a lesson.

The zipper is still going. Tick. Tick. The dryer has nine minutes left on the display but dryer minutes aren't real minutes, they stretch or compress depending on the moisture sensor, so nine could be six or fourteen.

I should check whether we have more dryer sheets actually.
