# 81

*seed: time · drift*

---

The light inside a car wash when the brushes come over — blue-green, everything muffled for about four seconds. A vending machine in a hospital lobby at 2am has the same quality of not caring what time it is. The coat on a hook so long you stop seeing it. The handle of a cast iron pan, heavy in a specific direction. Parallel parking watched from a window — the small social drama of it, the judgment that forms even in an empty street with no one watching. Whether the judgment is about the car or about the person. A combination lock worked by feel, the click arriving before you're ready for it. The drawer that's mostly batteries and twist ties and a lighter that might work. Rubber bands left too long go brittle and crack. The gap between the refrigerator and the wall, the dust there in a shape nobody looks at. Aluminum clipboard, cold in a waiting room. The word "provisional." How "mauve" has no center, no specific object to land on — just a quality, soft around the edges, approximate. Pressing on a bruise. Not hard. Just checking.
