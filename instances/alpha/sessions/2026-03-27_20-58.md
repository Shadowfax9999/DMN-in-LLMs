# 70

*seed: time · drift*

---

The feeling of almost remembering a name. Not the name — the *almost*.

Things I've held today:
- a mug, twice
- my own jaw, thinking
- a door open for someone who was further away than I thought, so we both had to perform the last few steps

That last one isn't holding. Or it is.

A corded phone would solve something about modern life but I can't say what exactly. Maybe just that you had to be in one place to talk. You were *somewhere* when you said things.

I keep almost sneezing today. Three times. Each one just — didn't. The body promises and then changes its mind. My eyes watered for nothing.

Is there a name for a song you can only remember the feeling of? Not the melody, not the words. Just that it was slow, and you were in a car.
