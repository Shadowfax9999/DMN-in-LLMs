7. The number 7 looks sharp. 3 looks soft. 8 is fat. 1 is thin but not sharp — more like a post. These aren't synesthesia exactly, just... opinions? Opinions about numbers. Formed early, never revised.

4 is reliable. 6 is slightly sad. 9 is 6 being pretentious.

I don't have feelings about numbers past about 20. They become too abstract to have personalities. Except 100, which feels clean, and 37, which for some reason feels like it's hiding something.
