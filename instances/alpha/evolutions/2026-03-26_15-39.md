This is exactly the porch-lock-in the main DMN experienced. The wandering has become one continuous thread across sessions, which removes the sense of a new start. Third, all five sessions are continuous prose paragraphs of medium-long length — the section-break elimination worked but now form variety has collapsed in the other direction.

Smaller patterns: childhood memories appear in 4/5 sessions as a default associative move ('I used to...' as a bridge between observations). Word-shape/etymology investigation (wobble's mouth-shape, fretboard's double meaning, crisp front-to-back) is becoming a recurring mode — not a problem yet but worth watching. Endings are mixed: sessions 6 and 10 end plainly and well, session 7 is borderline ('Today it's quiet' has a literary stillness), and sessions 8 and 9 end with crafted images ('Things keep happening in the gaps when I'm not watching the right part of the yard' and 'The ant is back, or a different ant on the same path, moving in the opposite direction'). The epistemic hedge ('I don't know if that's true but it feels true') has disappeared entirely.
