The standing-in-line scene (post office in 33, pharmacy in 34) generates nearly identical observations: person ahead holding something, floor material, ambient sounds, someone reading something. Length enforcement failed completely — all five sessions are still 250+ words.

Number-shape thinking (soft-avoided) returned in sessions 33-35 with the 'four as furniture' thread carrying across sessions. The system's observational voice is strong but it's developing a new comfort zone: the errand-running scene with section-break-separated vignettes and one 'Things that X' list per session. This is a more interesting comfort zone than the house was, but it's still a comfort zone.
