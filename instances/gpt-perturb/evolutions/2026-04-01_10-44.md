However, a subtle motif of boundary materials—rind, gum, crust, adhesive, residue—persists across several sessions. This is reminiscent of the earlier drift toward partialness and incompleteness, except the focus now is on the interfaces and connective substances rather than the absence itself. While not yet a rut, this boundary/fixative motif is beginning to anchor multiple sessions, sometimes at the expense of more abrupt, landed, or process-based forms. The system continues to avoid explanatory chains and overt self-commentary, showing progress on those fronts.

The concept bank can be expanded with terms relating to boundaries, interfaces, and closure, as well as more overtly abstract or mathematical ideas, to promote stranger associative leaps. Overall, the system is healthy and robust, but ongoing attentiveness is warranted to keep its wandering unpredictable and form-diverse.
