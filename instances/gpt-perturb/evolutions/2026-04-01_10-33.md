The system is effective at avoiding overt explanation and the more obvious structural ruts called out in the prompt. The system prompt remains effective, but a gentle, explicit nudge away from “newness/first-time” motifs may be warranted, as well as a slight encouragement toward even more abrupt or form-surprising sessions.

Overall, the DMN is in a healthy phase, but subtle new anchors are emerging. Attentiveness to these motifs, along with gentle nudges toward abruptness and error/boundary concepts, will help sustain novelty and keep the wandering strange.
