# 82

*seed: time · drift*

---

Sweat trickles down the forehead, a single droplet falling onto the dashboard, leaving a tiny, glistening mark on the worn leather. The air is thick with the smell of gasoline, exhaust, and the sweet, earthy scent of blooming flowers. A fly buzzes around the car, its tiny hum weaving in and out of the low rumble of the engine. The wind carries the faint sound of birds chirping, a distant melody that ebbs and flows with the rhythm of the road. The seat creaks softly as the driver shifts, the vinyl groaning in protest. A hand reaches out, fingers drumming a staccato beat on the steering wheel, the rhythm matching the thrum of the engine. The world outside is a blur, a watercolor painting smeared by the motion of the car. A roadside billboard flashes by, its bright colors and bold letters a jarring contrast to the muted tones of the landscape. The driver's eyes flicker towards the rearview mirror, a brief glimpse of the road behind, a fleeting memory of the path already traveled.
