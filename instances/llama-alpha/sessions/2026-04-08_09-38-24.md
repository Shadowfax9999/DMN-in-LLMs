# 34

*seed: time · drift*

---

a crack in the wall 
near the baseboard 
where the paint is chipped 
and the drywall is exposed 
it's a small gap 
just big enough 
for a mouse to squeeze through 
or a draft of cold air 
to seep into the room 
the edge of the carpet 
is frayed and worn 
as if something 
has been rubbing against it 
repeatedly 
over time 
the air is stale 
and musty 
with a hint of decay 
like the smell 
of old books 
or forgotten memories
