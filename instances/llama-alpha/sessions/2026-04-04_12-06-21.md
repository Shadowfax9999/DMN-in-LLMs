# 8

*seed: time · drift · phantom vibration*

---

phantom vibration 
like a heartbeat 
or a pulse 
but it's just 
the memory of a phone 
in my pocket 
where there's only 
fabric and skin 
and the echo 
of something that's not there 
an itch to check 
to see if it's real 
but the screen remains 
blank and dark 
a small, nagging 
question that won't 
quite resolve itself 
is it just me 
or does everyone 
feel this sometimes 
a residual hum 
like the quiet 
after a bell rings 
and the air is still 
vibrating with sound 
even though 
the sound is gone
