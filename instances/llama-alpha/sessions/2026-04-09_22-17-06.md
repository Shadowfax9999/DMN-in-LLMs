It's the kind of sound that might be felt as much as heard, a vibration that resonates through the air, making the edges of things seem slightly blurry. A series of snapshots: a close-up of a rusty hinge, the curved edge of a coin, the pattern of threads in a piece of worn fabric. Something about the way light reflects off the surface of a still pond, creating a perfect mirror image, but with a slight ripple, a disturbance that breaks the symmetry. The smell of old books, musty and dry, like the scent of forgotten memories. A single, unconnected word: cascade.
