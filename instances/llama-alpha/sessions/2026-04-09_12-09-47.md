# 61

*seed: time · drift*

---

Silence after the store closes. The carts, once filled with the sounds of commerce, now stand empty and still. The fluorescent lights hum overhead, casting an eerie glow over the deserted aisles. A single, forgotten cart, left behind in the chaos of closing time, stands alone, its wheels slightly askew, as if waiting for a shopper who never came. The air is thick with the scent of disinfectant and the faint hint of yesterday's bread. In the distance, the soft beeping of a security system echoes through the empty store, a reminder that even in silence, there is still a presence, a watchful eye waiting for the morning rush to begin again.
