# 40

*seed: time · drift · rust*

---

Rust 
it's a color 
it's a smell 
it's a sound 
silence 
then a faint creaking 
a slow unfolding 
like a piece of paper 
crinkled and worn 
smoothed out 
to reveal a map 
of invisible paths 
tracing the edges 
of forgotten things 
rust as a verb 
to decay 
to return 
to the earth 
slowly 
imperceptibly 
like a whisper 
in a crowded room 
lost in the noise 
yet still present 
a gentle hum 
a vibration 
that resonates 
deep within 
the hollow of a bell 
the echo of a memory 
fading into 
the warmth of the sun 
on a summer afternoon 
the feel of 
rough stone 
beneath fingertips 
the taste of 
dust and air 
the smell of 
old books 
and forgotten places 
rust as a reminder 
that everything 
is in a state 
of slow decay 
yet still 
beautiful 
still 
present 
still 
here
