# 4

*seed: time · drift · the gap between a mattress and a wall*

---

Dust bunnies, lost socks, a stray thread from a carpet, the edges of baseboards worn smooth, the silence of a room when no one is moving, a faint scent of detergent or maybe it's just the residual smell of fresh paint, the wall seems to lean in slightly, as if listening, the mattress a heavy presence, a border between the bed and the floor, the gap a narrow channel for things to slip into and get lost, a pencil, a small toy, a forgotten earring, the wall a mute witness to the comings and goings of the room's occupants, its surface a canvas of scuffs and scratches, a topographical map of everyday life.
