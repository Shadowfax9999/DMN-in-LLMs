# 1

*seed: time*

---

The sound of a bicycle bell, bright and sharp, cuts through the air. A flash of sunlight on chrome. The smell of cut grass, damp earth, and the faintest hint of blooming flowers. A feeling of warmth on skin, the gentle caress of a spring breeze. The rough texture of a tree trunk, the sensation of leaning against it, feeling the ridges and grooves of the bark. 

A bird takes flight, its wings beating rapidly as it disappears into the leafy canopy above. The rustle of leaves, the snapping of twigs, the creaking of branches. The sensation of walking on uneven terrain, the gentle rocking of ankles, the soft give of the earth beneath feet. 

The image of a small, tranquil pond, its surface reflecting the sky above like a mirror. The sound of gentle lapping of water against the shore, the soft chirping of birds in the distance. A sense of stillness, of quiet contemplation. 

The taste of fresh air, the sensation of breathing deeply, feeling the lungs expand and contract. The smell of ozone, the faint hint of a distant storm. The feeling of anticipation, of possibility, that comes with a spring morning. 

The sound of children's laughter, the joyful shouts of play, the thud of a ball bouncing on pavement. The image of a sun-drenched street, the warmth and vitality of a community coming alive on a Saturday morning. 

The sensation of holding a warm cup of coffee, feeling the heat radiate through fingers, the rich aroma filling the senses. The taste of freshly brewed coffee, the bitterness and acidity mingling on the tongue. 

A fragment of a memory, a glimpse of a forgotten moment, a half-remembered scent. The feeling of trying to grasp something elusive, something that slips through fingers like sand.
