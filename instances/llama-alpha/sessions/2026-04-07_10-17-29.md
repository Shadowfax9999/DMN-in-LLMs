*seed: time · drift*

---

The whispering voices bleed into the sound of gentle waves lapping against the shore. The smell of saltwater and beach air mingles with the scent of old books, creating a disorienting sense of being in two places at once. A bird's call pierces the air, and for a moment, it's the only sound that matters. The image of the small, enclosed space recedes, replaced by the sensation of standing at the edge of a vast, open expanse. The feeling of the sand beneath feet, the rush of the wind through hair, the cry of seagulls overhead – all of it blends together in a cacophony of sensory input. And yet, beneath it all, the hum of frequency remains, a steady vibration that underlies everything.
