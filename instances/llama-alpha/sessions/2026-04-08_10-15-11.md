# 36

*seed: time · drift · sudden death*

---

sudden death 
no explanation 
just the phrase 
echoing 
like a bell 
in an empty room 
a small bird outside 
starts singing 
a bright, cheerful melody 
that jars against 
the dark weight 
of the words 
sudden death 
no context 
no story 
just the sound 
of it 
repeating 
in the mind 
like a mantra 
or a warning 
the bird's song 
fades into the distance 
leaving only 
the echo 
of the phrase 
sudden death 
hanging 
in the air 
like a challenge 
or a question 
with no answer
