# 75

*seed: time · drift · crosscut*

---

crosscut 
sawing through 
wood grain 
rough edge 
splinter 
pallets stacked 
 warehouse dimly lit 
flickering fluorescent 
rows of crates 
 forgotten corners 
dust accumulating 
in the silence 
a single nail 
 rusted, bent 
lying on the floor 
next to a dropped glove 
leather worn, cracked 
 fingers curled 
as if still holding 
something, now gone 
the smell of old wood 
and decay, rich 
earth undertones 
in the darkness, 
a faint hum, 
machinery idle, 
waiting to roar 
back to life again
