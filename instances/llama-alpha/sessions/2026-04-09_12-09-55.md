*seed: time · drift · nickname*

nickname 
sounded like a name itself
for a moment I thought of it as a person 
then a memory of a pet 
a cat with a name that wasn't its real name 
but the one we used 
it was a color 
the color of its fur 
not the actual name 
but what we called it 
the connection to nickname 
was the substitution 
one word for another 
not a replacement 
but an addition 
a new layer 
like a second skin 
I think of other words 
that do this 
stand in for something else 
without replacing it 
a gentle overlap 
a shadow 
the word nickname 
still resonating 
as I consider 
the way it attaches 
to a name 
like a small echo 
a second beat 
in the rhythm of a word
