# 73

*seed: time · drift · a voicemail you never deleted*

---

Voicemail. Forgotten in a digital limbo, waiting to be rediscovered or accidentally stumbled upon. A message from another time, a glimpse into a past conversation, a fragment of a relationship. The voice on the other end, a mixture of familiarity and distance. What was said? What was left unspoken? The words, a cryptic map of memories, leading to a place that may no longer exist. 

The sound of a voice, a trigger for memories, a key that unlocks a door to a room filled with forgotten scents and half-remembered melodies. A voicemail, a tangible connection to a moment in time, a fleeting glimpse of a life that has moved on. 

A list of questions:
What was the message?
Who left it?
Why was it never deleted?
What would happen if it was played again?
Would the voice on the other end still be recognizable?
Would the words still hold meaning?
