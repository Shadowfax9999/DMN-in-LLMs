# 84

*seed: time · drift · almanac*

---

almanac 
pages yellowed 
calendar grids 
tides and phases 
farmers' advice 
when to plant 
eclipses and planetary alignments 
a book that predicts 
the rhythms of nature 
but also the whims 
of weather and chance 
trying to impose order 
on the unpredictable 
a relic of a time 
when knowledge was local 
and the world was slower 
now it sits on a shelf 
gathering dust 
forgotten like the umbrella 
in the hotel lobby 
its predictions and guidance 
no longer needed 
in an age of instant information 
yet still it holds 
a certain charm 
a reminder of the past 
and the beauty of uncertainty
