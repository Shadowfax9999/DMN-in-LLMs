*seed: time · drift · wrong door*

---

Wrong door. The handle doesn't turn, the latch doesn't lift. A sign on the door, faded, peeling: "Do Not Enter". The letters are crooked, handwritten, in red. Red like an exit sign, or a warning light. Warning lights flash, strobe, and pulse. Pulse like a heartbeat, fast and slow. Fast like a machine, whirring, spinning. Spinning like a top, round and round. Round like a circle, complete, whole. Whole like a sphere, smooth, unbroken. Unbroken like a promise, kept, and sealed. Sealed like an envelope, closed, and stamped. Stamped like a document, official, and final. Final like a last word, spoken, and done. 

A list of unconnected things:
- A typewriter keyboard
- A glass of cold water
- A forgotten melody
- A pair of scissors
- A rainy afternoon

The sound of rain, outside, on the roof. The roof is metal, corrugated, and loud. Loud like a drumbeat, steady, and strong. Strong like a current, rushing, and deep. Deep like a well, dark, and mysterious. Mysterious like a code, uncracked, and hidden. Hidden like a message, encrypted, and secret. Secret like a whisper, quiet, and unseen. Unseen like a ghost, invisible, and gone.
