*seed: time · drift*

A faint hum, like the quiet buzzing of a fly, but it's not a fly, it's the refrigerator in the next room. The sound is constant, yet it's only noticeable when the house is quiet. Sometimes the hum is accompanied by a faint rattle, like the sound of a small object vibrating against the fridge's wall. The rattle is soft, almost imperceptible, but it's there, a subtle underscore to the hum. The sound of the fridge is like a steady heartbeat, a reminder that even in silence, there is always something present, always something humming in the background.
