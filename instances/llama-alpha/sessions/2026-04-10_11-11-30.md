# 81

*seed: time · drift*

---

a faint hum of an engine 
warm air on skin 
texture of worn vinyl 
scent of gasoline and leather 
a low rumble that vibrates through the seat 
the world blurs by in a green and brown haze 
a strip of asphalt unwinding like a black snake 
the sensation of movement, of velocity, of freedom 
no particular destination in mind 
just the act of moving, of going, of being in motion 
the wind whipping through hair, the sun beating down 
a sense of release, of escape, of letting go 
the world rushing by, a kaleidoscope of color and sound
