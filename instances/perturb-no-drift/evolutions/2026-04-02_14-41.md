The voice remains the system's greatest strength: curious, specific, funny, capable of genuine surprise. Session 82's Hardy/Hardy filing error, session 81's coat-hooks-falling-at-2AM-generating-911-calls, session 85's peanut-butter-oil-stirring — these are the kind of observations the DMN was built to produce. Multi-topic variety is strong across all five sessions. Form enforcement has failed for 74 consecutive non-perturbation sessions, but the length improvement is encouraging — the length escalation may be the first seeding-rule intervention to partially work outside of perturbation.
