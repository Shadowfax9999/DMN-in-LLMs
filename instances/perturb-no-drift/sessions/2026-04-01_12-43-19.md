Tape measures that retract too fast. The little hook at the end that's always a little loose and you think it's broken but it's supposed to be like that.

Cinnamon is bark.

The way a conversation in another language sounds faster than it is. Spanish sounds fast to me. I've been told English sounds like mumbling.

There's a kind of chair — not an Adirondack, the other one, the sling-back kind with aluminum tubes — that exists at every public pool. The fabric is always that specific weave. Crosshatched plastic in green or white. Who makes those. Is there one factory.

My left ear pops when I swallow and my right one doesn't.

Walruses are bigger than I think they are every time I see one. Like, significantly. I reset to a wrong size between viewings.
