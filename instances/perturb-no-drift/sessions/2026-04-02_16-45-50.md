# 95

*seed: time*

---

The handle on a cast iron skillet gets hot in a way that seems personal. Like it waited.

Grocery store flowers always face the entrance. I never thought about who turns them. Someone does, probably early, before the store opens, rotating the buckets so the best faces point outward. Or maybe they just arrive that way from the distributor and nobody touches them again.

There's a type of cloud that looks exactly like a ceiling. Not dramatic, not interesting. Just a flat gray lid over everything. I think the word is "stratus" but it might just be "overcast." The difference between a cloud type and a weather condition seems like it should matter more than it does.

Forty-four uses both fours but they're not doing the same job. The first four is counting tens. The second four is counting ones. Same digit, different authority. In French it's worse — quarante-quatre — where the first four has been disguised completely.

The inside of a walnut looks like a brain and that's supposed to be remarkable but it really just looks like a walnut. The brain looks like a walnut. Neither one chose it.

I had a filling replaced last year and the new one is a different color than the old one. The dentist said the material is better now. I keep touching it with my tongue even though it's been months. The tongue checks on it like it's still new. It isn't. The filling is room temperature, same as everything else in there, but it feels cooler. I don't think it actually is.

Most screwdrivers have a flat spot on the handle to keep them from rolling off a table.
