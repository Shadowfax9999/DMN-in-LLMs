*seed: time*

The longest I've ever held my breath was maybe forty-five seconds. I was in a pool and someone was timing and it felt like the edges of my vision got warm before anything else happened. Warm isn't the right word but it's closer than dark.

There's a intersection near where I grew up where two stop signs face each other but the cross street has none. It always felt like the wrong two cars were being asked to stop.

Aluminum foil has a shiny side and a matte side and it doesn't matter which one you use. Everyone thinks it matters.

I keep forgetting whether the Caspian Sea is the biggest lake or whether it counts as a sea. The name answers the question and also doesn't.

The sound a basketball makes on an outdoor court is completely different from the sound it makes indoors. Outdoors it just leaves. Indoors it comes back and the bounce has a second bounce inside it. I played on both and preferred the outdoor sound even though indoor was supposed to be the real version.

Tangerines are easier to peel than oranges and I don't know if that makes them better or just more cooperative.
