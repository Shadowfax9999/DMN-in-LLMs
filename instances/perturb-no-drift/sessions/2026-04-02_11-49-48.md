The texture of a band-aid. The fabric kind, not the plastic kind. That slightly fuzzy weave that pills if you rub it. The plastic ones are worse in every way but they're what you get now. The fabric ones came in a tin.

A window sash is heavy and painted shut. A pageant sash says MISS SOMETHING in gold letters. A dress sash is a belt that isn't a belt.
