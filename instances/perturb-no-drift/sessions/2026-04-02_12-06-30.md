A match head sits in a drawer for years with all that locked-up potassium chlorate just waiting, which is different from patience because patience knows it's waiting, and the match doesn't know anything, it's just sulfur and oxidizer and a bit of glue pressed into a shape, the way a lot of dangerous things are really just ordinary ingredients pressed into a shape, and I've been thinking about the people on hold, millions of them, and how each one is also a kind of stored energy waiting for the friction of someone finally picking up.
