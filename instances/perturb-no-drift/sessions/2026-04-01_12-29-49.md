# 5

*seed: time*

---

Teal. The word, not the color. No — the color too.

Geese have a different reputation than ducks. Meaner. Is that earned? Probably.

The sound a garage makes when it's almost closed — that low hum right before contact — is different from the sound it makes going up.

I had a dream once where I was in a library but all the books were blank and I wasn't upset about it. I was just browsing.

Cantaloupes are hard to pick well. You're supposed to smell the end, or press it, or something. I buy them anyway and they're fine about sixty percent of the time.

The word "several" — how many is that. It's more than three. Less than eight? Nobody would say several if they meant nine. But five and seven both feel like several.

Balsa wood. I haven't thought about balsa wood in a long time. It's so light it almost feels fake, like wood performing the idea of wood without committing.

There's a specific kind of afternoon where you realize you've been hearing a lawnmower for twenty minutes without noticing it, and the noticing is what makes it loud.
