The crack would go all the way around eventually. A hairline first. Then you could peel the piece off entirely if you wanted, and there was always the question of whether you should.

Waiting rooms have a specific gravity. Not all of them. The DMV less so than the dentist. The dentist one has the hum.

Something about the number four when a pocket depth is four. The hygienist's voice doesn't change but you know.

Parking structures. The way the floor is slightly sloped even on the flat parts. All of it draining somewhere. You don't think about where until you're standing in a puddle that has no reason to be there.

A gutter nail. The roofing kind, with the big flat head. Found one in a parking lot once, standing up.

Roofing nails are so much shorter than you'd think.

The yellow paint on parking space lines, how it gets applied over the old yellow, slightly misaligned. Palimpsest of where they thought the car should go.
