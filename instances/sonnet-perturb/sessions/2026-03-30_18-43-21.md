Every time. You know it says pull, you've done this before, and you still push first.

---

Spring evenings have a particular quality where the light stays too long and you don't know what to do with the extra. You've finished dinner. It's still light. The day isn't over but it also is.

A cardinal on the fence. Then not.

---

Thirty-two points on a compass rose. The in-between ones have names like WNW that feel like airport codes. There's a regional airport somewhere called that, probably. A single baggage carousel. A car rental desk that's also the information desk.

The smell of jet fuel is different depending on which way the wind is.

---

I've never understood what "as the crow flies" adds that "straight line" doesn't. Except that the crow does fly. The crow is real. The straight line is abstract. The crow just goes.

Bouillon cubes come in chicken and beef and sometimes vegetable, which always feels like an apology.
