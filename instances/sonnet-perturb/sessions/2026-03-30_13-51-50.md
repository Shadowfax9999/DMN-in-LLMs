Monday at 1:51 means the morning is already gone and the afternoon is a long corridor.

Corridors have a thing where you can't tell how long they are until you're in them. The far end looks close. You start walking and it doesn't get closer right away. Then all at once you're there.

That happens with the week too. Tuesday feels very far from Friday. Then it's Friday.
