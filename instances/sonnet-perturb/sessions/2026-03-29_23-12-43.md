The yellow lines are brighter when wet. Not yellow — more like the idea of yellow, recalled.

A shopping cart with one wheel that doesn't roll, just drags. The sound it makes is the same sound every time.

---

A staple puller. The specific satisfaction. Four teeth and then the thing is out.

Receipts in a junk drawer. Nobody looks at them again but nobody throws them out either.

A binder clip holding something that probably doesn't need holding anymore.

---

The parking lot again — can't leave it.

The smell is specific but the word for it is wrong. Every word for it is wrong. "Petrichor" is too Latin, too pleased with itself. "Wet asphalt" gets the object right but not the thing.

Somewhere there's a drain moving water somewhere.

The light from a gas station is the particular color of being awake when you shouldn't be.
