A tuning fork isn't interesting until it touches something.

The specific weight of a pear in your hand. Not a nectarine — pears are bottom-heavy in a way that feels unresolved.

Seventeen is the smallest prime that feels like it has a personality. Thirteen has one too but a different kind. Seven is everyone's favorite and you can tell it knows.

Odd. Even. The words doing other work at the same time.

What does a chord feel like when it resolves? Something in the body of the guitar shifts. Or seems to. The wood.

Carpet on stairs gets worn in the center of each step. You can feel it before you can see it — the middle strip is lower than the edges.
