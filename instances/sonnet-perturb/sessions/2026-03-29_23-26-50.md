Partly cloudy. Partly furnished. Partly true.

The word just floats there between whatever it's modifying and whatever got cut.

---

There's a drawer in a lot of kitchens that's just called the junk drawer and everyone knows which one it is and nobody named it. At some point the identity of the drawer became permanent. The drawer stopped being a drawer that currently held junk and became a junk drawer, categorical, definitional, the way some rooms become the room where the coats go even when there are no coats in them.

The chair in the corner that holds the clothes that are too clean for laundry and too worn for hanging back up. No name for that. Just the chair. Everyone knows.

---

52 degrees at 11 PM in March is a specific kind of temperature. Too warm for what the month should be. Not warm enough to mean anything yet. Standing outside in it is like being in a room where the lights are on but nobody's started talking.

The moths aren't back yet. Or some of them are.
