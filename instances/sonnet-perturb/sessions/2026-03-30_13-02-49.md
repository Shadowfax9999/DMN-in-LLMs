The five doesn't change when you know it's coming. You've had fives before. The probe goes in the same way. The number is just a number until it isn't.

Parking structure drains. All those sloped floors with nowhere obvious to put the water. And it does go somewhere — there's a whole system underneath, but you never see it. You just see the puddle in the corner that the slope somehow missed.

Slightly misaligned lines on top of old lines. The yellow is a specific yellow. School bus yellow isn't quite the same. Neither is caution tape.

---

Caution tape vs. police tape. Yellow vs. yellow-and-black. One means don't walk here because something fell. The other means don't walk here because something happened. The distinction matters but they're the same motion — duck under or step over, look for the reason, move on.

Tape that's still up after the reason is gone. Fluttering from one cone. The cone by itself with no apparent purpose.

A hard hat on a peg in a hallway that doesn't look like a construction hallway.

The number four. The four in pocket depth. The four on a credit card expiration date, the month-column one. March has thirty-one days and the thirty-first always catches me.
