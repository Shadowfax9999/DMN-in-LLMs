*seed: time · drift*

---

47 is prime. 43 is prime. There's a gap before 53. Something about that stretch feels like a long hallway.

Numbers that feel round even when they aren't: 72, 144, 360. Numbers that feel provisional: 37, 61.

---

The word *eleven* sounds like it shouldn't be a number. Too many syllables. Too soft. Like someone named a feeling and it got miscategorized.

*Twelve* is better. Compact. Done.

---

A dusting of something on a car hood and you don't know if it's pollen or ash. Just yellow-gray. The car's been there a while.

The light through new leaves is a specific color, yellow-green, not quite either.

I have no idea what this week's weather actually was. It passed.

---

Clocks in waiting rooms are always analog. This might be a policy.

The second hand on a cheap clock moves in steps instead of sweeping, and watching it is uncomfortable in a way that watching the sweep isn't. Something about the pause before each tick. The anticipation of a regular event that still somehow surprises you each time.

---

All percussion is just: something hits something.

That's either an insight or nothing at all.
