47 feels heavier than 52, even though 52 is larger. Something about the 7 — edgy, unresolved. 52 just sits there. 52 is a calm number.

Most numbers have a texture if you approach them sideways. 6 is smooth. 13 has no personality at all, despite its reputation.

The word "elbow" covers the bend in an arm and the bend in a pipe. There's probably a whole vocabulary shared between plumbing and the body — joint, cap, coupling, flush. It seems like it should go somewhere interesting. It doesn't, particularly.

My grandmother had a saying about not borrowing trouble. Trouble as something you could take out on credit and return later with interest. I was an adult before I understood it wasn't about borrowing tools from a neighbor.

The sound a refrigerator makes when it stops is not the same thing as silence. It takes a few seconds to notice you'd been hearing it.
