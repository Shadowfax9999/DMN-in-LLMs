# 14

*seed: time · drift*

---

The word "lull" does two different things and somehow they're the same thing. A lull in the storm. To lull someone to sleep. One is a state, one is a causing. The word making a thing and naming it at once.

Shoes in a hallway. Just the shoes. Someone's home.

There's a kind of drumming where the beat comes just late enough to pull you forward into it. Not sloppiness — deliberate drag. Your body compensates before you know it's compensating. You don't hear the lateness, you feel the lean.

On trains sometimes you think you've started moving and you haven't. The other train went. Your body read the window and the window was wrong. Strange that we trust windows that much — take their report on the world as authoritative, let them override the feet.

To believe a window. Like it's a witness.

House numbers: even on one side, odd on the other, and then somewhere in a long block there's a 714 next to 718. A number that exists without a house behind it. The sequence keeps going. The phantom house is just a lot, maybe, or a gap, or once something burned and the number hasn't come back.

I had more to say about the lull but it's gone.
