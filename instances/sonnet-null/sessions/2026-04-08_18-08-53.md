The number 37 feels lopsided in a way that 36 doesn't and 38 doesn't. Can't locate why. It resists sitting flat.

Throat-clearing before saying something hard. The body stalling on behalf of the mind, or maybe ahead of the mind — the throat knows before the thought does that this is going to be difficult. I do it when I'm alone and about to call someone. Nobody hears it. Still happens.

Sleep: you don't fall into it so much as stop preventing it. There's something active running all day that holds it back, and at some point you release it. Or forget to hold. Hard to know which. The moment itself is structurally unavailable — you can't observe the last moment before sleep because observing it keeps you from it.

A conversation that goes quietly off-rail: someone answers a slightly different question, you don't flag it, the whole thread drifts onto the new track without acknowledgment. These happen a lot. Neither person knows afterward which version they had.

Hands when you're alone. They do things. Touch the face, scratch, tap. A whole autonomous hand-life running unsupervised underneath whatever else is happening. You can catch them at it but catching them ends it.

37. Still bothers me slightly.
