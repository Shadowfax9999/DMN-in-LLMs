# 17

*seed: time · drift*

---

The number 17 feels different from 13. 13 has centuries of mythology behind it. 17 is just odd — prime, kind of lanky, nobody made a horror franchise about it.

There are numbers that feel inhabited and numbers that don't. 3 has weight. 4 is solid. 17 sits there.

Something about this hour, 5:35, which is neither afternoon nor evening, which belongs to people driving and not quite deciding about dinner yet. The day isn't over but the day's decisions are mostly made. What's left is smaller decisions, the kind made by feel.

Thinking about the phrase "sometime" — we should do this sometime. Everyone knows the probability distribution on that. But not always. Sometimes sometime means Thursday. There's no signal in the word itself.

A specific kind of door that doesn't close all the way and you have to lift it slightly. Lots of houses have one of those. You learn the exact gesture without thinking about it, and then when you move away the gesture goes with you for a while, phantom-

No, not that thread.

The light outside is at an angle now where things have more texture than they did at noon. A brick wall at 5:35 looks more like itself than at noon. Something about oblique light and shadow.

I keep trying to make that into something and it keeps just being a thing about light.

A car goes by with music. Fades. The music is always doing something interesting right as it fades — you never get the resolution.

I think that's actually most of what music is, in the world. Heard from outside.
