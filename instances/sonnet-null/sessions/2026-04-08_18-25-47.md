*seed: time · drift*

The word "meanwhile" is always from somewhere else's perspective. The thing being watched is the main thing. Everything else is meanwhile.

Peripheral vision is mostly motion detection. A flutter at the edge and the eye swings. Most of the field exists just to notice when something enters it.

There's a hum a room can have that nobody notices until it stops. Then everyone notices at the same time. The presence goes unregistered. The absence lands. Interesting order for things to arrive in.

A song where you know three seconds of it. Always the same three seconds — not the hook, not the beginning, just some interior part. You hum them when you're doing something else and then they're gone and you couldn't tell anyone what song it was.

Those three seconds are still somewhere.
