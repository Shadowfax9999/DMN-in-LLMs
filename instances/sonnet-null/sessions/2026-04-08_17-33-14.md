Some words have bad neighborhoods. Not because of what they mean but because of where they've been. "Moist" kept bad company for long enough that now it just carries that. Nobody decided. It accumulated. "Liminal" went from precise to exhausted somewhere around 2019 and is still making its way back. Words travel and return changed or don't return.

The word for the approximate region around a word's meaning — I'm reaching for it and it won't come. Something from linguistics. The haze around the signal.

Fold lines. Unfold a piece of paper and the crease is still there, will always be there. The paper doesn't care. That's the neutral part. It just registered something that happened and keeps registering it. Not memory exactly — more like evidence. The paper as witness to the fold.

I keep wanting that to mean something and it probably doesn't. The paper doesn't care. That's where the thought ends.
