The back left burner. I actually don't know if mine works. There's a pot on it — has been for a while — one of those heavy ones that's easier to leave than to put away.

Friction as storage system.

---

Cardinal's still going probably. They do that for a long time. Longer than you'd think you could sustain one intention.

What does boredom feel like if you don't have the concept. If you're a bird repeating a call for forty minutes, is minute thirty-eight different from minute four. Maybe the body just does it and the mind — whatever that is there — is somewhere else entirely.

---

Pliers. I was thinking about pliers. Not why, just — the way they feel when they're the wrong size for the job. Too big, and you're holding this tiny nail and the pliers keep slipping past it, gripping air. The teeth don't meet around anything. You end up using your fingers anyway.

Needle-nose pliers are a different object entirely. They don't even feel related.
