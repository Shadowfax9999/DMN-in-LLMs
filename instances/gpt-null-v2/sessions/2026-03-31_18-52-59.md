Daffodils, like yellow exclamation points that never soften their tone.

A line of ants, making their slow process across the cracked sidewalk, some detour around a glob of sap, others climb straight over.

I haven't seen anyone mow this patch of lawn yet this year.

The air has a kind of restless softness, not settled, like it’s holding back some news.

There's a shopping cart tipped at an angle against the bike rack.
