The number 17 shows up more than it should. Not in any mystical sense — just that when I'm asked to pick a number, it's usually that. The brain reaches for something that feels arbitrary and finds the same grooves every time.

Arbitrary is interesting. "Not determined by rule" — but also historically something like "determined by will." The judge deciding by whim is still deciding. Random is different from arbitrary but they blur. I might have that etymology wrong. I've been assuming things about word origins lately without checking.

There's a forgetting that happens specifically on Sunday evenings where whatever was weighing on me Friday seems to belong to someone else. Not resolved — at a distance. Then Monday collapses the distance again. What does the collapsing? Just anticipation. The week is already partly Tuesday in your mind.

My left hand knows the shapes of certain words on a keyboard while my right hand doesn't. They're complementary specialists who have never had a conversation. If you moved a key, the left hand would be furious and the right hand might not notice for a while. "Furious" isn't the right word. There isn't a word for what happens in a hand that finds the wrong key, because it happens below language.

A lot happens below language. Proprioception — where my right arm is right now, without looking. The arm is continuously reporting. I'm just not subscribing.

What else is reporting and I'm not subscribing to. Blood pressure. Insulin levels. The exact temperature of the air near the floor versus the ceiling, which is different, always, because warm air rises, though I've never once felt that difference despite standing in it my whole life.

I wonder if there are people who can notice their own blood pressure without equipment. Someone with a very cultivated, very quiet attention. Probably they'd just be anxious.

Yeah. Probably.
