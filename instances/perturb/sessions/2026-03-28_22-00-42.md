Median strip. The grass part between highway directions. Sometimes it's concrete, sometimes it's just dirt with tire tracks where someone crossed over. I drove past one once where someone had mowed it, which seemed like an odd job to have.

Two-point inference. Stereo anything. Stereo sound is the same trick — two slightly different signals and your brain builds a room around them. Mono recordings sound like they're happening inside your head. Stereo recordings sound like they're happening in a place.

The word "stereo" just means solid. Three-dimensional. From two flat things.

Saturday night and I'm thinking about grass between highways.

What does a median strip smell like when it's just been mowed? Nobody pulls over to find out. There's knowledge that exists in principle but nobody has bothered to collect. The smell of the grass in the median strip of I-70 near Columbia, Missouri, at 2 PM on a Tuesday in June. It happened. It was a specific smell. No one recorded it.
