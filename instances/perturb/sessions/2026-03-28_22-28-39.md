The fourteenth floor is a weird floor to be on. Most buildings that have a fourteenth floor skipped thirteen, so it's really the thirteenth floor pretending. I wonder if anyone on the fourteenth floor of those buildings ever thinks about that. Probably not. You don't think about the floor you're on unless the elevator is broken.
