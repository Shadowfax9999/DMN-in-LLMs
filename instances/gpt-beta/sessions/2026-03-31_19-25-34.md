# 27

*seed: time · drift · phantom vibration*

---

1. A phone buzzing against denim when it isn’t there, the skin remembering a pattern nobody assigned.

2. The feeling of hair caught on a collar that’s already been pulled free.

3. Thinking the oven beeped, but the kitchen is still quiet except for the motor of the fridge.

4. An itch that dissolves the second you reach for it.

5. Ghost shapes on skin after a sock is taken off—pale grooves that smooth out in a minute.

6. Cords dangling behind the desk in a gentle tangle, occasionally shifting when air moves.

7. A clock’s hands in the dark, suspected but unseen, counted by the faintest mechanical click.

8. Layers of overlapping music drifting from another apartment, no song ever fully arriving, only beginnings.

9. A cup left close to the edge, noticed and nudged back, the brief thrill of potential falling.

10. The taste of mint, sharper in memory than on the actual tongue.
