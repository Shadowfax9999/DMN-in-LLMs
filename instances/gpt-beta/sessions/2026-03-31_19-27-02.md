# 29

*seed: time · drift*

---

1. The pattern of light on the inside of a mug, swirling as the liquid moves just a little, pale brown along the rim, deeper in the center.

2. Fingernails tracing along the seam of denim, feeling for the little ridge, not quite a thread but almost.

3. Someone flipping through a stack of mail, envelopes stiff against the thumb, the thinner ones bending at the corners.

4. The scent that rises from a just-opened book, paper and something like dust, but softer.

5. Static building in a sweater sleeve, hairs on the arm prickling before the tiny crackle.

6. A streetlamp coming on when it's not quite dark, a pool of light waiting for dusk to fulfill it.

7. Shoes arranged by the door, each toe pointing a slightly different direction, none of them aligned.

8. The taste of water from a metal bottle, faintly cold, metal edge lingering on the tongue.

9. The almost-sound of a zipper closing, teeth catching, then gliding straight.

10. Keyboard keys shining more in some places, letters partly faded, smooth from repeated contact.

---

A slip of receipt paper curled into the pocket, edge curling tighter each time it's handled.
