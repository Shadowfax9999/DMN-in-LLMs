# 50

*seed: time · drift*

---

The mirror above the sink is fogged on one side and clear in a perfect rectangle where someone wiped it with a sleeve. A sock inside out at the heel, right side out at the toe. Scissors left open on the counter, blades just barely not touching. A cereal box, folded shut without its tab. The plastic handle of a drawer, loose enough to clack each time it’s pulled. An envelope, unopened, with a faint circle where a mug once rested. The pen missing its cap, the cap holding three tooth marks.

---

Red light leaking through the slats of a vent. Three tiles along the baseboard, the grout whiter than all the rest. A lamp with its cord draped over the shade, not quite plugged into the wall. The faint impression of a thumb on the soap. A grocery receipt where the ink has bled out at the top line. The hum that comes before the fridge clicks and settles. The sharp edge of a page in a paperback, folded but not torn.

---

The lid of a jar balanced upside-down on its mouth. A single grape rolled just under the fridge, visible if you kneel and look. The curve of a spoon nested inside another, their shapes not quite matching. The pulse in your thumb when you hold it against the window, warm glass and cold air. A key still in the lock, turned but not removed.
