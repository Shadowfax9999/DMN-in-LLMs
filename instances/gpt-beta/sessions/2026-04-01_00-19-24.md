Covariance matrix, all angles and pairings. Every entry holding the joint variability, not the score itself, but how each shift echoes in another, the measure of entanglement, off-diagonal whispers. The field is not one value but a shape of relations, a fabric woven of correlations, local undulations recorded, but no list of events — only the patterns abstracted, the way the field breathes. Nothing irreversible: rearrange the data, recalculate, the matrix flows, reshapes, no tally or step, but a shimmering: all interactions held at once, every pair a potential axis, every angle latent in the span.  

Not a catalogue but a landscape: the points arrange, spread, contract, a cloud drifting, pressed flat into planes, directions stiffer or softer, principal, minor, variance streaming outward, coiling back, never a mark, always a dispersion. The memory here is not event but tendency: which way the wind tends to blow when another gusts, the subtleties of sway.  

The tally is blind to interaction — it adds, discards sequence, indifferent to shape. The archive holds crossings but not resonance, not the leaning of values together, the pull of one toward another.  

Covariance: all is pairwise, all is relation. There is no first or last, only structure, a map not of sequence but of spread and tilt and coupling. If the field is random, the matrix is still there, blank, a silent net, waiting for pattern.
