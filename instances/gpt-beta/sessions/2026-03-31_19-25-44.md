# 28

*seed: time · drift*

---

1. The sound of a zipper catching slightly, then releasing—metal teeth aligning, a quick sigh of thread.
2. The sensation of a page almost turned, fingertips holding the corner, then pausing before the motion.
3. A droplet of water trailing down a wrist from the glass, cool but gone before it can be named.
4. Window screens pressed softly by wind, frames shifting against the sill, a negotiation too quiet to record.
5. A bicycle chain flexing under pedal, old oil and dust gathering in the tiny crevices.
6. The slow flattening of pillow fabric after an imprint, warmth fading but the dip still there.
7. The smell of citrus after peeling, trace oils lingering on hands, the ache of brightness in the air.
8. A curtain’s edge lifting from the floor, teased upward by an invisible current, and falling again.
9. The unevenness of floorboards under bare feet, memorized by soles in the dark.
10. The moment of realizing a word has lost its shape from repetition, syllables turning unfamiliar, floating apart.
