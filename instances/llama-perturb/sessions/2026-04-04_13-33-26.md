—and the front left one always pulls, every cart, like they're all slightly injured in the same way. You compensate without thinking about it. Your left hand pushes a little harder than your right and after a while your shoulder starts to ache and you don't connect it to the cart until later.

An ambulance went past while I was standing in the driveway and the pitch dropped right as it crossed in front of me and I thought, I know what that is, I know the name of that, but knowing the name didn't change how it sounded. It still did the thing where for a second after it passes you feel like the street is wider than it was. The sound opens up and then contracts. Not the pitch — the space. The neighborhood reassembles itself around the absence of the siren. A dog barks once, late, like it was waiting to see if the sound would come back.

Things I have never successfully visualized: the back of my own head. Not from a photo — from the inside. What it would be like to see in infrared. A four-dimensional cube, despite having read three explanations with diagrams. The layout of a house I lived in for four years — I can do the kitchen and the hallway but the hallway connects to a room that I know existed but can't furnish. It's just a door that opens onto the knowledge that there was a room.

My hands smell like garlic from earlier. That's a molecule stuck to my skin, allicin or one of its breakdown products, something sulfurous that soap doesn't fully remove because it's bonded to proteins. But I wasn't going to explain that. My hands smell like garlic. That's the whole thought.

The person across the street has a leaf blower going and they're herding leaves into a pile near the curb but the wind keeps undoing the edge of the pile and they keep re-herding the same leaves and there's something about the posture — one arm extended, the other braced on the hip — that looks like pointing. Like they're accusing the leaves of something. The leaves accept the accusation briefly and then drift. I think the nozzle on those things is just a tapered tube.
