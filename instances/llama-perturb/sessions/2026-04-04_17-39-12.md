# 40

*seed: time · drift · Aztec calendar stone and the problem of naming days*

---

Grout holds the wrong things together. No — it holds the right things apart.

Twenty day-signs in the Aztec calendar and I can only ever remember Death and Rain. The rest blur. Naming days seems greedy, like naming waves.

The grout between bathroom tiles is slightly darker where water sits longest. Nobody picks that color. It just arrives.

I wonder if "Tuesday" was ever controversial.

The rubber band on the doorknob keeps the latch from clicking shut.
