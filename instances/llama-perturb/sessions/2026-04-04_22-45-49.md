The thing about tuning a guitar is that the string doesn't settle right away — you bring it up to pitch and it's sharp, then flat, then sharp again but less, and you keep nudging it closer like you're trying to park a car in a space that's exactly the right size, back and forth in smaller and smaller increments until the needle sits still, except it never fully sits still, the needle always has a slight tremor to it, and I wonder if that's the string or the air or the tuner's own indecision. Anyway.
