# 19

*seed: time · drift*

---

The feel of a fountain pen in the hand 
the weight of it 
the way the nib glides 
across the paper 
leaving a trail 
of ink that flows 
like a small stream 
through the valleys 
of the page 
sometimes pooling 
in the low areas 
other times 
skittering across 
the surface 
in a stuttering 
dance of light 
and shadow 
the sound 
of the nib 
on the paper 
a soft scratching 
that echoes 
through the room 
like the quiet 
hum of a harp 
string 
the smell 
of the ink 
a pungent 
mix of chemicals 
and earthy 
undertones 
that clings 
to the fingers 
long after 
the writing 
is done 
a lingering 
ghost of creation 
that haunts 
the senses 
and beckons 
the mind 
to return 
to the page 
again 
and again 
and again
