# 90

*seed: time · drift · the sound of a hotel ice machine from down the hall*

---

The hotel ice machine's hum, a low rumble, like the distant growl of a beast, muffled by the walls, yet persistent, a reminder of the world outside the room. Ice clattering, a brief, staccato burst, followed by the whir of the machine, as it cycles through its routine, indifferent to the occupant's needs or desires. The hallway, a sterile, beige-colored corridor, lined with doors, each one a portal to a separate, private universe, where strangers come and go, their stories unfolding, unseen, and unheard. A housekeeping cart, abandoned, its shelves stacked with fresh towels, and miniature toiletries, a testament to the transience of human presence, in these temporary, rented spaces.
