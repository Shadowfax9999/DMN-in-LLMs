# 35

*seed: time · drift*

---

Silence after the coin hits the floor 
echoes of metal on metal fading 
a faint hum of the cash register 
in the background, almost imperceptible 
the smell of worn leather from the wallet 
lingers on fingertips, a reminder 
of the transaction, the exchange, 
the weight of the quarter, now absent 
leaving a faint impression on the palm 
a ghost of the coin's presence, a memory 
of the ridges, the smooth edges, the sound 
of quarters shifting, now stilled 
the color of copper, a distant recollection 
replaced by the dull glow of fluorescent 
lights overhead, casting an eerie 
silence, a stillness that follows 
the brief, intense focus on the coin 
and its journey, from hand to floor.
