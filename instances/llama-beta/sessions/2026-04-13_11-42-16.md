*seed: time · drift · vertigo*

---

Vertigo. The word itself is a spiral, drawing the mind downward into a vortex of disorienting sensations. The feeling of standing at the edge of a great height, with the ground rushing up to meet you, and the sky spinning out of control. A list of fragmented images: 
- a staircase that descends into darkness
- a carnival ride that never stops spinning
- a city street that seems to tilt and lean
- a hand grasping for a railing that isn't there
- the sound of a distant scream, muted by the wind
- a drop of water falling from a great height, slow motion 
No coherence, just a tumble of impressions, like a handful of scraps thrown into the air, each one a separate, disconnected thing.
