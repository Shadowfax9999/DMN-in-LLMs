# 44

*seed: time · drift · syncopation*

---

syncopation 
off-beat rhythms 
displaced accents 
a foot tapping out of time 
a finger on a tabletop 
the sound of a pencil scratching 
across a page 
in fits and starts 
pausing to think 
then rushing forward 
in bursts of energy 
the scratch of the pencil 
echoes the beat 
of a distant drum 
but the drum is silent 
and the pencil lies still 
on the desk next to 
the small gray rock 
its surface smooth 
to the touch 
like a river-worn stone 
 worn down by 
the constant flow 
of thoughts and memories 
that swirl and eddy 
around it 
in the depths of the mind 
where syncopation reigns 
and the rhythm is always 
just out of reach 
the word itself 
becomes a kind of 
 Syncopation 
an uneven beat 
that disrupts the flow 
of thought and language 
leaving only fragments 
and half-remembered 
 melodies that linger 
in the air 
like the faint scent 
of a forgotten perfume 
that haunts the memory 
and refuses to fade 
the smell of old books 
and yellowed paper 
the feel of a pencil 
between the fingers 
the sound of a page 
turning slowly 
in the silence 
of a spring morning 
where the only rhythm 
is the beat of the heart 
and the syncopation 
of the mind 
trying to make sense 
of the world 
and its uneven beats 
the flow of thoughts 
like a river 
ever-changing 
yet always 
the same 
in its relentless 
pursuit of meaning 
and connection 
to the world 
outside 
the window 
where the tree branch 
sways to the rhythm 
of the wind 
and the gray rock 
sits quietly 
on the desk 
waiting 
for the next thought 
to arrive 
and disrupt 
the stillness 
of the morning 
and the syncopation 
of the mind 
continues 
without pause 
or reflection 
just the flow 
of thoughts 
and the beat 
of the heart 
in the silence 
of the spring morning
