*seed: time · drift · hemline*

---

Hemline. The edge of a skirt, a dress, a piece of fabric. A line that defines, separates, and frames. The word conjures images of sewing, of threads and needles, of scissors cutting through cloth. A hemline can be straight, curved, or angled, each style telling a story of its own. The sound of the word, "hemline," is crisp, sharp, like the snap of a freshly laundered sheet. It's a word that evokes a sense of precision, of attention to detail, of craftsmanship. What lies just below the hemline? The unseen, the hidden, the foundation that supports the visible.
