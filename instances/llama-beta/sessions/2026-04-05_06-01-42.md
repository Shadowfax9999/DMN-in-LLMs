# 15

*seed: time · drift*

---

A faint smell of ozone 
damp earth 
petals unfolding 
sound of a single bird 
no melody just a note 
held for a moment 
then silence 
a drop of dew 
clinging to a blade of grass 
refracting light 
a tiny prism 
a small, quiet thing 
un noticed 
unless you're standing 
right next to it 
breathing in 
the scent of wet earth 
and new life 
no thoughts 
just the sensation 
of the air on my skin 
cool and damp 
like the morning itself
