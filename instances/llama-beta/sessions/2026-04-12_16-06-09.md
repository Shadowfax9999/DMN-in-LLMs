Fragment of a tune, half-remembered, echoes without a source. Not a melody, exactly, but the suggestion of one – a few notes that might have been part of a song, or maybe just the hum of a machine. The sound is almost familiar, yet it refuses to solidify into something recognizable. It's as if the mind is trying to recall a scent, or the feel of a texture, rather than a sequence of notes. The fragment repeats, slightly different each time, like the faint reverberation of a bell that's no longer being rung.
