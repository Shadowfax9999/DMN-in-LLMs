# 8

*seed: time · drift · sundial shadow*

---

sundial shadow 
falls across a cracked sidewalk 
the sound of children playing 
in the distance, a ice cream truck 
melody, a faint smell of waffle cones 
and sweet cream, the feel of warm 
asphalt beneath bare feet, 
a forgotten memory of a summer 
afternoon, the taste of a cold 
treat on a hot day, the sensation 
of a sticky finger, a laugh, 
a carefree moment, lost in the 
rhythm of a simple pleasure, 
the world slows down, and for 
a brief instant, everything 
feels right, the sound of the 
ice cream truck fades into the 
distance, leaving behind a 
sense of longing, a nostalgia 
for a time when life was simpler 

the smell of saltwater 
and the sound of seagulls 
the feel of sand between 
toes, the taste of a 
saltwater taffy, 
the sensation of a 
cool ocean breeze 
on a hot summer day, 
a sense of freedom, 
a sense of adventure, 
the world stretches out 
before you, full of 
possibilities, the sound 
of the waves crashing 
against the shore, 
a constant reminder 
of the power and 
beauty of nature 

a small, forgotten 
bookstore, the smell 
of old books and 
dust, the feel of 
pages turning, the 
sound of a clock 
ticking, the sensation 
of being lost in 
a good story, 
the world outside 
fades away, and 
all that's left is 
the words on the 
page, the characters, 
the plot, the 
emotions, the 
journey, the sound 
of a page turning, 
a new chapter 
begins, a new 
adventure unfolds 

the sound of rain 
on the roof, 
the smell of wet 
earth, the feel 
of raindrops on 
skin, the sensation 
of being cozy, 
warm, and safe, 
the world outside 
is wet, and gray, 
but inside, all 
is warm, and bright, 
a cup of hot tea, 
a good book, 
a comfortable 
chair, the sound 
of rain, a lullaby, 
rocking gently, 
back, and forth, 
the world slows down, 
and for a brief 
instant, everything 
feels right
