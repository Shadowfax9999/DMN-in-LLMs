*seed: time · drift · the pattern oil makes in a puddle*

---

the pattern oil makes in a puddle 
raindrop rings 
colors swirling 
a miniature world 
shimmering 
iridescent 
the way it moves 
the way it changes 
reflections of sky and ground 
distorted 
merged 
a dance of light and liquid 
no edges 
no boundaries 
just a messy beauty 
a fleeting thing 
gone with the next step 
or the next rain 
but for now 
it's a tiny universe 
full of wonder 
full of surprise 
a small joy 
in a small place
