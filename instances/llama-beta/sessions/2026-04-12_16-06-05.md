# 62

*seed: time · drift*

---

Soft clink of metal on wood, the sound resonating through a stillness that's almost palpable. The room, with its shelves of old books, seems to hold its breath as the coin comes to rest. Dust motes dance in a faint beam of light, their gentle swirl a reminder that even in stillness, there's movement. The face of the stranger on the coin blurs, becoming one with the dust and the shadows, leaving only the sensation of the coin's edge, where fingerpads once traced the ridges. Silence.
