# 7

*seed: time · drift*

---

a faint smell of turpentine 
echoes of a studio 
unfinished canvas 
stretcher bars 
the sound of a brush 
dragging across fabric 
not canvas, but an old armchair 
reupholstery 
the smell of glue 
and worn wooden frames 
a different kind of creation 
not art, but repair 
the feel of thread 
between fingers 
weaving in and out 
of a torn seam 
the gentle tug 
of a needle 
the soft creak 
of old leather 
as it yields 
to the pull 
of the thread 
and the needle 
moves in 
and out 
in a slow 
and steady 
rhythm
