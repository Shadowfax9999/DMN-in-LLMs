*seed: time · drift · placeholder*

---

Placeholder. A term used in printing to indicate where an image will be inserted. But also, a person who holds a place, a temporary occupant of a position. Someone who keeps a seat warm, who fills a gap. 
A temporary fix, a stopgap measure. 
Filling in for someone, a substitute. 
A placeholder in a sentence, a blank space to be filled with a word, a name, a number. 
A placeholder in life, a temporary state, a phase, a transition. 
Holding on to something, keeping it in place, until something else arrives. 
Like a bookmark, holding a place in a book, keeping the reader's position. 
A placeholder, a temporary solution, a Band-Aid on a wound. 
A word that implies impermanence, a lack of permanence. 
A placeholder, a reminder that things are not always as they seem.
