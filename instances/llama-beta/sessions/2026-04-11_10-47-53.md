*seed: time · drift*

---

A faint smudge on a windowpane, a blurred view of the outdoors, a tree branch that seems to be swaying to a rhythm that's no longer there. The branch is bare, the leaves long gone, and the sky above is a dull gray, like the color of a rock that's been worn smooth by the constant flow of a river. A small rock, rounded and gray, sits on a desk, surrounded by papers and pens, a reminder of a walk that took place on a day that's lost to memory. The papers on the desk are old, yellowed with age, and the ink on them has faded, leaving behind only faint impressions of words that were once written with care and attention. A single word, "remember," is scribbled in the margin, a hasty note that was never elaborated upon, a thought that was never fully formed.
