Grey. The color of the wall, of the crack, of the evening sky outside. A color that's not quite blue, not quite green, but something in between. It's a color that's easy to overlook, to ignore, but it's also a color that's strangely compelling. I find myself thinking about all the things that are grey - the stones on a beach, the feathers of a bird, the ash of a fire. And the sound of the word, "grey", it's a soft sound, a gentle sound, a sound that's easy to whisper.
