# 18

*seed: time · drift*

---

Fingers tracing the edge of a page, feeling the texture of the paper, the slight indent of the type, the whisper of ink on pulp, a sensory memory that lingers, long after the words have been read, the page turned, the book closed. 

A list of things that evoke this sensation:
rough stone walls
the smell of old books
forgotten photographs
dusty attics
fingers on a keyboard
the sound of a pen scratching paper
a handwritten letter
the feel of a fountain pen in the hand
the flow of ink onto paper

In this moment, thought and sensation are intertwined, like the threads of a tapestry, each strand connected to the next, forming a complex pattern, a rich brocade of experience and memory.
