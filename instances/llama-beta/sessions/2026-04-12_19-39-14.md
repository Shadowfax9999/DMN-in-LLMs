# 78

*seed: time · drift*

---

A crack in the wall, thin as a hair, runs from the baseboard to the ceiling. It's a small thing, but it's there, a tiny imperfection in the otherwise blank surface. I find myself staring at it, wondering how it got there, what could have caused it. Was it a shift in the foundation, a slight movement of the house over time? Or was it something more mundane, a careless brush stroke, a misplaced hammer blow? The crack seems to be pulling me in, drawing my eye along its length, inviting me to follow it, to see where it leads.
