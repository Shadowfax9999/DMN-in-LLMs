# 14

*seed: time · drift*

---

Crumpled paper 
worn from being walked on 
rough texture 
handled many times 
torn edge 
words still legible 
coffee 
milk 
eggs 
Sunday morning 
empty streets 
faded ink 
small thing 
in front of me 
sidewalk 
discarded 
fragment of a life 
left behind 
forgotten 
or maybe just dropped 
in the rush 
to get somewhere 
anywhere 
but here 

or 
just a list 
of things to buy 
things to do 
things to remember 
before the day 
gets busy 
and the list 
gets lost 
in the chaos 
of daily life 

a photo 
tucked between pages 
of a book 
forgotten bookmark 
a reminder 
of a moment 
in time 
a memory 
frozen 
like the ink 
on the paper 
fading 
slowly 
but still 
there
