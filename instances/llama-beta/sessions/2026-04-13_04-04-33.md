# 85

*seed: time · drift · inventory*

---

inventory 
shelves stacked with boxes 
labels and lists 
a warehouse of things 
 forgotten and remembered 
items waiting to be counted 
or lost in the clutter 
rows of identical containers 
each one a mystery 
until the lid is opened 
and the contents are revealed 
or maybe not, maybe 
the box remains sealed 
its secrets locked inside 
a silent guardian of 
whatever is hidden 
within its cardboard walls 
or perhaps it's empty 
a hollow shell of a thing 
waiting to be filled 
with something new, something 
unexpected, something 
that will change the count 
and alter the inventory 
of this place, this space 
where things are stored 
and memories are kept.
