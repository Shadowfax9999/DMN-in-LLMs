# 60

*seed: time · drift · sandbagging*

---

sandbagging 
the weight 
an earthy smell 
damp and musty 
overfilled 
seams straining 
a makeshift thing 
used once 
then discarded 
left to deteriorate 
in a forgotten corner 
exposed to the elements 
slowly coming apart 
at the seams 
the sound of sand 
trickling out 
a steady stream 
of tiny grains 
falling, falling 
onto the ground 
pattering against 
the earth below 
a soothing melody 
a lullaby of sand 
and forgotten things
