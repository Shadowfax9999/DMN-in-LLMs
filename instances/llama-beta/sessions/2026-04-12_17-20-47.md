# 73

*seed: time · drift*

---

Echoes in an empty hallway, footsteps fading into silence. A chandelier, its crystals muted, reflecting dim light. The air is stale, with a hint of dust and forgotten memories. A door at the far end, slightly ajar, inviting curiosity. The creak of the floorboards beneath your feet, a gentle warning that you're not alone. Shadows cast by the faint light, like dark tentacles stretching across the walls. 

A faint scent of old books, wafting from a room to the left. The musty smell of pages turned yellow with age, and the whisper of forgotten knowledge. A book lying open on a nearby table, its pages fluttering softly, as if stirred by an unseen breeze. The words on the page, a jumbled mix of forgotten languages, and the feeling of trying to decipher a code that's been lost to time. 

The sound of whispers, a gentle hum, like the quiet buzzing of a harp string. The vibrations of the sound, felt rather than heard, like the quiet thrumming of a cello. A single note, sustained, filling the air with an expectant hush. The silence that follows, a held breath, waiting for the next note to be played. 

A window, high above, filtering the last rays of sunlight. The colors of the glass, muted, yet vibrant, casting a kaleidoscope of hues across the floor. The patterns, intricate, like the delicate threads of a spider's web. A single thread, broken, dangling in the air, swaying gently, like a pendulum marking time.
