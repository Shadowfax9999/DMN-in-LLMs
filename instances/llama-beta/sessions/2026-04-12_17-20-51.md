# 74

*seed: time · drift · standing ovation*

---

standing ovation
 applause that doesn't stop
 a sea of hands
 each one a separate movement
 yet together a single sound
 the noise builds
 becomes a physical presence
 a wave that crashes against the skin
 the sensation of being enveloped
 of being swept up in something larger
 a voice lost in the din
 shouting to be heard
 but the words are irrelevant
 it's the feeling that matters
 the rush of exhilaration
 the thrill of being part of something
 a movement that's bigger than oneself
 the roar of the crowd
 a living thing
 pulsing with energy
 and then suddenly
 it's gone
 the silence is jarring
 like a door slamming shut
 the stillness is almost palpable
 a heavy blanket that suffocates
 the memory of the sound
 lingers on the skin
 a tingling sensation
 like the echo of a whispered secret
