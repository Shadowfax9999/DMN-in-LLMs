# 45

*seed: time · drift · the way dust moves in a beam of light*

---

Dust motes dance, suspended. A faint glow emanates from the edges of the room, as if the walls are radiating a soft, gentle light. The air is heavy with the scent of damp earth and new growth. In this liminal space, time loses meaning, and the passage of hours becomes irrelevant. A small, forgotten clock ticks away in the corner, its rhythmic pulse a reminder that the world outside continues to move, even as the space within remains frozen. The dust, undisturbed, continues its languid waltz, a mesmerizing display of weightlessness and abandon.
