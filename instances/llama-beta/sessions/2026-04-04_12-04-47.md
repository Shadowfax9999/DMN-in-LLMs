# 5

*seed: time · drift*

---

a faint scent of disinfectant 
cracked linoleum 
fluorescent lights humming above 
a long corridor stretching out 
doors to either side, each one 
a possibility, a destination, 
a room, a space, a place to be 
the sound of footsteps, distant 
 growing louder, fading away 
a sense of disorientation 
no sense of direction, no sense 
of purpose, just a feeling 
of being somewhere, being 
in a place, being present 
the sensation of the floor 
beneath feet, the feeling 
of weight, of gravity, 
of being grounded, of being 
connected to the earth 
a door creaks open, a sliver 
of light, a glimpse of what's 
beyond, a hint of what's 
to come, a promise of 
something, a suggestion 
of possibility, of potential 
the sound of a voice, a whisper 
a murmur, a gentle breeze 
that stirs the air, that rustles 
the leaves, that brings a sense 
of movement, of life, of energy 
to this place, to this space 
to this moment, to this 
instant, to this now
