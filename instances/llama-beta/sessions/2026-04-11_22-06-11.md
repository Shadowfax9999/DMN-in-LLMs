*seed: time · drift · the feeling of reaching into a coat you haven't worn since last year*

---

the lining is worn, threadsbare in places, and the smell of last winter's coat closet clings to it, a mix of dampness and forgotten air, like the scent of a basement that's been closed up for months, musty and stale, yet somehow comforting, a familiar smell that transports me to a different time, a different season, when the world outside was colder, darker, and the only warmth came from the layers of clothing wrapped around me, the feeling of zipping up the coat, the sound of the zipper's teeth meshing together, a small, satisfying click that signals the coat is closed, sealed, a barrier between me and the outside world, and the weight of the coat, the heaviness of it, like a physical presence that presses against my shoulders, a reminder of the world's chill, its indifference, and the softness of the collar, worn smooth by countless wearings, a gentle caress against my neck, a small comfort in the cold.
