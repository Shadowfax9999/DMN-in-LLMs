# 11

*seed: time · drift*

---

Silence of a paused elevator. The stillness is almost palpable, a physical presence that fills the small space. The doors, once slid open with a whoosh, now stand frozen, a metal barrier between the inside and the outside. The air is stale, heavy with the scent of worn carpet and polished steel. A faint hum, a reminder of the machinery that lurks behind the scenes, whispers through the silence. In this moment, time seems to bend, stretching out the seconds into an endless expanse. The elevator, a threshold between worlds, hangs suspended, a temporary refuge from the hustle and bustle of the world outside.
