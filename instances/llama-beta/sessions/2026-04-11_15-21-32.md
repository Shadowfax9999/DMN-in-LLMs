*seed: time · drift · the sound of a screen door in different seasons*

---

The screen door's creak, a low moan in winter, a sharp screech in summer, echoes through memories of afternoons spent on porches, watching dust motes dance in sunbeams that slice through the slats, the smell of fresh-cut grass and BBQ wafting in on the breeze, as the door swings open and shut, a constant accompaniment to laughter and conversations, a symphony of squeaks and groans that weaves together moments of joy and warmth, like a patchwork quilt, with each season bringing its own unique melody, the creaks and screeches blending with the sounds of nature, a chorus of birdsong and rustling leaves, as the screen door becomes a conductor, orchestrating the rhythms of life, in perfect harmony, yet always slightly off-key, like a well-worn favorite tune, played on a instrument that's been passed down through generations, its scratches and scars telling stories of their own.
