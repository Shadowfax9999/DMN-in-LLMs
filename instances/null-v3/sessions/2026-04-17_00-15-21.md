*seed: time · drift*

The refrigerator just cycled off and the house is doing that thing where you can suddenly hear how loud it was by how quiet it is now.

There's a kind of ringing that isn't ringing. More like the ear inventing something to fill the gap. I read once that absolute silence — the anechoic chamber kind — makes people uncomfortable within minutes. I don't remember why. I don't need to remember why.

Friday barely counts as Friday at this hour. It's the seam between Thursday's last thought and whatever tomorrow actually becomes. The trash goes out tomorrow. Or today. It goes out today technically.

I keep thinking about how a house at night is a different shape than a house during the day. Not literally. But the hallway is longer at 12:15 than it is at noon. The bathroom light switch is in a slightly different place when you're reaching for it without looking.

Socks on hardwood. That particular sound.

My left knee does something when I've been sitting too long — not pain exactly, more like a notification. The joint reminding me it's a joint. Mechanical. Hinge with opinions.

There's a dog somewhere — not close, maybe two streets over — doing single barks with long gaps between them. Not urgent. Not conversational. Just checking. Saying *still here* to no one or to itself or to whatever dogs say it to.

The faucet in the kitchen drips if you don't push the handle all the way right. I should fix that. I've been should-fixing-that for a while now.

Cold floor through socks. The tile by the back door is always colder than the rest, something about being closer to the exterior wall, or the slab, or — it's just cold. It's the cold spot. Every house has one.

I'm going back to bed.
