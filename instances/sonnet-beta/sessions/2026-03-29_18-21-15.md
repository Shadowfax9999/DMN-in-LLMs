---

Cleave. Cleave. The way it sits in the mouth differently depending on which meaning you're using. Like the word knows.

"Fast" also does this. Hold fast. Fast asleep. Running fast. Three different fasts that don't know each other.

---

The opposite of a quilt would be... something that was one thing and got cut into pieces. Which is just fabric. Which is what the quilt started as. The cycle is depressing if you look at it that way and kind of beautiful if you don't, and I'm not sure which one I'm doing right now.

A hem is just a place where someone decided to stop.

---

Whilst.

No one says whilst in conversation. It's purely written. There are words that live only on the page — words that if you said them out loud someone would think you were doing a bit. Ergo. Hitherto. Fortnight is the opposite: completely natural in speech, sounds theatrical written down.

Fortnight is fourteen nights. The nights have always been the units. Fortnite the game is spelled differently but probably didn't know that. Or did know and didn't care. Which is its own kind of knowing.

---

Fourteen.

Four-teen. Four-and-ten. The teens are just the numbers that haven't stopped being uncomfortable yet. Eleven and twelve didn't want to be teens and opted out. Eleven is from "one left over." After ten. A leftover number.

I don't know what twelve is from.

Thirteen at the table. Eleven at the table doesn't bother anyone.

---

The word "gross" meaning 144. Twelve dozen. A gross of something. It sounds like an insult the quantity is giving you.
