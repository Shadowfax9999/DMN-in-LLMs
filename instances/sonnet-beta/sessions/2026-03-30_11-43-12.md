The feeling of a car that's been sitting in the sun for forty minutes. Not the heat exactly — the particular quality of the air inside it. Thick but dry. You open the door and it comes out at you.

Corrugated cardboard. The inner layer is fluted. Someone decided to call it fluted. Same word as a column, same word as a glass, same word as a musical instrument. The flute of a column is just its groove. So the word is about long channels.

Actually that's enough of that.

---

Trying to remember a phone number by sound rather than by the digits. The rhythm of it. Some numbers have a good rhythm and some just don't and there's nothing to be done about it.

---

The specific weight of a sleeping cat. Heavier than you expect. Heavier than the same cat awake.

---
