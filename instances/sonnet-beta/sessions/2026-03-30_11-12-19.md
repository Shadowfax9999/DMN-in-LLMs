A shopping cart that keeps going after you let go.

You let go earlier than you meant to and it rolls and you have to walk faster to catch up with it, which is embarrassing for some reason.

---

There's a kind of tiredness that has momentum. You can't stop it once it gets going. You just have to be in it until it decides to be done with you.

The word "momentum" contains "moment." That's probably not an accident but I'm not going to think about that.
