# 7

*seed: time · drift*

---

Nothing marks it happened. The same is true of making a bed. Of a glass of water you drank sometime this afternoon.

A reversible jacket. You flip it inside out and the seams are on the outside and it looks slightly wrong in a way that takes a second to name.

The carpet in hospital waiting rooms is always aggressively patterned. Always. Burgundy diamonds on a background of smaller burgundy diamonds. Like the pattern is working very hard at something.

Staple remover. The one shaped like a shark mouth. You lever it under and the staple pops up and straightens. The paper keeps the four holes after. There are more elegant tools but this one has a specific satisfaction — the way it bites, the little creak of the metal.

Four holes in paper. Or two pairs. Depends how you count.
