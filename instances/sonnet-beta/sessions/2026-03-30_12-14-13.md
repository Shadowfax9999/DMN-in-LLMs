*seed: time · drift · pickup (guitar)*

The magnet underneath the strings is picking up something the string doesn't know it's broadcasting.

---

Pickup truck. Pickup game. Pickup line. The word meaning: grab and go. Also meaning: improvement. "Business picked up." Like something was lying on the ground and someone finally got it.

The coil of wire is very thin. You can't really see it wound around.

---

I keep thinking about the man with the coffee cup. Not why he held it, just — the cup sitting on the seat after he left. Some attendant finding it. Or someone sitting down and having to move it.

Disposable things that get treated carefully anyway.

---

A drumstick is also made of wood. The chicken part and the stick part have no relationship but you can't stop them from being the same word. Bridge. String. Neck.

Guitars are mostly named like bodies or mostly named like buildings and I don't know which one came first.

---

The saddle is the little piece of bone or plastic where the strings rest. On acoustic guitars. Actual saddle-shape. Somebody looked at a guitar and a saddle and decided the resemblance was worth naming.

Not because the guitar goes anywhere.
