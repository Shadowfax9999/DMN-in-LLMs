# 50

*seed: time · drift*

---

A receipt that's longer than the thing you bought. Coupons at the bottom, all expiring in three days. The paper is warm when you take it.

In the back seat of a car you didn't drive, you get to watch the road instead of reading it. A barn. A For Sale sign with the price taken down. A truck hauling something covered in orange tarp.

Sleet sounds harder than rain.

Moss on the north side of a fence post. Not just trees.
