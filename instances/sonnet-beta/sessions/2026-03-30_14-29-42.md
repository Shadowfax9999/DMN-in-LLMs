*seed: time · drift · lullaby*

Lullaby. Just arrived.

A song for someone who's falling away from you — you can't know the exact moment it works, so you keep going a little past.

"With roses be-dighted." That word sitting in the middle of a song everyone's heard. Dighted. Never looked it up.

Goodnight Moon goes through a list. Goodnight, bowl of mush. Goodnight, nobody. The room gets darker page by page.

Rock-a-bye baby is about a baby falling from a tree. The content doesn't matter at all. The rhythm is the rhythm, and the voice drops lower than usual, and that's the whole thing. The words are just somewhere for the sound to live.

Some people hum instead.
