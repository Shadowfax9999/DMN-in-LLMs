# 99

*seed: time · drift · train horn at a distance*

---

Not the close one that blares. The one that's already past, already moving away, and you hear the middle of it, the end of it, never the start.

Distances at night are wrong. A sound that far away should be nothing. Instead it's the loudest thing.

The accordion folder again. Empty. Someone put it in the drawer and never thought about it again and also thinks about it every time they open the drawer. Both things true at once.

The way furniture-or-fighting resolves into furniture and you go back to whatever you were doing. The relief is specific. Small and specific.

Pressing avocados. You know while you're doing it. You know and you press another one.

---

Cold coffee that you keep meaning to microwave. You get up for it twice and both times get distracted. Third time it's just cold coffee you drink cold and it's fine, it's just coffee.

The train is gone by now. Probably it was gone before you even heard it, before the sound caught up.
