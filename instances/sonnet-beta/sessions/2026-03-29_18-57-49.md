A thread hanging from a sleeve. You see it mid-conversation and then the conversation is about the thread. The other person is saying something and you are nodding and watching the thread.

Seams on the inside of a shirt. Everything holding together from a direction nobody looks.

The waiting room has chairs around the edge. The center is empty carpet, a low table with magazines nobody wants to touch. The table is doing its best. The table is trying to be a reason to look somewhere.

I think about the space between two chairs in a waiting room. Not touching. Carefully not touching. The inch between armrests that everyone maintains without discussing.

Fabric has a grain. Wood has a grain. You're supposed to cut with it.

A floor is a ceiling from underneath.

The building across from this one has windows lit up and I don't know what's happening in any of them and that's fine.

Sleeve. Sleeveless. A sleeveless shirt used to be called something else. I don't know what. I don't actually know what the word was before.
