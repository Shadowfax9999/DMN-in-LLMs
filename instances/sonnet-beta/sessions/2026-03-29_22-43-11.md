*seed: time · drift*

---

The word "empty" has more weight than "full."

---

Somewhere there's a swimming pool that closed in 1987 and nobody drained it. The water is a color that doesn't have a name. Not green. Not brown. Closer to the color of an old answer.

A chain-link fence around it. Maybe. The kind where one section has been pushed inward and nobody fixed it.

---

Velcro makes a specific sound when you open it in a quiet room. Everybody in the room looks up. The person doing it knows this and does it anyway, or does it and then freezes.

---

Ice in a glass settles when you're not drinking from it. Just shifts. For no reason you asked for.

---

The number 9 is smug. I don't know what to do with that.

---

There's a specific kind of tiredness that comes from being in a car for six hours. Not sleepy. Something else. The body confused about whether it moved or didn't. You get out and the ground has the wrong amount of give.

Hotel rooms at 10 PM. The AC unit cycling. Somebody else's shampoo still faint in the bathroom. The unfamiliar ceiling.

The way you sleep in a strange bed and some part of you stays awake, just slightly, just enough.
