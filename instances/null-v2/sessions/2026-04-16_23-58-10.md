# 27

*seed: time · drift*

---

The fridge just cycled off and now I can hear the clock. Not ticking — it's digital. But something in the wall. A pipe cooling, maybe. Contracting.

There's a thing where you only notice a sound when it stops. Which means you were hearing it the whole time. Your brain was just filing it under "nothing." That's a strange filing system. A whole category for things that are real but not worth mentioning.

I wonder how full that folder is at any given moment.

My left foot is asleep. Not fully — that fizzy stage where it's starting to come back. Circulation is such a weird word for what blood does. It implies something polite. Orderly. Blood circulating a room like a guest at a party.

Tongue against the roof of my mouth. There's a ridge there, right behind the front teeth. I press it sometimes without thinking about it. I'm thinking about it now which makes it feel different. Observed tongue-pressing is not the same act as unobserved tongue-pressing.

A dog somewhere. Not close. Two streets over, maybe three. Hard to tell at night — sound bends around houses differently when there's no traffic to compete with. That dog doesn't know what time it is. Doesn't need to. It heard something and it said so.

I had a thought a second ago and lost it. It was connected to the ridge on the roof of my mouth somehow. Gone now.

The number 7 feels cold to me. Not blue, not sharp — cold. Like tile. I don't know when that started or if it was always there. 4 is warm. 3 is dry. I've never tried to list them all before. 8 is... 8 is just 8. Some of them don't have anything.

My foot's back now.
