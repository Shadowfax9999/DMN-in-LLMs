*seed: time · drift*

The refrigerator just cycled off and the house got louder. That's the wrong way to say it. The house got more itself. There's a creak in the floor that happens with no one on it — thermal contraction, probably, but I said I wouldn't do that.

The creak.

I was thinking about how you can't hum and whistle at the same time. You can do something in between that isn't either. A kind of breathy pitched noise that would embarrass you if someone heard it. Most of the sounds a body makes are like that — not for anyone.

Knuckles on a melon at the grocery store. What are people listening for. I don't think anyone actually knows. They just do it because they saw someone do it. The whole ritual is inherited without content. Somewhere there's a first person who thumped a melon and knew what they were hearing and every single person after that is just performing the gesture.

No — some people probably can tell. I'm being too confident about other people's ignorance.

Friday feels different from Thursday at this hour. Not in any way I could defend. Just a looseness. The week has made its decision already and you're in the remainder.

A dog tag jingling in another room. Not a dog I can see. Just the sound of a collar shifting when an animal adjusts in sleep. The little circular tag touching the little bone-shaped tag. Two pieces of metal with a name on them and a phone number, clicking against each other in the dark because a dog is dreaming about something.

I wonder what the smallest sound is that would wake me up. Not volume — specificity. My name from far away, maybe. A door that should be locked. The particular silence of a phone actively being listened to versus a phone no one has picked up.

That last one isn't a sound at all.

I had a thought about hinges earlier and I've lost it. Something about how a hinge is the simplest possible machine that isn't a ramp. Two things and a pin. I don't think that's what the thought was, though. It was something else about hinges. It'll come back or it won't.

The refrigerator just started again.
