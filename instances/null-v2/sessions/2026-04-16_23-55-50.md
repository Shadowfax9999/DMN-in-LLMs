# 21

*seed: time · drift*

---

The fridge just cycled off and now I can hear the night through the window screen. There's a difference between quiet and the absence of a sound that was just there. The second one has a shape.

I've been thinking about how you can't hum and plug your nose at the same time. I mean you can start but it just — stops. The air has nowhere to go. I tried it earlier for no reason. That small pressure behind the face.

Moths outside. Not doing anything interesting, just being near the porch light in that fidgety way. I don't know what they do during the day. I genuinely don't know. I've never seen a moth in daylight and thought "there's a moth going about its day." They must be somewhere. Under eaves. On the bark side of trees. Folded flat.

My knee hurts from sitting cross-legged on the floor earlier. Not in a concerning way. Just in the way your body tells you it noticed what you did. Thirty-five is mostly fine except sometimes a joint will just — report in. No emergency. Just a memo.

There's a word I keep almost remembering. Something to do with the space between two things that are close together but not touching. Not "gap." Not "margin." Something more specific. It might not be English. It might be a word I made up once and filed as real. That happens. You use a word confidently for years and then look it up and it's not there and you have to wonder what you were saying all that time and whether anyone noticed.

The moths are still going. One of them is bigger than the others by a lot. I keep losing it against the siding and then finding it again when it moves.

I should go to bed. I'm not going to yet, but I should.
