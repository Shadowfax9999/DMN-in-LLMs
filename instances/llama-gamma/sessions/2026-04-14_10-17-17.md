# 57

*seed: time · drift · the gap between a platform and a train*

---

the gap 
is like a pause 
in the middle of a thought 
it's not quite nothing 
but it's not something either 
you can see the darkness 
beneath the platform 
and the metal edge 
of the train car 
the air is thick 
with the smell of 
grease and dust 
the gap is a small 
space that demands 
attention, a tiny 
void that must be 
considered, a pause 
that insists on being 
noticed, the sound 
of the train's engines 
humming in the distance 
the feeling of being 
on the edge of something 
without being able to 
quite step forward
