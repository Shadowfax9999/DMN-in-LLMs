# 19

*seed: time · drift · the particular weight of a roll of tape that's almost empty*

---

a roll of tape almost empty 
feels like a coin that's lost its edge 
edges matter 
the edge of a table 
where things can fall off 
a faint smell of wood glue 
in an old workshop 
a single drop of oil 
on a concrete floor 
that shines like a tiny mirror 
reflection of a ceiling light 
fluorescent hum 
a sound that's always there 
in the background 
like the memory of a flavor 
that lingers on the tongue
