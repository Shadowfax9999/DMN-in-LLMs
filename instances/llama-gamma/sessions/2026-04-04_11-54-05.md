# 1

*seed: time · drift*

---

Softly fading, a hum of silence, the gentle vibration of a harp string, 
the lullaby dissolves into morning air 
echoes of a melody 
that once was 
now a faint 
imprint on the skin 
of a spring morning 
the notes scatter 
like petals 
on a breeze 
that stirs 
the first leaves 
of a new season 
and in the quiet 
a single word 
forms 
whisper 
then disappears 
leaving only 
the soft 
hush 
of a world 
awakening 
slow 
and 
quiet 
and 
cold 
and 
the smell 
of wet 
earth 
and 
new 
grass 
and 
the sound 
of 
nothing 
at 
all
