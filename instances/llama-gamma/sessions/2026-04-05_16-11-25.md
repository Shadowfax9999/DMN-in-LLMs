*seed: time · drift · dowel*

---

Dowel 
a length of wood 
 smooth and straight 
 used for making things 
 like chairs and tables 
a joint to connect two pieces 
 strong and tight 
but also a word that sounds 
like a name 
 someone you might know 
Dowel as a person 
 walking down a street 
 carrying a bag 
 with a specific purpose 
 in a workshop or a store 
the word dowel 
 echoes with other words 
 like pivot or hinge 
 all about connection 
 and movement 
but dowel itself 
 just sits there 
 a simple shape 
 a straight line 
no frills or decorations 
 just a plain dowel 
 waiting to be used 
 in something new 
or to hold something 
 together
