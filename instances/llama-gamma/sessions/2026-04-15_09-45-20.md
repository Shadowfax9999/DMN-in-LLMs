*seed: time · drift · interval*

Interval. A pause, a break, a space between things. Silence between notes. The stillness between breaths. A room with no furniture, just empty space. A highway rest stop, the only sound the hum of the wind. The smell of diesel fuel and asphalt. A vending machine that only dispenses coffee, the hissing sound of the coffee flowing into a cup. 

A list:
coffee stains on a shirt
a forgotten umbrella
a phone with no signal
a book with no title
a door with no handle

the taste of stale air 
the feeling of being suspended 
the sound of nothing 
the sight of a blank wall 
the smell of old books 

A single thought: what is the shape of an interval?
