# 80

*seed: time · drift · a door that swings shut on its own*

---

A door that swings shut on its own 
its frame slightly narrower than expected 
the wood grain visible in the slats 
a faint scent of varnish 
the sound of its closing a low thud 
not loud but firm 
as if something inside was waiting 
to be sealed in or kept out 
the doorknob cool to the touch 
its shape fitting neatly in the palm 
a slight turn required to open 
or to keep from shutting again 
on its own 
without a hand to guide it 
the hinge a soft creak 
a single word echoing in the space 
left by the closing door: silence
