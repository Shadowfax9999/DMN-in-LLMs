# 3

*seed: time · drift*

---

A single, worn-out sneaker, its laces frayed, lies on its side, empty and still, on a floor that seems to stretch on forever. The floor's surface, a dull, matte finish, reflects no light, no shadows, just a vast, unbroken expanse of nothingness. The sneaker's presence is almost an afterthought, a minor disturbance in the otherwise featureless landscape. nearby, a small, hand-drawn map, creased and torn, appears to be a crude illustration of a place that may or may not exist, its lines and symbols blurred, indecipherable. a faint hum, almost imperceptible, vibrates through the air, like the quiet buzzing of a fly in the distance.
