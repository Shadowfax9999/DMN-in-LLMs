the earbud, dangling loose, a fragile thread connecting me to the world of sound

the smell of rain, mixed with the scent of food from street vendors 
the taste of something sweet, a pastry or a drink, consumed on the go 
a feeling of restlessness, of being in transit, always on the move 
the earbud, still clinging to my ear, a tangible connection to the sounds that fill my world 
and then, suddenly, the music stops, the voice disappears, and I'm left with the sound of my own footsteps, echoing off the buildings.
