Squinting makes my eyes water, and I blink, and for a moment, I'm thinking about the way eyelids feel, like thin, fragile sheets of skin, and how they can be so sensitive, but also how they can get dry and scratchy, like the pages of those old newspapers, and that makes me think of the sound of pages turning, a soft, rustling sound, like leaves blowing in the wind, or the sound of a small animal scurrying through dry underbrush.
