Voicemail. The sound of a disembodied voice, a message left in the void. A list of fragments:
- a forgotten password
- the echo of a ringing phone
- a message that starts with "hello?" 
- a pause, a hesitation
- the click of a hang-up
- a phone number scribbled on a napkin
- the smell of old paper
- a recording that says "the person you are trying to reach is not available"
- the hum of a machine
- a room with no windows
- the feeling of being left behind
