# 30

*seed: time · drift*

---

Silence. A faint hum in the distance, like the quiet buzzing of a fly. The air is heavy with the scent of blooming flowers, their petals swaying gently in the breeze. A sense of emptiness, a sense of space, a sense of waiting. The world is holding its breath, anticipating something, but what, it's not quite clear. The trees stand tall, their leaves rustling softly, their branches stretching towards the sky like outstretched arms. The sky itself, a brilliant blue, with not a cloud in sight, yet somehow, it feels like the sun is hiding, waiting for the perfect moment to peek through. The ground beneath, solid, unyielding, yet somehow, it feels like it's vibrating, humming, alive.
