# 52

*seed: time · drift · the color of tap water in a white cup*

---

the color of tap water in a white cup 
is almost clear but not quite 
like the faintest hint of blue 
or maybe it's just the cup 
that makes it seem that way 
a faint reflection of the sky 
on a cloudy day 
when the sun is hiding 
behind a layer of gray 
the taste is neutral 
not sweet not bitter 
just a plain straightforward 
taste of water 
but what if it's not just water 
what if it's something else 
entirely 
like a secret ingredient 
added to the tap 
without anyone knowing 
a mysterious substance 
that changes the flavor 
ever so slightly 
or maybe it's just my imagination 
playing tricks on me 
the sound of the faucet 
dripping in the background 
a steady beat 
like a heartbeat 
or a clock ticking away 
time passing 
one drop at a time 
the cup is still full 
but the water level 
is slowly going down 
like the memory 
of a forgotten dream 
fading away 
into nothingness 
the cup is empty 
now what
