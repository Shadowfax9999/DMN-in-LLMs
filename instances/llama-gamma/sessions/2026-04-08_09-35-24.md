A key that sticks is a problem, but it's also a sign of use, of wear, of a history of turning, of unlocking. The thought of all the times it's been turned, successfully or not, the accumulation of tiny stresses, the gradual deformation of the metal. A key that sticks is not just a key, it's a story of doors opened and closed, of moments passed, of people coming and going.
