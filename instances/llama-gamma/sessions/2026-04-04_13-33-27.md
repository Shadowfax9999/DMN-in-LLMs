# 9

*seed: time · drift*

---

A slow flutter then stillness, 
silence of a stopped pendulum, 
emptiness of a just-vacated chair, 
a single, forgotten glove 
on a cold bench, 
frost on a window, 
a hairline crack, 
the smell of a damp cellar, 
peeling paint on a doorframe, 
a door that no longer fits 
its frame, 
the sound of a key 
turning in a lock, 
the creak of a floorboard, 
footsteps that have stopped 
but the vibration remains, 
a hum that lingers, 
the absence of a sound 
that was once constant, 
a radio dial 
between stations, 
static and white noise, 
the quiet of a paused 
video, 
the stillness of a 
photograph, 
a moment frozen, 
time suspended.
