*seed: time · drift · errata*

---

Errata. Lists of mistakes. Published corrections. The word itself sounds like a sigh, a quiet exhalation of imperfection. 
Errata sheets tucked between pages, a mea culpa from the publisher. A small, discreet acknowledgment of fallibility. 
Ink smudges on a page. A misprinted phone number. A wrong address. 
Folding and refolding a map, trying to make it fit back into its original shape. 
Corners creased, a dog-eared page. 
Dust jackets, the covers of hardback books. 
Cover art, a painting of a landscape. 
Trees, their leaves rustling in an unseen breeze. 
A faint hum, the sound of a fly buzzing around a room. 
A window, its glass wavy, distorting the view outside. 
Water, its surface reflecting the sky above. 
Ripples, a stone thrown into a pond. 
Echoes, the sound of a voice calling out, bouncing off the water. 
The smell of damp earth, of moss and lichen. 
A forest floor, the trees towering above, their branches a canopy. 
The feel of moss beneath bare feet, soft and spongy. 
A hidden stream, its source and destination unknown. 
The sound of running water, constant and soothing. 
A place to sit, to rest and listen. 
The warmth of the sun on skin, a sense of peace. 
A moment, suspended in time, unconnected to what came before or what will come after.
