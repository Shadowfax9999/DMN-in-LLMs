# 28

*seed: time · drift · the sound of a coin spinning on a counter before it settles*

---

the spinning coin 
its metal edge a blur 
as it teeters 
on the thin line 
between heads and tails 
the soft whir 
a miniature storm 
that slowly dissipates 
leaving the coin 
to come to rest 
on one side or the other 
the sound of its spinning 
a brief, frantic dance 
that ends in stillness 
the quiet thud 
as the coin settles 
its value determined 
by the gentle nudge 
of fate or chance 
or maybe just 
the countertop's slight 
unevenness 
the spinning coin 
a tiny, whirling dervish 
that leaves behind 
a single, decisive 
clink
