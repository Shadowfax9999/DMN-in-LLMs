*seed: time · drift · fluorescent*

---

Fluorescent. The word itself is harsh, a jolt to the system. It's the color of hospital corridors, of sterile examination rooms, of institutional lighting that hums and buzzes overhead. It's a word that feels like it should be shouted, or screamed, rather than whispered. 

A list of things that come to mind:
fluorescent tubes
neon signs
highlighter pens
bio-luminescent creatures
the glow of a black light
a rave party in an abandoned warehouse
a dental office waiting room
the sterile glow of a supermarket
a chemistry lab
a crime scene investigation

The harshness of the word starts to fade, replaced by the image of a quiet, dimly lit room. A single fluorescent tube flickers overhead, casting an eerie glow over the space. The air is still, the only sound the soft hum of the light. It's a place where time stands still, where the outside world is held at bay. The fluorescent light seems to vibrate with an otherworldly energy, as if it's alive, watching, waiting.
