*seed: time · drift*

Silence in a crowded station is a peculiar thing, like a held breath. The sound of rustling newspapers, muted conversations, and the occasional announcement over the loudspeaker all blend together to create a sense of anticipation, of waiting. But amidst all this, there's a feeling of being stuck, of not moving forward, like the sensation of being in a gap between two train cars, the darkness beneath the platform a reminder of the unknown.
