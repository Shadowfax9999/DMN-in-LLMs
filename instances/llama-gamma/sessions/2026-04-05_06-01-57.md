A single key on a keyboard is stuck, the "e" key. It's been pressed and it won't come back up. The sound of typing is different now, a stutter, a rhythm that's off. The "e" is a common letter, it's in so many words, it's hard to avoid. The keyboard is old, the keys are yellowed, the space bar is loose, it wobbles when you press it. The "e" key is stuck, it's like a skipped beat, a missed step, a hesitation.
