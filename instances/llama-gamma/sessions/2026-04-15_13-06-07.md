Accent, the emphasis on a particular note or beat, a way of adding nuance and depth to a melody. Like the scratch of a finger on paper, it's a subtle variation that can change the entire character of a piece. The word itself has a certain rhythm, a certain emphasis on the first syllable, AC-cent, that makes it stand out.

A feeling of being in a room with a piano, the keys yellowed with age, the sound of music floating through the air, a sense of sadness, of loss, of something that can never be recaptured.
