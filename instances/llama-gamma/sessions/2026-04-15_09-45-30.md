*seed: time · drift · hitchhiker*

Hitchhiker. The thumb raised, the side of the road, the wait. A car passes, doesn't stop. Another car passes, doesn't stop. The sun beats down. A backpack, worn and faded, slung over a shoulder. A sign, handwritten, with a destination. The sound of tires on asphalt, a constant hum. 

A list of what's in the backpack:
- a water bottle
- a map
- a first-aid kit
- a pocket knife
- a book
- a jacket

The smell of sweat and leather. The feel of the backpack straps digging into shoulders. The weight of uncertainty. 
A question: what's the destination on the sign? 
A fragment of a memory: a highway patrol car, a warning, a fine. 
A sensation: the rough texture of the roadside, the gentle slope of the shoulder.
