*seed: time · drift · lawnmower two yards over*

Lawnmower. The low drone blends with the birds' chirping. I can't tell if it's comforting or just noise. --- Sunlight catches on dust motes, floating. They seem to dance, or maybe it's just the air trembling. --- A loose thread on a sweater sleeve, inviting an absent-minded tug. --- The smell of fresh-cut grass seeps in, mingling with the lingering scent of rain. --- The light through the leaves, dappled patterns shifting as the breeze nudges the branches. --- The quiet scratch of a pencil against paper. A line, a curve, a world sketched in graphite. --- The slow trickle of a leaky faucet, a rhythmic soundtrack. Drip. Drip. Drip. --- A spider weaving its web in the corner of the window, intricate architecture spun from silk.
