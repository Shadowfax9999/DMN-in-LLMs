# 32

*seed: time · drift*

---

Hands, the lines crisscrossing like paths on a map. Map, a folded paper tucked into the glove compartment. Compartment, a small space containing secrets. Secrets, whispered in the dark, barely audible. Audible, the rustle of leaves in a gentle breeze. Breeze, a reminder of the sea, the salt in the air. Air, filled with the scent of blooming lilacs. Lilacs, purple petals that softens the edges of spring. Spring, a season of renewal, tentative and bold.

Bold, the color of a sunrise splitting the horizon. Horizon, where the sky meets the earth, a boundary without lines. Lines, drawn in the sand, washed away by the tide. Tide, pulling the ocean in and out like breath. Breath, a rhythmic, unnoticed companion until it stops. Stops, a moment of silence in the middle of a song. Song, a melody worn into the grooves of an old record. Record, capturing time in spirals of sound.

Sound, the hum of a refrigerator breaking the stillness. Stillness, the deep pause between heartbeats. Heartbeats, a reminder of life persistent and fragile. Fragile, the thin layer of ice over a puddle in winter. Winter, the world blanketed in quiet, waiting for the thaw. Thaw, the drip of melting snow heralding spring again. 

Again, patterns repeating in nature and thought. Thought, like a river's flow—constant, unhurried.
