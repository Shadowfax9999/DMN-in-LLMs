Depth of field. The focus falls on one thing while all else blurs, a lens choosing its subject, a mind deciding what's important in a moment. What remains unseen, out-of-focus, is still there, waiting. Depth cues. A way to separate the near from far.  

---

The scent of fresh-cut grass, thick and green in the air, filling the lungs with the sensation of summer. A string of pearls, light catching on the surfaces, each pearl holding its own tiny reflection of the world. --- The turning of a page, fingers brushing the paper, the subtle texture under fingertips. --- A gust of wind carrying a hint of rain, anticipation in the air.  

---

Tunnels under the city. Hidden pathways, unseen arteries through which life moves. --- A clock ticking, marking time, steady and relentless. --- The creak of a floorboard in an empty house, the sound echoing solitude. --- A child's laughter, bright and effervescent, music in its purest form.  

---

A cloud shaped like a ship sailing across the sky. The sky itself a vast ocean of possibility.
