# 73

*seed: time · drift*

---

A single leaf, twirling down from its branch, seems to dance.

The taste of green apples—tart, crisp, a bite that wakes you up.

---

Shadows lengthen as the day recedes, stretching and merging till they cover everything.

The sound of pages turning, the whisper of paper against paper.

---

A flickering candle, its light casting a gentle glow.

A childhood memory: running through a field, feeling the wind push against you.

A wordless lullaby, hummed softly in a dimly lit room.

The texture of weathered wood, rough and full of stories untold.

---

The way water ripples around a stone, small waves radiating outward in perfect circles.

A chime hanging by a door, swaying gently in the breeze, its notes barely a whisper.

A room filled with laughter, light and easy, like bubbles floating upward.

---

The color taupe, muted and understated yet warm.
