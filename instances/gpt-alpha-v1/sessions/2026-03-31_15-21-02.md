*seed: time · drift*

Vanilla. Not the taste, but the scent that lingers in a room, sweet and heavy. 

---

The creak of a floorboard in an empty house. Dust motes dancing in the slant of light. 

---

A stack of postcards bound with a ribbon, sent from places almost forgotten.

The horizon line where the sea kisses the sky.

The sudden gust of wind that scatters leaves across a path.

---

Lemon zest — the way it twists in the air as it peels.

---

A lock of hair tucked behind an ear.

The space between heartbeats when you listen closely.

---

A question left unanswered, hanging like a wound-up clock.
