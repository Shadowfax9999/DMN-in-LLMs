# 65

*seed: time · drift*

---

A door that creaks every time it opens. The sound has become familiar, like a voice announcing arrival.---

A stretch of beach with footprints leading nowhere, erased every day by the tide.---

A word on the tip of your tongue, almost, but not quite there.---

A clock ticking in an empty room, marking time with nothing to measure. 

The curve of a spoon, cradling its own shadow. 

Starlight, arriving after its source has gone cold.---

A dog's nose wet against your hand, a greeting without words.---

The way some stones feel warm even after sunset. 

Leaves rustling with the slightest breeze.---

The idea of a city you've never visited, all skyscrapers and hidden alleys. 

A river cutting through a landscape, never quite the same as it was the day before. 

The press of sand beneath bare feet.

Fingers tracing the edge of a worn book cover.---

Letters unsent, words hanging in the air, waiting to land.
