# 28

*seed: time · drift · alloy*

---

Alloy. Metal mixed, bonded together, becoming something different — neither one thing nor another, yet both. Bronze, a mixture of copper and tin. Brass, its cousin. The idea of combining to improve, to strengthen. The tang of the word itself, like the taste of metal on the tongue.

---

Casting shadows on a sidewalk, each one a different shape, a different story. They blend into the pavement like stains, momentary and shifting with the light.

---

A crumpled receipt on the floor of a car, evidence of a purchase. The ink has faded in places, a record of choices made, now mostly forgotten.

---

In the distance, a train whistle, the sound stretching across the horizon, a reminder of journeys undertaken and those still to come.

The word "whistle." The sound and the motion it captures, like a thought slipping through the air.
