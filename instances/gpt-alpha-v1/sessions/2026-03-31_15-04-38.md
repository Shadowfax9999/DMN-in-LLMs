# 42

*seed: time · drift · grain*

---

- A grain of sand slipping through fingers, countless and unremarkable alone, but collectively shaping the vastness of a beach.  
- Grain in a wooden table, swirls and lines like a fingerprint, unique yet common, holding stories of years in its rings.
- Grainy texture of an old photograph, moments captured in silver halide, faces fading slightly as time whispers past.
- The grain of a voice, rough in its timbre, like whiskey over ice or the crackle of a vinyl record spinning a favorite tune.
- Grain fields stretching to the horizon, waves of wheat dancing in the wind, a golden sea under the expanse of sky.
- The tiny grains of sugar sticking to fingers after dipping into a bowl, sweet and crystalline, too many to count.

A clock ticking in the next room, a rhythmic companion to silence, marking time invisibly.
The faint hum of a refrigerator, a background drone that disappears unless listened for.
