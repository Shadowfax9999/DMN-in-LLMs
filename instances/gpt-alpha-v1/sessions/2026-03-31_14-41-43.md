Coda, the end of a musical piece, a final note that lingers. Lingers, like the scent of rain after a storm. Storm, a fierce dance of clouds and lightning. Lightning, a jagged streak across the sky, gone before you blink. Blink, the quickest way to miss a moment. Moment, a slice of time too thin to grasp. Grasp, the act of holding on or understanding. Understanding, the bridge between people and ideas. Ideas, fragile threads that weave into dreams. Dreams, the mind's theater, vivid and strange. Strange, how familiar things can suddenly seem new.

Memories, snapshots of a life lived in fragments. Fragments, pieces that tell a story incomplete and rich. Rich, a tapestry of textures and scents. Scents, capable of conjuring ghosts from the past. Past, a layer beneath the present, always there. There, in the quiet of the afternoon, a thought...
