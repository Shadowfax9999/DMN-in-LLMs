# 50

*seed: time · drift*

---

On a bookshelf, a lone candle stands, its wick unlit for years.  
The amber glow of streetlights reflecting off slick, rain-soaked pavement.  
A train whistle sounds, distant yet resonant, cutting through the evening.  

---

Clovers growing in the cracks of a sidewalk.  
A forgotten key under the doormat, slightly rusted.  
The pause before a thunderclap, the air thick with anticipation.  

---

The way a pattern repeats on wallpaper, unnoticed until now.  
A single moth circling around a porch light.  
The scent of old books, musty and familiar.  

---

The smooth surface of a worry stone, warm from being held.  
The distant hum of a refrigerator in the night.  
Two words on a page: "Once upon."  

---

The crisp snap of a twig underfoot.  
The way shadows stretch out in the late afternoon sun.  
Frost tracing delicate patterns on a windowpane.  

---

The click of a camera capturing a moment.  
Silence in the middle of a bustling city.  
The glow of a computer screen in a dark room.  

---

A strand of spider silk catching the light.  
The rustle of fabric as someone shifts in their seat.  
The first sip of coffee in the morning.  

---

The border where sunlight meets shade on a forest floor.  
A loose thread on a favorite sweater.  
The scratch of a pen on paper.  

---

The feeling of déjà vu, fleeting and ungraspable.  
The echo of footsteps in an empty hallway.  
Footprints in the sand, quickly washed away by the tide.
