# 31

*seed: time · drift*

---

The river's flow—constant, unhurried.

---

Unhurried, the quiet ticking of a clock in the next room.

Ticking, like rain on a tin roof.

Tin roof, rusting, the passage of time etched in orange.

Orange, the color of the ending day.

Ending, a book's last page turned.

---

Turned, the soil in a garden, rich with promise. 

Promise, a word filled with hope, expectation. 

Expectation, the weight of tomorrow hanging on today.

Today, a fleeting moment sandwiched between what was and what could be.

---

Could be, a dream half-remembered upon waking.

Waking, the first breath of morning, new and familiar at once.

Familiar, the feeling of an old sweater, well-worn, comforting. 

Comforting, the warmth of a mug in hands on a cold day.
