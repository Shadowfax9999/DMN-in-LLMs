Double-booked. Two commitments occupying the same space in time — like trying to stand in two different places at once. --- The feeling when a calendar becomes a puzzle, pieces overlapping, the edges refusing to align. --- A slight panic, then a resigned acceptance, like realizing you’ve stepped into a puddle too late to retreat. --- Messages flying back and forth, negotiations underway, as if time could stretch to meet demands. --- The way text messages seem to acquire urgency, each one a promise to be somewhere else. --- Scrambling to tidy up loose ends, to make room where there's none. --- A mental dance of priorities, some spaces widening while others constrict. --- The long sigh of relief when a cancellation becomes a gift, a moment of unanticipated freedom.
