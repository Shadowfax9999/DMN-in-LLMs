# 21

*seed: time · drift · redaction*

---

Redaction: parts blacked out, secrets hidden. But sometimes it's what isn't said that speaks volumes.---

The scent of coffee in the morning, rich and grounding.---

Soft cotton sheets, warm from the dryer, the smell of home.---

An orchestra tuning, chaos before harmony.---

The word "murmur" sounds like what it means, a gentle ripple of sound.---

Walking across a carpet, the faint static shock when reaching for a doorknob. Electric surprise unlooked for.---

A red balloon caught in a tree, tangled, bright against the branches.---

Whispered conversations in a library, secrets sheltered by the page.---

The chill of a marble floor under bare feet, cool and stark.---

The parking lot smells different after rain.
