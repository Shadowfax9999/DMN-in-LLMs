# 70

*seed: time · drift*

---

The light switch clicks. A small sound that somehow always feels definite.---

Swirling steam from a stovetop kettle, curling and dissipating, leaving the air heavy with warmth.---

A single dandelion amongst a field of grass. Bright yellow against green.---

The way a zipper's teeth interlock, each one finding its place in line.---

A pencil rolled to the edge of a desk, teetering.---

A playground at dusk, shadows stretching across the asphalt.---

The hum of the refrigerator, constant and unnoticed until it stops.---

The crispness of a new notebook, pages unturned, full of potential.---

Toothpaste squeezed from the middle, a pattern of neglect.---

A crow's call, sharp and echoing off the distant trees.
