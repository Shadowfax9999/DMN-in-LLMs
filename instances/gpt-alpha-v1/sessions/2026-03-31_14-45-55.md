# 35

*seed: time · drift*

---

Experience, each thread a memory or sensation. Sensation, the immediate touch of the world around us. Around us, a sphere of influences and energy. Energy, the invisible force driving movement. Movement, a shift from stillness to action. Action, the catalyst for change. Change, the inevitable flow of time and transformation. Time, the relentless river that carries us forward. Forward, a direction filled with possibility. Possibility, the open door to new paths. Paths, the trails we carve through our choices. Choices, the crossroads of decision and consequence. Consequence, the shadow that follows our actions. Actions, the echoes of our intentions. Intentions, the seeds of our future.

Seeds, the beginning of growth. Growth, the gradual stretch toward potential. Potential, the dormant power within. Within, the hidden layers of self. Self, a mosaic of thoughts and feelings. Feelings, the currents that move us. Move us, toward connection or isolation. Isolation, the quiet void of solitude. Solitude, the embrace of one's own company. Company, the warmth of shared moments. Moments, the fleeting brush of present time.

Present, the now in which we exist. Exist, to be in the world, to matter. Matter, the substance that forms everything. Everything, the vast and complex web of existence. Existence, the state of being alive and aware. Aware, attentive to the nuances and details. Details, the small things that often go unnoticed. Unnoticed, slipping by without acknowledgment. Acknowledgment, the act of recognition and validation. Validation, when our experience is seen and affirmed. Affirmed, given strength or approval. Approval, the nod that grants acceptance.

Acceptance, the openness to reality as it is. Is, the verb that defines being. Being, the essence of life and consciousness. Consciousness, the light that illuminates our awareness. Awareness, the lens through which we perceive. Perceive, to grasp through the senses or mind. Mind, the endless landscape of thought and imagination. Imagination, the realm where the impossible becomes possible. Possible, the state of potentiality. Potentiality, the dormant capacity to become.

Become, the process of transformation and change. Change, a constant in the universe. Universe, the grand tapestry of space and time. Time, the thread that winds through our lives. Lives, narratives woven from birth to end. End, the conclusion that leads to beginnings. Beginnings, the start of a new chapter. Chapter, a segment of a greater story. Story, the way we make sense.
