Double-take — the momentary jolt when the mind demands a second glance.

---

Things that make me pause: 
1. A color that doesn't quite belong.
2. A familiar face in an unfamiliar place.
3. A repeated word in a conversation, like an echo.
4. A stray thread on a piece of clothing.
5. A sudden shift in music tempo.

---

Shadows can catch the eye in a way that needs verification. Did something move, or was it just the play of light?

---

The way a dog's ears perk up at a distant sound, attentive and alert. 

---

There's a moment when a word feels foreign, as if it's lost meaning through repetition.

An unexpected sound in the hush of night — what could it be?
