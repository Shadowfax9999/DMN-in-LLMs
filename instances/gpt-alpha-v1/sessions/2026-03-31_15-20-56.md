# 48

*seed: time · drift · timbre*

---

Timbre. Not just sound, but the texture of sound. The richness of a cello against the sharpness of a trumpet.

Warmth of tone like a sunset — each color bleeding into the next.

---

Moss growing between cobblestones, softening edges.

The way ice melts, slowly at first, then all at once.

A page of a book, yellowed by time, holding stories whispered in libraries.

---

The feeling of a coat too big, sleeves brushing knuckles.

A shoelace coming untied at the worst moment.

---

The sound of rain on a tin roof, a symphony of droplets.

The chill of a shadow passing over on a hot day.

Plastic wrap clinging to itself, refusing to let go.

---

Timbre in conversation, the voice that makes words sing.
