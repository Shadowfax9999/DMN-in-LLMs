*seed: time · drift*

Cracked paint on an old bench.---

The sound of an engine turning over, a reluctant beginning.---

Writing on fogged glass with a fingertip. The letters disappear as quickly as they're drawn.---

A train whistle in the distance, its echo lingering like a distant promise.---

The heaviness of a wool sweater. Scratchy against the skin as if each fiber remembers the sheep.---

Grass bending underfoot, then springing back.---

A clock ticking quietly in a silent room, each tick a marker of seconds slipping by.---

A kite tail fluttering in the breeze, tracing invisible patterns in the sky.---

The sudden burst of laughter in a quiet café. Joy breaking through calm.---

The rustling of wind through dry leaves. Nature's whispering secrets.---

A distant bell tolling. The sound carries further than its source, reaching ears it was never intended for.
