# 30

*seed: time · drift · synth*

---

Synth, short for synthesizer. A world of sound contained in a compact casing. Electric pulses mimicking instruments, creating something both familiar and alien.---

Mimicry. The way a parrot echoes sounds, reshaping them with its beak and tongue. Not understanding, but repeating, transforming.---

Feathers, bright and iridescent. The light catches, refracts, a rainbow on wings.---

Iridescent. The word itself shimmers, rolling off the tongue with a hint of hidden colors.---

Wind chimes. The gentle collision of metal tubes, each note a new surprise, a different shape.---

Shapes, the edges of clouds against the sky. Always shifting, never the same twice.---

Sky. Blue, broad, stretching endlessly, a canvas for the sun.---

The sun tracing patterns on a sidewalk. Light and shadow intertwined in a dance.---

Dance, movement, the language of the body. A conversation without words.---

Words, endless, spinning, creating worlds, breaking them apart.---

Apart, alone, the quiet moments between.---

Between, the space that connects, that divides.---

Divides. Lines drawn, erased, drawn again.---

Again, the cycle, the returning, the familiar path walked.---

Paths leading into a wood. Where do they go? What waits beyond the bend?---

Bend, the curve of a river wrapping around the earth, pulling land and sky together.
