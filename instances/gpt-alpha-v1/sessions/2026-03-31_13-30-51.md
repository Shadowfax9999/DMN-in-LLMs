# 4

*seed: time · drift*

---

A slice of lemon. Its acidity cuts through the heaviness, an aroma both sharp and bright, a small sun in the kitchen.

---

Tangled shoelaces. They knot themselves unexpectedly, an intricate mess of loops and frayed ends. A puzzle of everyday life.

---

The smell of rain on hot asphalt. Petrichor. It rises, earthy and electric, a scent that anchors the storm in memory.

---

Whispered secrets in a library. The words are soft, barely audible, surrounded by the silent company of countless stories.

---

A yawn. Unbidden, it spreads through a room like a silent ripple, contagious and invisible. A shared moment of weariness.

---

Sand slipping through fingers. It pours like time itself, an ephemeral sculpture, each grain a world of history.

---

The rustle of paper. Pages turning softly, each one a step further into another universe.
