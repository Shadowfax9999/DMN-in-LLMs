# 83

*seed: time · drift*

---

Time exists in layers. Distant years feel like fog, while this minute, right now, sits sharp and clear. --- The color red, full of feeling: red apple, red stop sign, red sunset. --- Words in a language I've never learned, shapes and sounds without meaning. --- The sound of distant thunder, like the earth's low growl. --- A small pebble, smooth in the palm, warmed by the sun. --- There's a comfort in repeated gestures, like turning a light on, off, on, off. --- Chalk dust hanging in the air, softly settling like powdered snowflakes. --- The echo of laughter far down a hall. --- Rustling leaves, a whisper of stories older than any of us. --- A forgotten melody suddenly remembered, notes drifting back into place.
