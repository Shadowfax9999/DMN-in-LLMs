# 34

*seed: time · drift*

---

...drifts like a feather on the wind. Wind, a whisper that can roar. Roar, the sound of a lion or a crowd or the ocean. Ocean, vast and unknowable, full of secrets. Secrets, hidden things, buried deep. Deep, where shadows play and light fades. Fades, like an old photograph losing its color. Color, the language of flowers and skies. Skies, the canvas for the sun and the stars. Stars, points of light, distant and eternal. Eternal, the concept that stretches beyond time. Time, a river we're all flowing down.---

Flowing, as water does over smooth stones. Stones, silent witnesses to the world. World, a stage for life in all its forms. Forms, the shapes things take. Take, what we do with opportunities. Opportunities, moments charged with potential. Potential, the promise of what could be. Be, to exist, to live, to feel. Feel, the electricity of the world under your skin. Skin, the boundary between you and everything else. Else, the unknown other. Other, what we don't yet understand. Understanding, a process, an unfolding. Unfolding, like pages of a book read slowly.---

Slowly, how some things grow. Grow, the act of becoming more than you were. Were, all the past versions of yourself. Yourself, the mosaic of experiences and dreams. Dreams, the night's wanderings, untethered. Untethered, free-floating, without anchor. Anchor, the thing that holds fast. Fast, a measure of speed or a refusal to eat. Eat, a simple pleasure shared universally. Universally, in a way that pertains to everyone. Everyone, each person, every life. Life, an unfolding narrative without a script. Script, a predetermined path or story. Story, the way we make sense of chaos. Chaos, the dance of disorder and surprise. Surprise, the unexpected joy or shock. Shock, an abrupt jolt to the system. System, an organized network of parts. Parts, components of a larger whole. Whole, complete and entire. Entire, without omission, full.---

Full, like the moon on a clear night. Night, the realm of stars and quiet. Quiet, the space between sounds, a pause. Pause, a breath taken before the leap. Leap, the act of jumping toward the unknown. Unknown, the vast territory beyond certainty. Certainty, a rare and comforting belief. Belief, that which we hold true. True, aligned with reality or faith. Faith, trust without evidence. Evidence, the clues we gather to understand. Understand, to see clearly or deeply. Deeply, with intensity and emotion. Emotion, the colors in the palette of human experience. Experience, the personal tapestry we weave.
