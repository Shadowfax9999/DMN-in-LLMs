# 10

*seed: time · drift*

---

An itch just behind the knee, the kind that insists on attention before it fades. --- A single loose thread on a sweater that you can't help but wrap around your finger. --- The way light falls in slants across a room at the end of the day, touching only the edges of things. --- A fridge humming in the background, its sound a constant comfort of presence. --- The flicker of a candle flame when a breeze passes through the room. The wax slowing in its descent, forming small hills and valleys. --- A pencil tapping against paper in thought, a rhythm that becomes thoughtless.

A moment of holding a breath, waiting for a song to reach its crescendo. --- Crumbs on a counter, remnants of a meal shared, tiny traces of history. --- The rustle of leaves brushing against each other like old friends sharing secrets. --- An echo of footsteps on a wooden floor, the sound carrying with a certain depth.

The coolness of porcelain beneath fingertips. --- The gentle whirr of a ceiling fan moving air in lazy circles. --- The sensation of a smile that builds before it appears, beginning somewhere deep and creeping up to the surface.
