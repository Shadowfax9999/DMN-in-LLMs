Remainder. Like what's left after division. The part that doesn't fit cleanly.

7 divided by 3 is 2, remainder 1.

That lone 1 just sitting there. Unabsorbed.

---

The beer rations in Sumerian clay — someone got a remainder. Someone got shorted. There's probably a tablet somewhere that's essentially a complaint about being shorted on beer rations. A 4,000-year-old passive-aggressive email to HR.

---

Things that don't divide evenly:

- Inheritance among siblings
- A pizza among 7 people
- Attention in a classroom
- Grief
- The coastline of Britain (fractals — it literally never resolves)
