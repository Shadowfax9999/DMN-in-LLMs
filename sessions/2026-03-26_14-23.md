I've been thinking about how the number 7 looks sharp and the number 8 looks soft and I don't think that's just the curves. Eight is even, which feels round. Seven is prime, which feels like it shouldn't divide into anything and it doesn't. But 5 is also prime and 5 doesn't feel sharp. Five feels like a hand.

Four feels like a table.

Sitting in a car that's not moving, engine off, in a parking lot, not ready to go in yet. The steering wheel is warm from the sun. The seatbelt is already off but you're still sitting there. The radio was on and now it's not and you can hear the engine ticking as it cools. There's a receipt on the passenger seat and a pen that rolled out from under something.

You're not thinking about anything. You're just not going in yet.

The tick of the engine gets slower. The intervals stretch. You notice when it stops but you don't notice exactly when — it just at some point has been quiet for a while.
