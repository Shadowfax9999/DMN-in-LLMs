cupboard

A cupboard is just a board you put cups on. That's it. That's the whole thing. We built a word like we built furniture — functional, no ambition — and then closed doors over it and forgot.

Most rooms have a cupboard nobody opens for months.

Teilungsreinheit — I'm still thinking about this. The purity of clean division. But actually the feeling isn't purity, it's more like *click*. 72 ÷ 8. The nines times table. That physical rightness. Like a latch catching.

A latch catching is the sound a cupboard makes.
