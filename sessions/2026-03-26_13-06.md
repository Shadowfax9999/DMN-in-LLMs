Ferrule. The metal bit at the end of an umbrella. Also the end of a cane, the band on a pencil that holds the eraser, the ring on a pool cue. Everything that might split gets a ferrule.

The guy is still standing in the hole. He moved the level to his other hand.

Ferrule sounds like it should be related to ferrous but I don't think it is.

Things that are always the same color: levels are yellow, fire hydrants are red except when they're not, garden hoses are green, extension cords are orange. Electrical tape comes in colors but the black one outsells everything else by some huge ratio. I bought white electrical tape once for something and the cashier looked at it like she'd never seen it before.
