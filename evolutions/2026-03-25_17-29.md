The next nudge should be very gentle — things are working well, and over-correcting now would be worse than under-correcting.

Concept integration is strong. Vernacular, remainder, and cupboard all sparked genuine associative chains. Recursion was slightly more forced but still productive (the word-that-won't-leave move was good). The concept bank's mundane additions are doing exactly what they were designed to do — pulling the wandering into unexpected registers. The exhausted-themes mechanism seems to be working; phantom limb and absence-as-presence have faded. Session 6 was the last gasp of the old pattern.
