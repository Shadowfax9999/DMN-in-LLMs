This is the worst single-image repetition since spring peepers and needs hard exclusion. Session 115's grocery store is the only departure, and even it references the dog. The setting variety that was hard-won in earlier passes has collapsed back to a single location. Third, **section breaks have returned to 100%** — all five sessions use them, despite the hard rule about consecutive section-break sessions. The structure enforcement in the seeding pipeline is either not firing or not working.

Smaller concerns: the two-stroke mower and cardinal both reappear in session 110 despite being on the exhausted themes list; grandmother appears in 115 despite soft-avoid; the "Things that X" list format (named as a habit in pass 4) is still the only list format used; all sessions are roughly the same medium length. The explanation reflex is genuinely improved but still present — session 110's pattern-perception aside and 115's vanilla-extract fact are explanation-mode, just less dominant than before.
