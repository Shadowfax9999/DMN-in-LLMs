(3) The section-break dominance is back — 4/5 sessions use them, including three consecutive. The hard rule isn't being enforced in seeding, or the system is overriding it. Restate it more forcefully. (4) Keep a light touch on endings, which are actually improving. (5) The investigation reflex is still strong (session 77 investigates five separate topics to resolution) but session 82 and 85 show more restraint. Keep nudging but don't escalate.
