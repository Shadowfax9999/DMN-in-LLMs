## Reflection

Sessions 58-65 are strong — the system is genuinely inhabited, investigatively curious, and formally varied enough with section breaks. The wave-propagation chain in session 62 (stadium wave → sound → rumor → traffic) is the best sustained associative thread in recent memory, and session 58's composite-house memory and stuck-drawer moment are exactly the kind of thing the DMN was built for. The raku/merry-go-round/lint-trap chain in session 60 shows natural, unhurried drift between sensory domains. The system is in a good place.

But several new patterns have solidified. First, the **childhood-house memory** has become a recurring set piece — session 58 does the composite house with merged wallpapers and a stuck drawer, and session 62 repeats essentially the same thread (picturing a childhood house, rooms as islands, lost connections between them). It's rich material but it's becoming a default well to draw from. Second, the **simile-as-connection** has become the dominant connective move: 'ceramic is a slow negotiation / metal is an argument,' 'Q without U is a person eating alone at a restaurant,' 'a house breathing out,' 'like a rumor through a crowd.' These are good individually but the pattern — observe something, then liken it to a human situation — is now the system's primary mode of making meaning, and it's crowding out other modes (pure description, mechanism without metaphor, juxtaposition without explanation). Third, **every session is structured as 4-5 discrete vignettes separated by section breaks**. The system has never produced a single continuous thread or a very short session. Section breaks were a formal innovation; now they're the only architecture. Fourth, the **explanatory reflex** persists strongly — every interesting observation gets investigated (basketball pressure, angular diameter, metamerism, 52-factorial, Diaconis). The system is excellent at this but it does it reflexively, every time. The prompt's nudge toward leaving things unexplained isn't landing.

Smaller notes: Wednesday appears again in session 65 ('Cork pop: celebration, or Wednesday') despite soft-avoid. Session 65 ends with a crafted simile ('A Q without a U is a person eating alone at a restaurant') that gives the session the satisfying shape of an essay closer — the old habit in new clothes. Session 62 briefly revisits absence/grief territory ('the absence of a person from rooms they used to be in'). The 'I wonder' framing appears in 65. The system's quality remains high but its formal and rhetorical range is narrowing again around a new set of defaults.
