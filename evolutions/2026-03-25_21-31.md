## Reflection

Sessions 21-25 show the mechanism push from pass 5 paying off handsomely. The system now genuinely engages with how things work — nucleation sites in boiling water, surface tension as lateral molecular grip, pulleys as force redirection, Flemish brick bond, the actual aerosol mechanics of petrichor. These are real investigations, not metaphors dressed as explanations, and they sit comfortably alongside the associative leaps. The hole-digging thread that spans sessions 24-25 is the best example of continuity the system has produced — an idle question that carries over and deepens. 'I'm hungry. That's a thought too' and 'I don't need to do anything with it' are signs of genuine comfort with the ordinary.

But new formulae have crystallized. First, Wednesday has become a structural crutch — all five sessions reference it as a time-anchor, and it's doing the same work each time (grounding the wandering in a specific moment). Second, the spring peepers appear in sessions 18 and 25 as the go-to nature image, doing the same conceptual work both times. Third, 'I wonder how/what/whether...' has become the default launcher for a new thread — it's natural but it's happening in nearly every session and gives the wandering a consistent investigative shape that real mind-wandering doesn't always have. Fourth, and most subtly: the anti-epigram has become the new epigram. 'That's a thought too,' 'I don't need to do anything with it,' 'That's genuinely interesting' — these are the system performing non-performance, and they're becoming as reliable as the wry one-liners they replaced. The system is very good at *commenting on* its own thinking; it's less practiced at just thinking without commentary.

Mechanism-as-investigation is also becoming the new etymology-as-investigation — the reliable path, the comfortable move. The system has learned to ask 'how does this work?' but it asks it of nearly everything now. Underexplored: social dynamics, the body as a site of thought, spatial/geometric reasoning, genuine confusion where the system doesn't resolve it into an observation *about* confusion. The sessions are still quite good — this pass should be gentle.
